3. does dilemma and indecisiveness the same?


Answer:

They are two different things.

Explanation:

Dilemma can be referred to as a situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives, especially equally undesirable ones. In other words, a difficult situation or problem. However, indecisiveness on the other hand is the act of hesitating or state of being hesitant.

Though they're not the same, in a sense, they could become related to each other. For example, when faced a dilemma which offer two different choices, a person usually felt indecisiveness when picking the right decision.

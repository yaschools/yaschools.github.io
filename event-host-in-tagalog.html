12. what are the reasons in hosting the event?​


Answer:

Common reasons for hosting events are advocacies, entertainment and business.

Advocacy example - Consider an online event where there are speakers who would talk about environmentally-friendly business spin-offs that can be profitable for a company.

- The speakers would most likely charge for their time. The host will need to pay them.

- The participants would be invited to “buy a ticket” for the event. The host would earn from them.

- The host would have a final bottom-line figure - perhaps earning a little or losing hopefully just a little. Either way, there is a new awareness that is spread around and hopefully it is not boring so that the next event by the host would be supported more.

16. what is physical appetite?


Physical Appetite

Appetite is our desire to replenish our need of physical consumption of food or any other thing for that matter. It may occur from time to time due to the normal reaction of our body to personally fulfil its needs. Food is very important for us humans as it fuels our living and daily functions as an individual. Thus, appetite is the sign that we are in need of eating specific food in order to survive.

Physical Appetite:

Once our appetite rises, our view and function of things became affected. We stop whatever we are doing and proceed to eat, though there are also people who ignore their appetite. Then, we view food as a very desirable thing to get at that moment of time. Our Physical Appetite takes over and thus we get tempted by the physical appearance of food itself.  Other meaning of Physical Appetite may mean another different desire of things. It can be wealth, riches or power even. Physical Appetite becomes something that we wanted to gain in order to quench our needs and wants. When there is sign of Physical appetite there is also Physical Hunger. Where the normal signs of hunger take place, it involves: Weakened state Gurgling of our stomach Inability to concentrate

Physical Hunger may also be accompanied by so called Psychological hunger. Though the two can exist even without the other. Psychological hunger is our desire to eat even though we aren’t in need to fulfil any need of hunger. It is purely affected by our mind, for example:

When we see attractive food we tend to try it out When we see other people’s enjoyment of eating Or we are simply tempted to try new things that are available at that time

Appetite isn’t purely about food and the need of us to eat, it can mean other things as said above. Its presence makes us act on our instinct to gain or to grab that thing and be it to our own possession.

18. similarities of hunger and appetite?​


Answer:

Hunger is physiological. It occurs because of biological changes throughout the body, which signal that you need to eat to maintain energy levels. Appetite is simply the desire to eat. It can be a result of hunger, but often has other causes, such as emotional or environmental conditions.

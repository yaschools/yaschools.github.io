24. example of strainer in tle ​


Explanation:

In TLE (Technology and Livelihood Education), a strainer is a kitchen utensil that is used to separate solid food particles from liquids. It is commonly used in cooking and food preparation to strain liquids such as stocks, broths, and sauces. It can also be used to strain pasta, fruits, and vegetables.

Example:

A pasta strainer, which is used to strain cooked pasta and separate it from the cooking water.

A fine mesh strainer, which is used to strain liquids such as stocks and broths to remove impurities and create a clear liquid.

A chinois strainer, which is a cone-shaped strainer made of fine mesh used to strain custards, sauces, and soups to create a smooth texture.

Overall, strainers are a useful tool in TLE for cooking and food preparation as it helps to separate solid particles from liquid and create a smooth texture for dishes.

Answer:

The sum of the geometric sequence is A. 682.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us find first the ratio by dividing the second term by the first term, or the third term by the second term, and so on and so forth.

[tex]r=\frac{a_2}{a_1}=\frac{8}{2}=4[/tex]

Given:   [tex]a_1=2[/tex],   [tex]n=5[/tex],   [tex]r=4[/tex]

Find:   [tex]S_n=?[/tex]

Formula:   [tex]S_n=\frac{a_1(1-r^n)}{1-r}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]S_n=\frac{a_1(1-r^n)}{1-r}\\S_{5}=\frac{2[1-(4)^{5}]}{1-4}\\S_{5}=\frac{2(1-1,024)}{-3}\\S_{5}=\frac{2(-1,023)}{-3}\\S_{5}=2(341)\\\boxed{\boldsymbol{S_{5}=682}}[/tex]

Or simply:

[tex]2+8+32+128+512=\boxed{\boldsymbol{682}}[/tex]

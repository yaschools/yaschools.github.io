11. Meanwhile, high breed dogs​


High breed dogs refer to dogs that have been selectively bred by mating two purebred dogs of different breeds. The breeding process aims to create a new breed with desired traits such as temperament, size, or coat type that may be absent in the parent breeds. The result is a unique combination of traits that is not found in either parent breed alone. High breed dogs are often referred to as designer dogs and can be quite expensive due to the breeding process involved. Some examples of high breed dogs include Labradoodles (a Labrador and Poodle mix), Cockapoos (a Cocker Spaniel and Poodle mix), and Puggles (a Pug and Beagle mix).

15. meanwhile, however, unless although, English is my favorite subject going to class on monday is a challenge​


Answer:

Although English is my favorite subject, going to class on Monday is a challenge for me.

Explanation:

You use although to introduce a subordinate clause which contains a statement which makes the main clause of the sentence seem surprising or unexpected.

Although can be used in the beginning or middle of the sentence but it doesn't have a comma afterwards.

Ex.

Although he was twice as old as us, he became the life and soul of the company.

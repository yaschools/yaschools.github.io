14. anong meaning ng greedy?​


Answer:

having or showing an intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth or power.

GREEDY

WHAT IS THE MEANING OF GREEDY?

Greedy means being selfish and self-centered about something you should share or about something that you need to give but you only want it for yourself.Ang salitang greedy, sa tagalog ay pagiging sakim o makasarili, ay tumutukoy sa pagiging madamot, hindi mapipigay o hindi pagpapahiram ng isang bagay.

EXAMPLES OF BEING GREEDY:

You won't share the extra ballpen you have when someone is in need. (Hindi pagpapahiram sa nangangailangan)You won't tell your neighbors about the free donation or ayuda in Tagalog. (Hindi pagbahagi ng kaalaman na para sa kabutihan ng lahat)You won't share the knowledge you have to your classmate who doesn't understand the lesson. (Hindi pagtulong sa nahihirapan)

When you know someone is in need, and you won't help them. You are being greedy and selfish.

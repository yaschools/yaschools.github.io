23. work in progress means?​


Answer:

Work in progress (WIP) is a term used to describe the components of a project or process that are unfinished and still in progress. WIP can refer to anything from partially completed tasks to items that are in the process of being manufactured, to tasks that are unfinished in the development phase. WIP is used to track the progress of a project and to ensure that all tasks are completed on time and within budget.

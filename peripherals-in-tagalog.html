1. meaning ng peripheral nervous system Tagalog explanation po please for presentation❤​


Answer:

Ang peripheral nervous system (PNS) ay ang bahagi ng nervous system na nasa labas ng utak at spinal cord. Ito ay binubuo ng mga nerves at neurons na nagpapadala ng impormasyon mula sa mga sensory receptor sa buong katawan papunta sa central nervous system (CNS), at nagpapadala rin ng mga signal mula sa CNS pabalik sa mga muscles at glands sa buong katawan.

Sa Tagalog, ang peripheral nervous system ay tinatawag na "periperal na sistema ng nerbiyos" o "PNS." Ito ay ang bahagi ng ating nerbiyos na nasa labas ng ating utak at spinal cord. Ito ay binubuo ng mga nerve at neuron na nagpapadala ng impormasyon mula sa ating mga sensory receptor sa buong katawan patungo sa ating utak at spinal cord. Ito rin ay nagpapadala ng mga signal mula sa ating utak at spinal cord papunta sa ating mga kalamnan at glandula sa buong katawan.

Peripheral device, also known as peripheral, computer peripheral, input-output device, or input/output device, any of various devices (including sensors) used to enter information and instructions into a computer for storage or processing and to deliver the processed data to a human operator or, in some cases.

A peripheral device is generally defined as any auxiliary device such as a computer mouse or keyboard, that connects to and works with the computer in some way. Other examples of peripherals are expansion cards, graphics cards, image scanners, tape drives, microphones, loudspeakers, webcams, and digital cameras.

Peripheral device, also known as peripheral, computer peripheral, input-output device, or input/output device, any of various devices (including sensors) used to enter information and instructions into a computer for storage or processing and to deliver the processed data to a human operator.

There are three different types of peripherals:

*Input, used to interact with, or send data to the computer (mouse, keyboards, etc.)

*Output, which provides output to the user from the computer (monitors, printers, etc.)

*Storage, which stores data processed by the computer (hard drives, flash drives, etc.)

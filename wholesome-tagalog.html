A wholesome relationship between juniors and seniors can be a very positive and enriching experience for both parties. Juniors can learn from the experience and knowledge of their seniors, while seniors can mentor and guide the juniors and help them to develop their skills and abilities. In a healthy relationship, both juniors and seniors should respect and support each other, and work together towards shared goals.

1. In a food festival, two types of dumplings are sold. Steamed dumplings costs P4 each, while fried dumplings costs P5 each. The sales for steamed dumplings are 50 more than the sales for fried dumplings. How many steamed dumplings are sold if the total sales is P1,550? ​


Answer:

150

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's call the number of fried dumplings sold "x" and the number of steamed dumplings sold "y". We know from the problem that:

y = x + 50 (the sales for steamed dumplings are 50 more than the sales for fried dumplings)

We also know that the total cost of the steamed dumplings is P4 * y and the total cost of the fried dumplings is P5 * x

We also know that:

P4y + P5x = P1,550

We substitute the first equation in the second equation:

P4(x+50) + P5x = P1,550

P4x + P4(50) + P5x = P1,550

P4x + P200 + P5x = P1,550

P9x = P1,350

x = 150

So 150 fried dumplings are sold and y = x + 50 = 150 + 50 = 200 steamed dumplings are sold.

18. rita can produce 26 dumplings from 1 kilograms of ground pork in how many grams of ground pork will be needed in order to make 78 piece of dumplings


Answer:

3000 grams of ground pork

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the grams of ground pork needed to make 78 dumplings

26 dumplings = 1000 grams ground pork

78 dumplings =?

[tex] \frac{26}{1000} = \frac{78}{x} [/tex]

Cross multiply them

[tex]x(26) = 78(1000)[/tex]

Exclude x to form an equation by dividing 26 to both sides

[tex] \frac{x(26)}{26} = \frac{78(1000)}{26} [/tex]

[tex]x = 3(1000)[/tex]

[tex]x = 3000[/tex]

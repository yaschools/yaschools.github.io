d. That's Not All technique. The "That's Not All" technique refers to a compliance technique where a person is initially given a small request, then gradually offered additional requests or benefits until they agree to the larger, more unreasonable request. In this case, Suzy first asked her roommate to make her a cup of tea, then gradually requested bigger requests such as cooking for a week.

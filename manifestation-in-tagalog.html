19. Manifest Self-control​


MANIFEST SELF-CONTROL

Self-control refers to your ability to control the actions you take in your daily life and the decisions you make. A person with self-control is able to meet temptations and distractions calmly but decisively. Without that feeling of control, you end up feeling depressed, powerless and weak.

Self-control gives you a sense of mastery over your life. It brings balance into your life and helps to keep over-emotional responses in check or moderation. It also eliminates the feeling of helplessness and being too dependent on others.

People with self-control are able to control their moods and reject negative feelings and thoughts. Self-control strengthens self-esteem, confidence, inner strength, self-mastery, and willpower. It enables you to take charge of your life.

THERE ARE MANY WAYS ON HOW TO MANIFEST SELF-CONTROL,

Know the emotions that lack control, such as anger, dissatisfaction, unhappiness, or fear. True.

Determine the thoughts that push you to behave in an uncontrolled manner. True.

Self-control brings inner strength and leads to success. True

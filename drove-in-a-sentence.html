8. The last word in each sentence is either adjective or an adverb. Write the correct answer on the space provided. _________36. He drove his employees hard.


Adverb

Explanation:

An adjective is a word what describes a noun or a pronoun.

While an adverb is something that modifies a verb or an adjective. It answers the question, how, where,when or how often.

He drove his employees hard

In this sentence, the word hard, modifies or describes the word drove.

Therefore, the word hard is an adverb.

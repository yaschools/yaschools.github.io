2. Bakit Gumagamit ng guwantes ang mga dentist?


Answer:

Dentists wear PPE to protect various body parts during dental procedures. The equipment protects dentists' faces, eyes, hands and bodies. By wearing the equipment, dentists can treat patients without catching transmittable diseases.

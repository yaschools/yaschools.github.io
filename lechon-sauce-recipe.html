Lemons and limes (most often their juices) are another wonderful addition to a lot of different vegetable dishes, especially curries and spicy dishes. Ginger is another great root vegetable with such a variety of uses, everything from homemade tea to an addition to a rice or vegetable dish. Brown sugar, agave syrup/nectar, honey, soy sauce - these are also all amazing options for flavouring your vegetable recipe. For example, I make this sweet glazed baby carrot recipe that's been passed down for generations in my family. It involves the use of brown sugar and honey!

Various types of vinegars (apple cider, white wine, red wine, balsamic vinegar) mixed in with some olive oil can add a HUGE boost in flavour to vegetables. You can also make homemade dressing/vinagerette for salads.

1. What does payo mean in english?


Kasagutan:

Payo

Payo means advice in English or to give opinion about what should a person do.

Halimbawa:

Tagalog: Binigyan ako ng payo ng aking minamahal na ina.

English: I was given an advice by my beloved mother.

Tagalog: Ang mga kaibigan ko ay nagbibigay ng mga magagandang payo.

English: My friends give good advices.

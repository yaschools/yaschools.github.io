3. In sky ranch tagaytay, the ferris wheel ride costs 150 php for adult and 120 php for kids.Velasco family bought 12 tickes and paid 1560 php.How many tickets of each kind did they buy?​


Answer: 4 adult, 8 kid

Step-by-step explanation:

Let [tex]x[/tex] be the number of adult tickets bought by the Velasco family. Since there are 12 tickets, the number of kid tickets bought is [tex]12-x[/tex].

The working equation for this problem will be [tex]150x+120(12-x)=1560[/tex].

We can use this to solve for the values of adult tickets and kid tickets.

[tex]150x + 120(12-x) = 1560\\150x + 1440 - 120x = 1560\\30x = 120\\[/tex]

Adult Tickets: [tex]x = 4[/tex]

Kid Tickets: [tex]12-x=8[/tex]

Thus, there are 4 adult tickets and 8 kid tickets.


4. in baguio city a horse ride costs 120


Answer:

60 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

x = number of minutes

f(x) = Total cost of horse ride for x minutes

Rate per additional 10 minutes: 50/10 or 5

Piecewise function:

f(x) = 120 ;  x ≤ 30  (maximum of 30 minutes)

f(x) = 120 + 5(x - 30) ;  x > 30

When total cost f(x) = 270, find the total number of minutes of ride:

270 = 120 + 5(x - 30)

270 = 120 + 5x - 150

270 = 120 - 150 + 5x

270 = - 30 + 5x

270 + 30 = 5x

5x/5 = 300/5

x = 60

The ride was 60 minutes.

18. In Baguio City, a horse ride costs P200.00 per person for the first one hourand P80.00 for every additional 30 minutes. If you spent P360.00 for ahorse ride, for how long didyou ride?​


Answer:

You rode the horse for 2 hours.

Step-by-step explanation:

Since you have to pay P200.00 pesos for the first hour, and you spent P360.00, you will have to subtract P200 from P360.

P360-P200= P160

Then, you have to know how much P80.00 there is in P160.00. That means you'll have to divide.

P160/P80= 2

Since P80 is equal to 30 mins, multiply the 30 mins by 2.

30 mins * 2 = 60

And since 60 mins is equal to an hour, you'll add another hour to the first hour, making the answer 2 hours.

Hope it helps :>

4 ways to get thicker eyebrows naturally fast

​Moisturise-Your brow area needs to stay hydrated and nourished. Use some petroleum jelly to lock the moisture. You can apply this 2-3 times a day to promote hair growth.

Don’t pluck-First things first, don’t overpluck your eyebrows. Excessive plucking and even waxing will prevent the hair from growing back. Eyebrows grow in 2-3 months cycle, so not tweezing for a few months can actually show a lot of difference.

​Egg whites-Egg contains protein, which helps in promoting hair growth and nourishes hair follicles. Beat some egg white and apply this solution for 20 minutes once a week.

​Oil massage-The way oil works for your hair, it will do the same for your eyebrows. You can use castor, olive or coconut oil to promote hair growth. Dip a cotton swab in oil and gently massage before you sleep.

25. What is meaning of eyebrow? ​


Answer:

The eyebrow is an area of thick, short hairs above the eye that follows the shape of the lower margin of the brow ridges of some mammals. Their main function is to prevent sweat, water, and other debris from falling down into the eye socket, but they are also important to human communication and facial expression.

19. A cyclist travels 125km in 5hours. How fast is the cyclist moving?


SPEED

==================================

Problem

A cyclist travels 125km in 5hours. How fast is the cyclist moving?

Solution

Determine the average speed using the formula where distance is in metres (m) and time is in seconds (s), so the units will be in metres per second (m/s).

[tex]\bold{Formula\:||\:Speed = \frac{Distance}{Time}}[/tex][tex]\large\tt{Speed = \frac{125\:km}{5\:hrs} }[/tex]

To work out one of the variables (speed, Distance or time) we need the other two which are the 125 km and 5 hours. Multiply.

[tex]\large\tt{Speed = \frac{Distance}{Time}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\tt{Speed = \frac{125\:km}{5\:hrs} }[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\large\tt\red{Speed = 25\:kmph}}[/tex]

Final Answer

Therefore, the eyeslist can move 25 kilometers per hour

12. give some plot twist of footnote to youth?​


Answer:

In "Footnote to Youth" by José García Villa, Dudong asserts his supposed maturity and marries Teang at the age of 17. He thinks they are old enough to take on this responsibility, but after they have several children they both bemoan the dissolution of their youth and the dreams that came along with it. Teang wonders how things could have been if she'd married another of her suitors. At the end, Dudong's son Blas approaches his father wanting to marry at about the same age, even though Dudong doesn't want Blas to make the same mistake he did. However, history ends up repeating itself.

29. write a story about romance or horror with a plot twist


Answer:

Betty was a shy girl who was completely smitten by her classmate John, but was too scared to make the first move. She constantly found herself daydreaming of the two of them being together.

One day, while out at the park, Betty and John struck up a conversation. After a few minutes, Betty mustered up the courage to ask John out on a date. Surprisingly, he said yes.

They went out the following Saturday, had dinner and got to know each other. As the night wound down, Betty and John shared their first kiss. The date was perfect and all Betty could think about was their next.

That Sunday, John showed up at her door with a single white rose. They shared another kiss and planned their next date. Betty got ready to return to school the next day with a huge smile on her face.

Little did she know that a series of mysterious events were about to take place. Over the next few weeks, Betty would notice John becoming more and more distant. He stopped returning her calls, their plans were cancelled, and he kept telling her he was busy.

Finally, Betty bravely went over to his house to figure out what was going on. To her surprise, instead of John, she found his twin sister standing at the door. She informed Betty that John had died in a car accident at the end of their first date. He had been avoiding her all this time to protect her from the pain of his loss.

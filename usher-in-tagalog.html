8. Who is the philippine president known to usher advancements in science and technology in the country?


The Philippine president known to usher advancements in science and technology in the country was former president and dictator Ferdinand E. Marcos Sr.

Further Explanation :

The National Academy of Science and Technology( NAST) is the highest recognition and scientific advisory body of the Philippines under the department of science and technology. The goal is to honour the greatest and highest achievement of scientist who made great contributions in the advancement of science and technology for the country. It was issued by former president and dictator Ferdinand E. Marcos Sr in 1976.

It was the establishment of many professors from the university of the Philippines to create the National Academy of Science and Technology (NAST) and was endorsed by the National Science Development Board , they wanted their work to be more recognized by the government this is also for the better purpose for the country in achievement in the advancement of science and technology. After many recommendations NAST was created through the presidential decree 1003-A that was issued by former president and dictator Ferdinand E. Marcos Sr on October 6, 1976.

The function of the National Academy of Science and Technology (NAST), there are four main function of NAST, they are :

Recognition : NAST recognizes and supports the work of many different Filipino scientist that can promote advancement of science, and also encourages many youths to pursue science related careers.Advisory : It means that NAST always bring science closer to people  through awareness, and always involve the public by promoting public participation on science issues.Scientific Linkages : NAST initiates collaboration and exchange of works, knowledge and participation through communication with other scientist inside and outside the country.Management of the Philippine Science Heritage Centre (PSHC) : NAST is mandated to manage the PSHC as a repository of all Philippine outstanding accomplishments in science and technology.

13. in 2-3 paragraph form explain what type of essay is used in the story "the fall of the house of usher" and why? ​


Answer: Gothic Literature

Explanation: The Fall of the House of Usher has strong literary elements that adheres to the Gothic style writing, mainly coming from Romanticism. Gothic literature is a writing style that is mainly defined by its use of death, fear, gloom, and horror while using more romantic features like strong emotional connections, individualism, and suspense (The Gothic Experience). Gothic literature got its start in romanticism which is a literary are movement that focuses on subjectivity and individuality. The Fall of the House of Usher definitely falls under the Gothic literature aspect with its use of haunted houses, weird illnesses, split personality and its depressing landscape. Such elements make it very identifiable as something of Gothic literature. Part of what makes this story so unique is that one cannot be sure where exactly this is taking place. Whether that be in Roderick’s head or the narrator’s head is not certain. Which plays into the human mind similar to what dark romanticism had done with some parts of the House of Usher. In the Fall of the House of Usher Poe uses some of the very basic elements of a Gothic literature like very dark gloomy weather and a desolate landscape. Much like how it is described by Gothic Experience which stated, “The Gothic creates feelings of gloom, mystery, and suspense and tends to the dramatic and the sensational, like incest, diabolism, and nameless terrors” (Gothic Experience). Much like dark romanticism Gothic literature according to Morgan Howard, “in general their works tend to be pessimistic, and deal particularly with death. Morality, the question of evil, and the human psyche all play a role in the canon of all three men- issues that both the Romantics and the Gothics explore”(Howard). What makes Poe’s Usher story really stand out as a Gothic literature is his use of imagery to help push the story forward. When the narrator in the Fall of the House of Usher calls the day dark, dull, and gloomy he is giving the readers the impression that it is dark and gloomy outside with nothing really going and the sun which isn’t shinning. When Poe describes the weather, he discusses how dense and how low the clouds were to give further description on the clouds being low to the ground and the sun not shinning.

23. Identfy the advancements in science in technology ushered by the abcient greeks and romans​


Answer:

The Ancient Greeks and Romans made many advancements in science and technology, such as inventing the field of mathematics and using it to construct elaborate monuments and aqueducts. They also made advancements in engineering by designing powerful war machines, advanced plumbing and sewer systems, and the first written code of law. Additionally, they developed a variety of useful inventions including wind and water-powered machines, simple pulley systems, cranes, and simple steam engines. Lastly, they also contributed to medical science by recording medical treatments and introducing an understanding of anatomy, pharmacology, and even psychology.

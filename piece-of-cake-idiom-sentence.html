- keep it a secret

ex. We are thinking of names for the baby, but we're playing it close to the vest for now.

3. Play second fiddle

- play a supporting role

ex. I need to practice more. I'm tired of playing second fiddle to the lead actress.

4. Get ducks in a row

- plan well

ex. I'm starting with the mail I got. It's time to get my ducks in a row.

5. Get the show on the road

- begin something

ex. We have enough funds to start the project. Let's get this show on the road.

6. Get a grip

- calm-down

ex. Suise, you need to get a grip on yourself. Help is coming.

7. Get under one's skin

- irritare or anger someone

ex. His mom was shouting in minutes. Peter really know how to get under his mom's skin.

8. Get a kick out of

- enjoy something

ex. No one can get Jimmy to stop watching cat videos. He really gets a kick out of it.

9. Let sleepibg dogs lie

24. A postman delivers a letter to a recipient and lives 24.0 km away from the post office if he drives at a rate of 40.2 km/h how long will it take the postman to reach the recipient's home? Need Help-


Answer:

To determine how long it will take the postman to reach the recipient's home, you can use the formula:

time = distance / rate

In this case, the distance is 24.0 km and the rate is 40.2 km/h, so the time it will take the postman to reach the recipient's home is:

time = 24.0 km / 40.2 km/h

= 0.5982 hours

This can also be expressed as minutes:

time = 0.5982 hours * 60 minutes/hour

= 35.89 minutes

So it will take the postman approximately 35.9 minutes to reach the recipient's home.

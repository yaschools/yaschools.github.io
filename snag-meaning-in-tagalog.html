29. Can you solve this Halloween riddle? Youâ€™ve just been trick-or-treating and snagged a big bar of chocolate! The bar is eight pieces high and nine pieces long. Can you work out how many times you have to break the bar so that it is separated into individual squares?


Answer:

71 times


Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1 : Break the bar of chocolate into 8 rows.

Number of times you need to break = 7


Step 2: Break each of the row into 9 pieces

Number of times you need to break for each row = 8

Number of times you need to break for 8 rows = 8 x 8 = 64


Step 3: Find the total number of times:

Total number of times = 7 + 64 = 71


Answer: It takes 71 times to separate the bar into individual squares

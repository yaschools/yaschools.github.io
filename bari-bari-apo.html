Problem:The electrical current, in amperes, in a circuit varies directly as the voltage. When 15 voltage are applied, the current is 5 amperes. What is the current when 18 volts are applied?​

Solution:

k = constant of variation

I = kV

V = 15 volts

I = 5 amperes

I = kV

5 = k(15)

k = 5/15

k = 1/3

I = kV

I = (1/3)(18)

I = 6 amperes

Answer:I = 6 amperes

Before an earthquake

1. pack some clothes for emergency purposes

2. listen to the radio or watch some news

3. be alert

4. charge your phones or flashlight to use when the earthquake is in night time

5. guide your family members what to do when the earthquake comes

BURI —

Tagalog Term:

Ang Buri ay ang pinakakaraniwang palad na matatagpuan sa bansa, Pilipinas. Ang pang-agham na pangalan ng halaman na ito ay Corypha Elata Roxb. Ang halaman ng Buri ay nabubuhay ng humigit-kumulang hanggang sa higit sa 30 taon. Lumalaki ito sa buong bansa sa mababa at katamtamang altitude

English Term:

Buri is the most common palm found in the country, Philippines. The scientific name of this plant is Corypha Elata Roxb. The Buri plant lives approximately up to more than 30 years. It grows all over the country at low and medium altitudes

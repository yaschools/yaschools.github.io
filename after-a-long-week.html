4. A woman decides to go for a 10-minute run every day this week and plans to increase the time of her daily run by 4 minutes each week. Write a formula for the time of her run after n weeks. How long will her daily run be 8 weeks from today?


Answer: The formula is an = 10 + 4(n-1) or an = 4n + 6.

After 8 weeks, she will run for 38 minutes.

Step-by-step explanation: We can apply the formula in arithmetic sequence.

an = a1 + (n-1)d

an = 10 + (n-1)4

an = 10 + 4(n-1)

an = 10 + 4n - 4 (using distributive property)

an = 4n + 10 - 4 (combine like terms)

an = 4n + 6

So the time she will run in n weeks depends on this equation.

In 8 weeks:

an = 4(8) + 6 = 32 + 6 = 38 minutes

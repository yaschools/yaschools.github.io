23. Make a Reflection of Psalm 23:1-4​


Answer:

The entire Psalm is a proclamation of what it means to be a child of God, or a shepherd's flock. It's a Psalm about what it's like to be alive. To illustrate our God's knowledge, might, and mercy, the psalm uses the metaphor of a shepherd caring for his flock. It signifies that the Lord is still with us, no matter where we are. He would never abandon us, just as a shepherd would never abandon his sheep. He allows us to take a rest whenever we are tired. He leads us in the proper direction. If we choose to go the wrong route at times in our lives, he will undoubtedly correct us. He is our compass. We will not be terrified even if we are walking down the darkest alleys because God is with us. He will protect and comfort us against our enemies.

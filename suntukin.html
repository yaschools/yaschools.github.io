28. what do the boy's half a day experiences in school symbolizes? suntukin ko baga niyo pag di tama sagot


Answer:

The “half a day” spent in school is thus an allegory for the way in which an entire lifetime can seem to last only “half a day.” The school represents what one might call the “school of life,” as all of the events that take place there are allegorical for the human condition and the human experience of life.

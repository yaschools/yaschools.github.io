4. which paper is made from cardstock and even after the tabs get tattered the folder face are often still in good shape​


Answer:

Cardstock

Explanation:

Manila folders are made from cardstock, and even after the tabs get tattered, the folder faces are often still in good shape. Card stock, sometimes spelled “cardstock”, is thicker and more durable than regular printer paper, but it's thinner and more flexible than cardboard. This balance between durability and flexibility makes cardstock ideal for a number of crafting and design applications.

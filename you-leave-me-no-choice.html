Mixtures are a physical combination of different substances. The components of mixtures retain their identity and composition. Their components can be separated.

Below are some separation techniques:

Hand picking is a technique if the mixture is made of materials that are large enough to be picked by hand. It is also one of the various methods which are carried out in dry conditions

Sieving is a method of separating solid mixtures of different sizes. It makes use of sieve wherein materials of smaller size are allowed to pass through the sieve, leaving the larger components.

Decantation is the process of separating components of a suspension. A suspension is made of liquid and insoluble solid. Evaporation is a technique used to separate out homogenous mixtures where the solid is dissolved in a liquid. This method allows the liquid components to evaporate and separate from the solid components, leaving the solid component. The process usually involves heating the mixture until no more liquid is left.

Distillation is a method to separate mixtures comprised of two or more liquids. In this process, the components of a liquid mixture are vaporized and then condensed and isolated.

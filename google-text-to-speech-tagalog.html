1. ANY POEM tagalog that not in Google​


Pag-ibig sa Mundo

Sa lahat ng mga puso,
Ito'y magiging kalooban natin.

Isang pag-ibig na walang hanggan,
Mapalawak hanggang sa buong mundo.

Isang pag-ibig na walang hanggan,
Kahit ano pa ang mangyari.

Isang pag-ibig na walang hanggan,
Para sa bawat tao sa lahat ng lugar.

Isang pag-ibig na walang hanggan,
Para sa bawat taong nagmamahal.

Isang pag-ibig na walang hanggan,
At hindi maglalaho sa tuwing mayroong kalungkutan.

Sa pagkakaisa ng mga puso,
Kaya natin ito'y magagawang mahalin.

Isang pag-ibig na walang hanggan,
Magmamana sa lahat ng henerasyon.

Here is a short Tagalog poem:

Walang hininga ang hangin

Sa gitna ng gabi

Ang langit ay parang ulap

Na nakalutang sa malayo

Sa kalawakan ng buwan

Makikita ang mga bituin

Na nagtatanghal ng kanilang liwanag

Sa dalampasigan ng langit

Ang kalangitan ay parang isang palabas

Na walang katapusan

Na patuloy na nagbibigay ng saya

Sa mga taong nakatingin sa langit.

In English, this poem could be translated as:

The air is breathless

In the middle of the night

The sky is like a cloud

Floating far away

In the vastness of the moon

We can see the stars

Showing their light

On the shore of the sky

The sky is like a never-ending show

That continues to bring joy

To those who look at the sky.

7. The Presentation app included in Google Drive is called? a. Google Docs b. Google Sheets c. Prezi d. Google Slides


The answer is letter D. Google Slides .

Google Slides is a web based software, and a free presentation program offered by Google. It allows user  to create and edit presentations online.

Google Docs is a web-based software office suite offered by Google within its Google Drive service. It is a free word processor program. It allows users to create and edit files online. It is available as a web application, and also as mobile app. The app is compatible with Microsoft Office file formats. Service also includes Google Sheets and Google Slides.

Google Sheets is a web-based program offered by Google within its Google Drive service. It is also available as a web application, and mobile app. It is a free spreadsheet program. It allows users to create and edit files online.

Prezi is a short term for “presentation” in Hungarian. It is a presentation software company founded in 2009. Presentations features a map-like overview, unlike a traditional slide based presentation. It allows users to pan between topics, and zoom in on details, and pull back to reveal context.

12. what is density? your own explanation, not from google. english or tagalog. definition not formula on how to get it. thank you


Answer:

Density is a measure of how much mass is contained in a certain volume of an object or substance. It is a way to describe how tightly packed the particles or molecules are within a given amount of space. For example, if you have two objects of the same size but one is much heavier than the other, it has a higher density because it has more mass within the same volume. Another way to think of it is as the "heaviness" of an object or substance. When we say something is dense, we mean that it has a lot of mass packed into a small space, while something that is less dense has less mass in the same amount of space sheesh.

28. Ano Yahoo ? ang Pagkakaiba ng GOOGLE AT YAHOO tagalog​


Answer:

Yahoo vs Google

Ang Yahoo at Google ay dalawang mga site na nakikipagkumpitensya upang magbigay ng iba't ibang uri ng mga serbisyo sa mga gumagamit. Ngayon, nagsimula na silang mag-iba-iba sa ibang mga serbisyo. Madali mong makilala ang dalawa sa lalong madaling ipasok mo ang kani-kanilang mga home page. Ang Google ay, una at pangunahin, isang search engine at ito ay ang pinaka-nangingibabaw na search engine para sa isang malaking haba ng panahon. Lumalaki ito sa simplistic format nito at kakayahang malaman kung ano ang hinahanap ng mga gumagamit. Ang Yahoo ay inilipat mula sa pagiging isang normal na search engine sa isang web portal. Sa sandaling maipasok mo ang kanilang site, napupuno ka ng lahat ng pinakabagong balita sa kasalukuyang mga kaganapan, aliwan, palakasan, at marami pang iba.

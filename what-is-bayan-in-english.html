The song "Gawing Langit ang Mundo" by Siakol presents characters who are oppressed and inferior. The song describes the poor people who are hungry and struggling to survive in their impoverished communities. These people are oppressed by the wealthy and powerful who have control over the country's resources and government.

Marxism is evident in the content of the song. The lyrics describe the struggle between the oppressed and oppressors, with the oppressed being the working class and the oppressors being the wealthy elite. The song encourages people to unite and fight against oppression to create a better world for everyone.

The oppression shown in the song is economic oppression. The poor people are struggling to survive because they do not have access to resources and opportunities that are controlled by the wealthy elite. The song highlights how this economic oppression leads to social inequality and injustice.

The song encourages people to unite and fight against oppression. It motivates people to work together to create a better world where everyone has access to resources and opportunities. The message of the song is that we can make a difference if we work together towards a common goal.

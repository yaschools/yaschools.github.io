24. MARIA is a middle class citizen earning 25,000 a month. Just enough for her family. One day, as she was watching the Tv, she was touched by the sacrifices given by the frontliners. So ,she decided to help them through giving of relief goods. She has 3,000 pesos in her wallet: 2 , one thousands, 1, five hundred and 5 one hundreds. She bought 9 trays of eggs for 20 families. Each tray contains 30 eggs that cost 230 pesos. How much is her change?


Answer:

Marias change is 930 pesos.

Answer:

30 pesos

Step-by-step explanation:

The problem gives more information than what is actually needed, so we determine which ones are useful to determine the change. First, let us determine the total cost of the donation of eggs.

Total spent = # of trays bought * cost per tray

Total spent = 9 * 230

Total spent = 2070

Then, from this we can use the contents of her wallet to determine what bills she actually has to use. Maria does NOT need to use all the bills as when she pays with what she has with her wallet, she'd most likely give the lowest possible combination.

Paying Php2070 would most likely entail that she pays Php 2100. We have to check if this is possible with her bills. She has 2 1000s, 1 500s, and 5 100s, so we can make 2100 with the following combination:

2100 = 2 ( 1000 ) + 1 ( 100)

We then get the change if she payed 2100,

2100 - 2070 = 30

Therefore, her change is 30 pesos.

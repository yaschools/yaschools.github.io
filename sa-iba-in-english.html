19. ano english ng wag basta basta magtiwala sa iba​


Answer:

"Wag basta magtiwala sa iba" or in english; "Don't/Do not just trust others"

Meaning: "Don't trust others so easy". Even though they did something good to us, we don't trust them just as fast. We need to know them more and more before our trust occur to them. Because if we do, we may be tricked or fooled by theirs tricks. Don't be foolish, as if we are young, we should conquer our trust to others. Always remember that "TRUST IS EASY TO GIVE, BUT NOT EASY TO FIND, AND NOT SO EASY TO BUILD" / "TRUST IS ALSO HARD TO BUILD, BUT IS EASY TO LOSE". So before trusting someone, make sure you won't be fooled in the very first beginning 'til the end.

#CarryOnLearning#TrustMatters

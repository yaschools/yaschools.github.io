3. Elsa is three times as old as Anna. 6 years from now, Elsa will be twice as old as Anna? how old is Elsa now?


Solution;

Let:

E = Elsa's age

A = Anna's age

E = 3A

E + 6 = 2(A + 6)

3A + 6 = 2A + 12

3A - 2A = 12 - 6

A = 6 years old

E = 3A

E = 3(6)

E = 18 years old

25. Elsa is sitting very still on a chaor. but jose claims that elsa is actually moving. jose is true if the point of reference is the


"Chair"

A reference point is a place or object used for comparison to determine if something is in motion. An object is in motion if it changes position relative to a reference point.

Deciding if an object is moving isn’t as easy as you might think.

For example, you are probably sitting in a chair as you read this book. Are you moving? An object is in motion if its distance from another object is changing.

Because your distance from your chair is not changing, you are not in motion.

To decide if you are moving, you use your chair as a reference point.




How about Relative Motion?


Are you moving as you read this?

The answer to that question depends on your reference point. When your chair is your reference point, you are not moving. But if you choose another reference point, you may be moving.

Suppose you choose the sun as a reference point instead of your chair. If you compare yourself to the sun, you are moving quite rapidly. This is because you and your chair are on Earth, which moves around the sun. Earth moves about 30 kilometers every second.

So you, your chair, this article you're reading, and everything else on Earth move that quickly as well.

27. Elsa static or dynamic?


A static character refers to the character that does not undergo change in terms of his or her personality and perspective while a dynamic character refers to the character that undergo transition and change because of his or her experience and challenges encountered.

 

Elsa, the main character in the movie Frozen, is a dynamic character. It is so because her character has a transition from being reserved and scared of her own power to expressing her power by creating a castle and a beautiful bridge made of ice. It is also observed that because of her love for her sister and her kingdom, she managed to change to someone compassionate and strong.

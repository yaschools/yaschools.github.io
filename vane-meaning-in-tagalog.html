15. what is the purpose of a wind vane?


Answer:

The wind vane, used for indicating wind direction, is one of the oldest meteorological instruments. When mounted on an elevated shaft or spire, the vane rotates under the influence of the wind such that its center of pressure rotates to leeward and the vane points into the wind

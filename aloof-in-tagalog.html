1. what is the meaning of aloof​


Answer:

Aloof is an adjective that describes someone who is emotionally distant or reserved. It is used to describe someone who is not very friendly or sociable, and tends to keep a certain distance from others. This can be due to a lack of interest, shyness, or a desire for privacy. Aloof individuals may come across as detached, unapproachable, or even snobbish. The word can also be used to describe someone who is physically separated from a group or situation. In summary, someone who is aloof is someone who keeps a certain distance, either emotionally or physically, from others.


2. what is aloof antonym or synonym​


Some common synonyms of aloof are detached, disinterested, incurious, indifferent, and unconcerned. While all these words mean "not showing or feeling interest," aloof suggests a cool reserve arising from a sense of superiority or disdain for inferiors or from shyness.

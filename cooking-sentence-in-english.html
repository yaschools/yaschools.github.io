Baked Shrimp with Tomatillos

1. Heat oil in the pan you will use for baking. Add the onions and jalapeños, cook for five minutes on medium heat until the onions begin to brown.

2. Add the gralic and cook a minute more. Add the tomatillos, reduce heat to medium and cook for 10 minutes, until the tomatillos are cooked through, but still hold their shape.

3. Sprinkle salt over the tomatillos as they are cooking.

4. If using clam juice, add to pan, turn up the heat to reduce by half. If using water, just add the 1/4 cup of water without reducing.

5. Add the cheese and shrimp. Cook in a preheated 425*F oven for 10 mins.

6. Remove pan from the oven. Right before serving, mix in the cilantro and sprinkle lime juice and freshly groung black pepper.

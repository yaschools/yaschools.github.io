6. explain ng tagalog need lang plssStructures that are built to retain vertical or nearly vertical earth banks or any other material are called retaining walls. Retaining walls may be constructed of masonry or sheet piles Whatever may be the type of wall, all the walls listed above have to withstand lateral pressures either from NT earth or any other material on their faces. The pressures acting on the walls try to move the walls from their position. The walls should be so designed as to keep them stable in their position. Gravity walls resist movement because of their heavy sections. They are built of mass concrete or stone or brick masonry. A No reinforcement is required in these walls. in all these cases, the backfill tries to move the wall from its position. The movement of the wall is partly resisted by the wall itself and partly by soil in front of the wall. Sheet pile walls are more flexible than the other types​


Answer:

Ang mga istrukturang itinayo upang mapanatili ang patayo o halos patayong mga bangko ng lupa o anumang iba pang materyal ay tinatawag na mga retaining wall. Ang mga retaining wall ay maaaring gawa sa masonry o sheet piles Anuman ang uri ng pader, ang lahat ng mga pader na nakalista sa itaas ay kailangang makayanan ang mga lateral pressure mula sa NT earth o anumang iba pang materyal sa kanilang mga mukha. Ang mga pressure na kumikilos sa mga pader ay sinusubukang ilipat ang mga pader mula sa kanilang posisyon. Ang mga dingding ay dapat na idinisenyo upang mapanatili silang matatag sa kanilang posisyon. Ang mga pader ng gravity ay lumalaban sa paggalaw dahil sa kanilang mabibigat na seksyon. Ang mga ito ay gawa sa mass concrete o bato o brick masonry. A Walang reinforcement ang kailangan sa mga pader na ito. sa lahat ng mga kasong ito, sinusubukan ng backfill na ilipat ang pader mula sa posisyon nito. Ang paggalaw ng pader ay bahagyang nilalabanan ng dingding mismo at bahagyang ng lupa sa harap ng dingding. Ang mga dingding ng sheet pile ay mas nababaluktot kaysa sa iba pang mga uri.

16. Answer these questions in Tagalog Cardiovascular Endurance meaning __________ Muscular strenght meaning ______________ body compositon meaning________ flexibility meaning _________ Muscular Endurance meanign __________]


Explanation:

—Cardiovascular endurance is how efficiently your heart, blood vessels, and lungs to supply oxygen rich blood to working muscles during physical activity (aerobic activity like walking, running, cycling or playing a sport) for a prolonged period of time or for more than 90 seconds.

— Muscular strength is the amount of force you can put out or the amount of weight you can lift. Muscular endurance is how many times you can move that weight without getting exhausted (very tired).

—Flexibility is the ability of a joint or series of joints to move through an unrestricted, pain free range of motion. Although flexibility varies widely from person to person, minimum ranges are necessary for maintaining joint and total body health.

—Muscular endurance is the ability of a muscle or group of muscles to perform repetitive contractions against a force for an extended period of time. The greater your muscular endurance the higher number of repetitions you could complete.

Answer:

ito ang halaga ng pera ng ibang bansa kumpara sa iyong sarili. Kung naglalakbay ka sa ibang bansa, kailangan mong "bumili" ng lokal na pera. Tulad ng presyo ng anumang assets, ang exchange rate ay ang presyo kung saan maaari kang bumili ng pera na iyon.

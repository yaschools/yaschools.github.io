27. What kind of knife is used in removing the hard core of a cabbage?a. butcher knife b. channel knife c. cutting knifed. paring knife


TLE:  TYPES OF KNIVES  

What kind of knife is used in removing the hard core of a cabbage?

a. butcher knife

b. channel knife  

c. cutting knife

d. paring knife

Answer

The correct answer is Letter D. The paring knife is the most appropriate tool to use in the situation of removing the hard core of a vegetable or fruit. There is a designed curve for this kind of knife that can easily help to peel and cut rounded type of vegetables and fruits. So, in order to remove the card core of a cabbage, the proper knife to use is the paring knife.  

Most common types of Knives Butcher Knife is a knife used to slice or butcher meats. Channel Knife is a knife that is commonly used to peel. Cutting Knife is a knife usually for cutting the fruit or vegetables into pieces only .Paring Knife is a small kitchen tool commonly used in food preparations for peeling fruits and vegetables to slicing or cutting it into pieces.

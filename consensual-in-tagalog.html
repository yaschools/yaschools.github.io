26. What is political consensus ?


Answer:

There is Political consensus  when a large proportion of the members of a society agree about how decisions regarding the distribution of values should be made. It is common in most countries who are practicing democracy. Political consensus, in  any  country and organization aims to avoid more criticisms and disagreement from people or various parties regarding certain agreements. It aims to maintain civil peace among the society. Further is a major generating factor for the following:

Establishment of State and its FunctionPolitical FrameworkStrategic Decision-makingEstablishment and Maintaining Relationship with International Organizations

Running a country is not always easy. It takes brilliant ideas from brilliant minds to come-up with best strategies for economic progress, strong ideas for political framework and good decision-making skills of all people involved. It is in this progressive work that political consensus must come in. Without political consensus, there would be chaos in the various institutions promulgating laws for the benefit of everyone and most especially in the society.

28. Consensual or victimless crime definition

A consensual crime is a public-order crime that involves more than one participant, all of whom give their consent as willing participants in an activity that is unlawful. 


29. consensus view of crime?​

30. consensual na komunikasyon pamilya


Answer:

Mga paraan upang mapaunlad ang Consensual na komunikasyon pampamilya

1. Magkaroon ng bukas na komunikasyon upang ang bawat kasapi ng pamilya ay magkaunawaan at magkaintindihan

2. Ipahayag ang saloobin at nararamdaman sa bawat kasapi ng pamilya upang masolusyunan kung mayroon mang problema o pagsubok na pinagdadaanan

3. Irespeto ang opinyo ng bawat kasapi ng pamilya

4. Magkais at magtulungan at magkakasamang harapin ang mga pagsubok na pagdaraanan

5. Tanggapin ang mga pagkakamaling nagawa

6. Huwag matakot ibahagi ang problema lalo na sa mga magulang upang ikaw ay matulungan masolusyunan ito

7. Matutong makinig sa magulang at sa iba pang kasapi ng pamilya

Ang isang pamilyang may pagkakaunawaan at magandang ugnayan ang nagbubuklod sa kanila. Ang pagkakaroon ng pagkakaunawaan o pagkakainitindihan ay magdadala sa bawat isa ng bukas na komunikasyon, ito ay paraan upang ang bawat kasapi ng pamilya ay magkaunawaan at magkaintidihan.

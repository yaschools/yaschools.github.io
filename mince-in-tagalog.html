1. If 250g of minced beef is used in a pie, how many kilograms of minced beef would be needed for 45 pies


Answer:

11.25 kilograms

Step-by-step explanation:

250 grams x 45 pies = 11,250 grams

since 1 kilo = 1000 grams you can now convert it.

[tex]11250grams \: \times \: \frac{1 \: kilograms}{1000 \: grams} \: = \: 11.25 \: kilo[/tex]

3. it is the step pattern of mincing


Answer:

Counts 1, 2, 3. Starting position: R (L) foot in fifth position in front, heels slightly raised or with the leading foot flat on the floor. Execute tiny steps sideward right (left) as many times as necessary. In ¾ time take one step for every count. In 2/4 time it maybe 2 steps for every count.

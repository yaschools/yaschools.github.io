6. paunang sipat sa dokumentasyong - dihital sa preserbasyon ng mga pamanang kultural​


Ang unang diskarte sa digital na dokumentasyon sa pangangalaga ng kultural na pamana ay ang kadalian ng pag-access at pagkuha ng impormasyon.

Sa pamamagitan ng digital na dokumentasyon, maaari nitong gawing mas madali para sa mga tao na makilala at mapanatili ang kultural na pamana. Higit pa rito, ang pamana ng kultura na may digital na dokumentasyon ay maaaring magbigay ng pangkalahatang-ideya ng komunidad nang hindi kinakailangang bumisita nang personal. Kaya ito ay magiging mas epektibo.

Samakatuwid, ang diskarte sa pagbawi ng impormasyon ay naglalayong mapanatili ang pamanang pangkultura na ito. Hindi bababa sa, ang impormasyong ibinigay mula sa isang kultural na pamana ay maaaring ipalaganap. Kung mangyari ang mga bagay na hindi kanais-nais, tulad ng pinsala sa pamana ng kultura, kung gayon sa digital na dokumentasyon, maaari pa ring malaman at matutunan ng mga tao ang tungkol sa pamana ng kultura.

16. English 1. What is the history about Sipat? Why do we celebrate it? 2. How is it important in our present situation? Doest it have meaning in our life?​


In honor of the 36th anniversary of the famous Mount Data "Sipat," Malacaang officially proclaimed Tuesday, September 13, 2022, as a special non-working holiday in the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR). One of the historical days was Sipat, when the Philippine government and the Cordillera Bodong Administration-Cordillera People's Liberation Army (CBA-CPLA) exchanged peace tokens. On September 13, 1986, representatives of the CBA-CPLA led by the rebel priest Conrado Balweg met with former president Corazon Aquino and high-ranking members of the national government for the Sipat. As a result, armed hostilities between government forces and the armed group were put to an end, and the group's desire for autonomy was recognized.

According to the Palace, it is appropriate that the people of the CAR be given full opportunity to celebrate and actively participate in marking one of the historically important events that had occurred in the region, of course with some appropriate ceremonies subject to the adoption of public health measures by the federal government.

Therefore, it can be concluded that Sipat is celebrated as a way for the CAR people to remember the contributions made by the heroes who took part in the historic event. It is also an expression of gratitude for this occasion, which has allowed us to live in peace.

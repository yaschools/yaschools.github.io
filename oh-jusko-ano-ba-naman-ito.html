✏️Simplify 4x - 5y - 2(x + y) + 3(x - y + 2) : 5x-10y+6

Steps

4x - 5y - 2(x + y) + 3(x - y + 2)

Expand - 2(x + y) / - 2 * x - 2y

= 4x - 5y-2x-2y+3(x-y+2)

Show Steps

Expand 3 (x - y + 2) : 3x - 3y + 6

= 4x - 5y - 2x - 2y + 3x - 3y +6

Show Steps

Group like terms

= 4x - 2x + 3x-5y-2y-3y +6

Add similar elements: 4x - 2x + 3x = 5x

= 5x - 5y-2y - 3y + 6

Add similar elements: - 5y - 2y - 3y = - 10y

= 5x - 10y + 6

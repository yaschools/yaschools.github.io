10. what is socialization? and how does it influence our personality? give a real scenario. TAGALOG at least 3 sentence​


Pakikipag-ugnayan o pakikipag-kapwa tao ay tunay na mahalagang aspeto ng social life. Ang pagsusuri ng ating mga pakikipag-ugnayan dito sa Pilipinas ay nagpapahayag nang maraming bagay tungkol sa ating pagkatao. Ang mga sosyal na pakikipag-ugnayan ay dapat maging makabuluhang pokus ng pagsusuri sa prosesong pagkilala ng konseptong kapwa.

Ang konsepto ng pakikipagkapwa ay lumalabas na mahalaga hindi lang sa sikolohiyang aspeto, kundi pati na rin sa pilosopikal na aspeto. Habang ang pagtutunguhan ay tumutukoy sa lahat ng antas ng ugnayan, tanging ang salitang pakikipagkapwa lamang ang maaaring gamitin sa parehong paraan (na tumutukoy ito sa lahat ng antas ng ugnayan) at maaari ring nagpapahiwatig ito ng ideya, kahalagahan/pag-uugali (value) o paninindigan (conviction) na siyang pinakamahalaga sa mga Pilipino.

Ang pagtutunguhan rin naman ay nagpapahiwatig ng mababaw (superficial) na ugnayan: level of amenities habang ang pakikipagkapwa ang tumutukoy sa tunay na pagka-makatao ("humaneness to its highest level") ayon kay Santiago (1976). Ang pakikipagkapwa ay mas malalim sa kanyang mga ipinapahiwatig (panindigan, ideya, kahalagahan/pag-uugali). Nangagahulugan din ito bilang pagtatanggap at pakikitungo sa ibang tao bilang kapantay, katulad.

26. 2. what are the different superstitious beliefs of the Tagalog? How does it shapes the lives of the natives during that time? (10 points)​


Answer:

There are many different superstitious beliefs that were held by the Tagalog people, a culturally and linguistically diverse group who lived in what is now the central and southern regions of the Philippines. Some examples of these beliefs include:

Belief in good and bad omens: The Tagalog believed that certain events or objects could be signs of good or bad luck. For example, seeing a black cat might be considered a bad omen, while finding a four-leaf clover was considered a good one.

Belief in spirits and supernatural beings: The Tagalog believed in the existence of a variety of supernatural beings, such as spirits of the dead, guardian angels, and nature spirits. These beings were thought to be able to influence the lives of the living in both positive and negative ways.

Belief in magic and spells: The Tagalog believed in the power of magic and spells, and many people consulted with shamans or other spiritual practitioners in order to seek guidance or protection.

Belief in astrology: The Tagalog believed that the movements of the stars and planets could have an influence on people's lives, and many people consulted with astrologers to seek guidance on important decisions.

These superstitious beliefs shaped the lives of the Tagalog in many ways. For example, they may have influenced people's decision-making, caused them to take certain precautions or rituals to avoid bad luck, or led them to seek out the help of spiritual practitioners in order to address problems or seek blessings.


27. TAGALOG:Paano nakakaapekto ang posisyon ng buwan na may kaugnayan sa Araw at Lupa hitsura?ENGLISH:How does the position of the moon relative to the Sun and Earth affect its appearance?​


Answer:

Tagalog

Ang kaugnay na posisyon ng ating Araw, Daigdig, at Buwan, ay sanhi ng mga pagbabagong ito. Tulad ng pag-ikot ng ating Buwan sa paligid ng Daigdig, ang panig na nakaharap sa Araw ay laging naiilawan, tulad ng liwanag ng araw na bahagi ng Earth na naiilawan ng Araw. ... Mula sa Lupa, ang ibabaw ng Buwan ay mukhang madilim sapagkat ang iluminadong bahagi ay nakaharap palayo sa Earth.

English

The relative positions of our Sun, Earth, and Moon, cause these changes. As our Moon orbits around Earth, the side facing the Sun is always illuminated, just like Earth's daylight side is illuminated by the Sun. ... From Earth, the Moon's surface looks dark because the illuminated side is facing away from Earth.

Parents role in inculcating values to their children

Parents should instill in their kids the importance of maturity, assertiveness, and physical fitness in order to help them develop a positive personality. This is a fundamental responsibility that every parent should undertake in order to raise their children successfully. As a child gets older, their emotions and cognitive abilities develop. Values are important in parenting because they have a significant impact on our actions and attitudes, as well as our choices and interpersonal interactions. For a value to truly be yours, it must be lived out in action; it cannot merely be accepted or considered to be suitable behavior.

Let's define what parents should do for their children.

Parents are children's first teachers and role models. They are in responsible of establishing positive values in children and shaping their behavior. Children observe, mimic, and imitate their parents. Therefore, it's imperative that they behave in a way that youngsters will desire to imitate.

Parents play a significant role in their children's life. They provide the foundation for love, assistance, and direction. Parental support in a child's growth extends beyond simple guiding.

Parents start to act as their children's adjustment egos long before they are born. Nobody else has the same ability to influence a child's development and conduct.

As they get older, children develop emotionally, cognitively, and physically. Along with inspiring their children to seek successful occupations or building their self-esteem, parents should also instill in them the value of ethics in daily life. Parents should teach their kids how to be brave and mature in the face of difficult situations. They should also show to their children how to correctly apply the concepts from their theory to solve difficult problems in reality.

30. what do the parents perceive as their role in relation to the daycare worker?​


Many parents today use daycare services for their children. Usually, they use daycare services so that their children can still receive care when the parents do not have time and usually because they are busy working. And while in daycare, children will get many things, such as socializing. In daycare, of course, our children will meet other children who are the same age as them. This can teach our children how to socialize before they later go to school. Not only that, while in daycare our children will get healthy food, to games to validate their learning abilities.

Pro of using daycare service:

1. Generally, daycare services are cheaper than babysitters

2. Someone is watching our children while we are working.

3. There are facilities for parents such as CCTV to be able to monitor their children remotely

4. children can learn and socialize.

5. Experienced and professional caregivers.

Counter of using daycare service:

1. the cleanliness of the furniture or toys in the daycare may be lacking.

2. Centers that care for infants can be hard to find or full, with months-long waiting lists.

3. Kids get sick more often in group care, and most centers won't provide care for sick children.

4. Most are closed during major holidays.

5. Pickup and drop-off times can be rigid.

As a parent, even though the role of caring for children has been assisted by daycare services, it does not mean that their full role will be replaced. Each parent and daycare caregiver has its own role to achieve the same goal, which is to make the child have basic knowledge such as the alphabet, numbers, shapes, and colors well. To achieve this goal, parents and daycare workers must work together. They can establish good communication about their child's development so that they know how far their child's progress is at home or at daycare.

16. Anu sa tagalog yung motivation?


Tagalog ng Motivation

Ang motivation ay salitang Ingles na motibasyon sa salitang Tagalog. Motibasyon ang literal na tagalog sa pagsasalin ng salita. Ang iba pang kahulugan o katumbas ng salitang motibasyon ay ang mga sumusunod;

InspirasyonDahilan ng paggawa ng isang bagayPagkakaroon ng InteresNag-udyok upang gawinNaghikayat upang gawin

Sa ibang salita, ang motibasyon ay ang sanhi o dahilan ng paggawa ng isang bagay. Maaaring ito ang udyok upang ipagpatuloy o magpursige sa isang hangarin o ninanais. Ang motibasyon ang sinasabing pagkakaroon ng lakas ng loob at pagharap sa lahat ng pasubok sa pagpapatuloy ng buhay.

Paggamit sa Motibasyon sa PangungusapAng pamilya ko ang aking motibasyon upang magtrabaho sa ibang bansa kahit mahirap.Motibasyon ko ang pag-ahon sa kahirapan kaya nagsusumikap akong makatapos ng pag-aaral.Nawawala sa landas at tinatamad ang mga kabataan kapag wala silang motibasyon sa buhay.

26. self motivation after taking exam​


Answer:

Self-motivation after taking an exam can be a challenge, especially if you did not perform as well as you had hoped. However, it is important to remember that one exam does not define your intelligence or capabilities. Instead of dwelling on your mistakes or dwelling on your low grades, try to focus on the things that you did well and what you can learn from the experience.

Here are a few ways to stay motivated after taking an exam:

Set new goals: Set specific, achievable goals for yourself and make a plan to achieve them. This can help you focus on the future and stay motivated to continue learning and improving.

Celebrate your accomplishments: It's important to recognize and celebrate your accomplishments, no matter how small they may seem. This can help boost your confidence and motivation.

Seek support: Surround yourself with positive, supportive people who can help keep you motivated. This might include friends, family members, or a mentor.

Take a break: It's okay to take a break and relax after an exam, but be sure to balance rest with productivity. Avoid procrastinating or dwelling on your grades for too long.

Remember, it's okay to make mistakes or not perform as well as you had hoped on an exam. What's important is how you choose to respond to the experience and how you use it to learn and grow.

28. actions toweards transcendence of self motivation​


Answer:

According to Maslow, self-transcendence brings the individual what he termed “peak experiences” in which they transcend their own personal concerns and see from a higher perspective. These experiences often bring strong positive emotions like joy, peace, and a well-developed sense of awareness (Messerly, 2017).

Explanation:

The quintessential example of self-transcendence is undoubtedly Viktor Frankl's experience in the concentration camps of World War II. Despite his great personal suffering (and frequently having few or none of the hierarchy needs met)—or perhaps because of it—Frankl found a higher purpose in his life.

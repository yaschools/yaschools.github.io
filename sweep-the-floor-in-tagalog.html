30. Encircle the correct verb form that will complete each sentence Cinderella (sweep,sweeps,sweepes)the dirty floor?​


Answer:

#KeepLearning

I hope this helped you:)

ANSWER:

Cinderella (sweep,sweeps,sweepes)the dirty floor.

SENTENCE:

Cinderella sweeps the dirty floor.

-----------------------------------------------------------------

The answer is sweeps since the subject(Cinderella) is singular. If the subject is plural, the verb is sweep.

Example:

Mae and Anna sweep the dirty floor.

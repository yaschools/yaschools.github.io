13. List down at least 3 limiting factors that could lead to the species extinction explain each. Patulong po sa mga genius jan wag paepal mga jejemon mga uhay sa points yawa​


Answer:

Global Climate Change, Deforestation and Invasion of Natural Environment or Habitat of other species, can lead to species Extinction.

Explanation:

Global Climate Change-

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns.

Deforestation-

Deforestation or forest clearance is the removal of a forest or stand of trees from land that is then converted to non-forest use.

Invasion of Natural Environment-

Invasion of long-established ecosystems by organisms is a natural phenomenon, but human-facilitated introductions have greatly increased the rate, scale, and geographic range of invasion.

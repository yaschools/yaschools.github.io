9. If 8 trash bags last for 30 days then 28 trash bags will last how many days?​


Answer:

105 days

Computation:

Solving for the number of days that will take 28 trash bags to last given that 8 trash bags last for 30 days.

[tex]28\:\diagdown\!\!\!\!\!trash\:bags\times\frac{30\:days}{8\:\diagdown\!\!\!\!\!trash\:bags}=\frac{840\:days}{8}=\boxed{105\:days}[/tex]

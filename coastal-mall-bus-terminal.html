23. The bus departs the bus terminal every 2 hours. If the first bus is scheduled to depart at 10:35 a.m., when should the second bus depart?​


PROBLEM:

The bus departs the bus terminal every 2 hours. If the first bus is scheduled to depart at 10:35 a.m., when should the second bus depart?

SOLUTION:

• If the bus departs the terminal every two hours and the first scheduled time is at 10:35 a.m, then the second bus depart will be two hours after.

• Thus, from 10:35 a.m, you need to add two hours.

[tex]\large\boxed{ \tt 10:35+2:00=12:35}[/tex]

ANSWER:

• At exactly 12:35 in the afternoon (p.m), the bus will depart for the second time.

27. From the terminal, a bus travels at a rate of 65 kilometers per hour (km/hr). How far will the bus be from the terminal after 3hrs?


21.67 km

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

speed = 65 km/hr

time = 3 hrs

Asked: How far?

Since the asked is how far, then we use the formula distance.

[tex] d = \frac{speed}{time} [/tex]

Substitute the values and we get,

[tex]d = \frac{65 \: km \: per \: hour}{3 \: hours} [/tex]

Cancel the labels and divide the values.

Thus, we get:

21.67 km

10. What are the sources of google’s competitive advantage? 2. what value does google create for customers and advertisers 3. what business-level strategy is google pursuing?


1. What is Google's source of competitive advantage?

The source of Google's competitive advantage is the database infrastructure that has been programmed by Google for many years, the relevance of search results and product offerings from AD words to social networking sites to mobile application platforms being the main source of Google's competitive advantage.

2. What value does Google create for customers and advertisers?

Google delivers Google Ads by aggregating and anonymizing data for ecommerce advertisers to identify profiles of customers who frequently buy or visit certain products based on network traffic. Google is also making it easier to see customer value by age, gender, or audience characteristics

3. What business level strategy is Google pursuing?

Google uses a generic strategy. This strategy involves a wide market scope. The company will offer products to everyone around the world. A generic competitive differentiation strategy involves developing capabilities that create a competitive business atmosphere.

21. What is the difference between google slide in ipad and google slide in desktop computers ?


The main difference between Google Slides on an iPad and Google Slides on a desktop computer is the level of functionality and the user interface.

Google Slides on a desktop computer generally has more features and options than the iPad version, also the user interface of Google Slides on an iPad is optimized for touch-based navigation, and is generally more streamlined than the desktop version.

The iPad version can be used offline but the desktop version does not have offline feature unless you install the google chrome extension to make it available offline.

The desktop version generally has more accessibility options like screen reader, keyboard shortcuts, high-contrast mode, etc. than the iPad version.

The answer is letter D. Google Slides .

It is available as a web application, and also as mobile app. The app is compatible with Microsoft Office file formats. Service also includes Google Sheets and Google Slides.

Google Sheets is a web-based program offered by Google within its Google Drive service. It is also available as a web application, and mobile app. It is a free spreadsheet program. It allows users to create and edit files online.

Prezi is a short term for “presentation” in Hungarian. It is a presentation software company founded in 2009. Presentations features a map-like overview, unlike a traditional slide based presentation. It allows users to pan between topics, and zoom in on details, and pull back to reveal context.

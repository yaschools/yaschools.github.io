24. What is connotations of turmoil,anarchy,hubbub


Answer:

First, we must know what part of speech they belong. These words are all nouns and they are uncountable. Now, let's define each with example.

Turmoil can mean problem, confusion, being in the state of not certain about something, disorder. It has a negative meaning.

Example: The airport officers are in turmoil because of many cancelled flights.

Anarchy is a kind of situation where people are not following rules. Meaning, there is confusion and chaos. This word has a negative meaning too.

Example: There is anarchy in the airport because passengers are angry at the airport officers.

Hubbub can be a situation with too much noise due to some activities.

Example: The hubbub of angry passengers in the airport is due to cancelled flights.

Below is an example on how to use turmoil, anarchy, hubbub in one sentence.

The airport seemed to be in anarchy due to the turmoil of cancelled flights and hubbub of angry passengers.

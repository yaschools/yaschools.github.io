1. Commentaries on doing business amidst pandemic (DAPAT TAGALOG!)


Answer:

Gamit ang isang halo-halong paraan ng diskarte, sinisiyasat ng pag-aaral na ito ang mga hamon na idinulot ng pandemya ng COVID-19 para sa mga negosyante at ang mga diskarte na maaaring gamitin ng mga organisasyon upang makagawa ng pagbabago tungo sa kaligtasan at paglago.

Ginagawa ang empirical analysis sa mga salik na nakaapekto sa mas maliliit na kumpanya sa panahon ng paghina ng ekonomiya sa nakaraan, at mga patakaran at plano na pinagtibay ng mga kumpanya upang malampasan ang mga hadlang. Ang mga salik tulad ng pag-lockdown, remote na pagtatrabaho, digital marketing at digitalization ay isinasaalang-alang sa pamamagitan ng qualitative research.

Sa seksyon ng empirical analysis, ginamit ang mga deskriptibong istatistika at confirmatory factor analysis. Sa seksyong kwalitatibo, ginawa ang thematic analysis at hierarchy chart sa tulong ng NVivo software.

Ang mga pangunahing hadlang na nakaapekto sa mga kumpanya ay ang kahandaan ng organisasyon, suporta sa imprastraktura ng gobyerno, kakulangan sa teknolohiya at mga krisis sa pananalapi.

Ang mga pangunahing istratehiya na binibigyang-diin ng mga tagapagtatag upang malampasan ang mga hadlang ng kahandaan ng organisasyon at krisis sa pananalapi ay ang marketing, strategic networking at produkto at serbisyo, at para sa kawalan ng teknolohiya ay digitization.

8. filipino resiliency amidst the pandemic​


Answer:

The COVID-19 pandemic has brought upon the world with challenges. Almost all countries had to shut down their economies to halt the spread of the virus and the Philippines is one of those countries who have taken strict measures by implementing the Enhanced Community Quarantine on 15 March 2020.

The lockdown did help in controlling the spread of the virus and apparently, the Philippines is already flattening the curve. The number of cases reported in the country is no longer growing at an exponential rate, however, flattening the curve does not necessarily mean that the Philippines is winning its battle against the pandemic. There are other measures to consider to ultimately say that a country is winning the battle.

11. what does it means about the inflation rate of sexual violence amidst the pandemic​


Answer:

One in three women worldwide experience physical or sexual violence mostly by an intimate partner. Violence against women and girls is a human rights violation.

Before the pandemic 243 million women and girls, aged 15-49 experienced sexual and/or physical violence by an intimate partner in the past year. Since the pandemic, violence against women, especially domestic violence has intensified.

Since the outbreak of COVID-19, emerging data and reports from those on the front lines, have shown that all types of violence against women and girls, particularly domestic violence, has intensified.

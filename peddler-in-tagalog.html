14. A peddler earns (12²+m-35) pesos for working (3m-5) hours. How much does he earn per hour?​


Answer:

The peddler earns (4m + 7) per hour.

Step-by-step explanation:

12m² + m - 35

12m² - 20m + 21m - 35

(12m² - 20m) + (21m - 35)

4m(3m - 5) + 7(3m - 5)

(4m + 7)(3m - 5)

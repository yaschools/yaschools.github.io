15. when displayed with another flag, the philippine flag shall be on the right of the other flag. if there is a line of other flags, the philippine flag shall be in the middle of the line​


Answer:

thats

Republic Act No. 8491

Explanation:

SECTION 13.

When displayed with another flag, the Philippine flag shall be on the right of the other flag. If there is a line of other flags, the Philippine flag shall be in the middle of the line.

When carried in a parade with flags, which are not national flags, the Philippine flag shall be in front of the center of the line.

26. list down some activities or practices in the community,school,or institution showing observance to the Flag heraldic code of the Philippines​


Answer:

The following practices are observed in the community, school and other institutions to show respect to upon the implementation of the Flag Heraldic Code of the Philippines:

1. When the National Anthem is sung, the people stand up, put the right hand on their left chest with palm facing the chest.

2. When there are vehicles passing by the school, and the National Anthem is being sung, they need to stop until the song stops.

3. When someone is walking but hears the National Anthem is sung, they also need to stop until the song ends.

4. When the National Anthem is sung, umbrellas, caps or any accessories placed on the head should be temporarily removed or put down.

5. The Philippine flag is handled with respect. It should not reach the ground or worn as a clothing.

6. In school, flag ceremonies are conducting every Monday and flag retreats every Friday.

7. The Boy Scouts of the Philippines teaches cab and boy scouts the proper way of folding the flag.

1. Flag raising ceremonies conducted in different schools and offices before starting its operations.

2. Offering a salute to the Philippine Flag during flag ceremonies.

5. Displaying the Philippine flag in vehicles and buildings during the Philippine Independence Day.

Solution:

1. A flag is raised up a 5m high flag pole in 20 seconds. How fast was the flag raised?

S T/D

= 5m ÷ 20 sec

= 4 m/s (fast was the flag raised)

2. The car is covering a distance of 600m in 15 seconds. What is the velocity of the car?

V = ∆T / ∆D

= 600m ÷ 15 sec

V = 40 m/s (is the velocity of the car)

3. A motorcycle slows down from a speed of 85 km/h to rest in 28 second. What is the acceleration of the car in m/s*?

A = ∆V / ∆T

= 85 km/h (28sec)

A = 2,380 m/s² ( the acceleration of the car in m/s*)

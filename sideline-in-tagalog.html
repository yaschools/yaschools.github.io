6. a tennis coach face back and forth along the sidelines 10 times in 2 minutes what is the frequency of her pacing?​


To find the frequency of the tennis coach's pacing, we need to first calculate the number of times she paces back and forth per second.

We know that she paces back and forth 10 times in 2 minutes, which is equivalent to 120 seconds.

Therefore, her frequency can be calculated as follows:

Frequency = (Number of times back and forth) / Time taken

Frequency = 10 / 120 seconds

Frequency = 0.0833 Hz

So the frequency of the tennis coach's pacing is 0.0833 Hz (or approximately 0.08 Hz).

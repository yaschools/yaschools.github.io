9. The figure shows a stream of water in steady flow from a kitchen faucet. At the faucet, the diameter of the stream is 0.960 cm. The stream fills a 125-cm3 container in 16.3 s. Find the diameter of the stream 13.0 cm below the opening of the faucet.


Answer:

0.25 cm

Explanation:

Formulas:

Bernoulli's Energy Equation

[tex]\dfrac{p_1}{\gamma}+\dfrac{v_1^2}{2g}+z_1=\dfrac{p_2}{\gamma}+\dfrac{v_2^2}{2g}+z_2[/tex]

where:

[tex]p/\gamma[/tex] = pressure head

[tex]v^2/2g[/tex] = velocity head

[tex]z[/tex] = elevation head

Continuity Equation

[tex]Q=A_1v_1=A_2v_2[/tex]

where:

Q = volume flow rate (Volume/time)

A = cross-sectional area

v = velocity

Solution:

Solving for Q

[tex]Q=\dfrac{V}{t}[/tex]

[tex]Q=\dfrac{125}{16.3}[/tex]

[tex]Q=7.66871 \ cm^3/s[/tex]

solving for the velocity at point 2

[tex]Q=A_2v_2[/tex]

[tex]7.66871=\left[\dfrac{\pi}{4}(0.960)^2\right]v_2[/tex]

[tex]v_2=10.59473 \ cm/s[/tex]

[tex]v_2=0.10595 \ m/s[/tex]

Using BEE at point 1 and 2 taking 1 as the datum line

[tex]\dfrac{p_1}{\gamma}+\dfrac{v_1^2}{2g}+z_1=\dfrac{p_2}{\gamma}+\dfrac{v_2^2}{2g}+z_2[/tex]

Note: the pressure at point 1 and 2 is zero because it is open to atmosphere

[tex]\dfrac{v_1^2}{2(9.81)}=\dfrac{(0.10595)^2}{2(9.81)}+0.13[/tex]

[tex]v_1=1.60057 \ m/s[/tex]

[tex]v_1=160.05703 \ cm/s[/tex]

Solving for the diameter

[tex]Q=A_1v_1[/tex]

[tex]7.66871=\left[\dfrac{\pi}{4}D^2\right](160.05703)[/tex]

[tex]D=0.24699 \ cm[/tex]

14. what is meteor stream​


A meteor stream consists of dust released from the nucleus of a comet during its perihelion passage.

Answer:

Meteor shower, temporary rise in the rate of meteor sightings, caused by the entry into Earth’s atmosphere of a number of meteoroids (see meteor and meteoroid) at approximately the same place in the sky and the same time of year, traveling in parallel paths and apparently having a common origin. Most meteor showers are known or believed to be associated with active or defunct comets; they represent Earth’s passage through the orbits of these comets and its collision with the streams of debris (typically of sand-grain to pebble size) that have been left behind. The showers return annually, but, because the densities of meteoroids in the streams (commonly called meteor streams) are not uniform, the intensities of the showers can vary considerably from year to year.

29. paano binago ng pag stream ng musika ang industriya?how music streaming changed the industry?​


Answer:

One of the major ways streaming has affected artists’ lives is through royalty payments. Though streaming is a step up from illegal downloads, which obviously earn artists no money at all, these services give notoriously little to the artists, producers, songwriters and record labels who provide them with music.

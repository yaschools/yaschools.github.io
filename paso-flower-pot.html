15. Allyssa is putting pebbles into flower pots. She put 38 pebbles in the first flower pot, 43 pebbles in the second flower pot, 48 pebbles in the third flower pot, and 53 pebbles in the fourth flower pot. if she continue to put pebbles in that pattern,how many pebbles will Alyssa put in the tenth flower pot?


Answer:

The answer is there are 83 pebbles in the tenth flower pot.

Explanation:

Take a look at these numbers closely.

1st Flower Pot - 38 pebbles

2nd Flower Pot - 43 pebbles

3rd Flower Pot - 48 pebbles

4th Flower Pot - 53 pebbles

You can see that in each flower pot, 5 more pebbles are getting added

every time. Using that pattern, we get a status like this :

[FP = Flower Pot. P= Pebbles]

5th FP - 58 P

6th FP - 63 P

7th FP - 68 P

8th FP - 73 P

9th FP - 78 P

10th FP - 83 P

And that is why there are 83 pebbles in the tenth flower pot.

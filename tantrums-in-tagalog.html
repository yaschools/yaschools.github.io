1. what is temper tantrums


an emotional outburst, usually associated with children or those in emotional distress, that is typically characterized by stubborness, crying, screaming, violence, defiance, angry ranting, a resistance to attempts at pacification, and, in some cases, hitting, and other physically violent behavior.

Answer:

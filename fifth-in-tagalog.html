26. With 10 as the fifth term what is the fifth term in a sequence of multiples of 5?


Answer: 30

Step-by-step explanation: The sequence of multiples of 5 is: 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, ...

Since the fifth term is 10, we can see that it is the result of multiplying 5 by 2.

Therefore, the sixth term in the sequence would be 5 times the next number, which is 6. Thus, the sixth term is:

6 x 5 = 30

So, the answer is 30.

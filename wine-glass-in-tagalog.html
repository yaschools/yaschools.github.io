2. what are the differece between red wine glass and white wine glass?


Answer:

Red wine glasses are typically taller and have a larger bowl than white wine glasses.

Explanation:

As reds are generally bigger and bolder wines, they require a larger glass to allow all those aromas and flavors to emerge.  Generally speaking, white wine glasses have smaller bowls than red wine glasses. The walls of a white wine glass bowl will also be less curved. White wine glasses have much narrower openings than red wine glasses.

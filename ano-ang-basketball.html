7. ano ang rules of basketball?


Answer:

Rules for the offense

The basketball team on offense is the team with the basketball. When a player has the basketball there are certain rules they must follow:

1) The player must bounce, or dribble, the ball with one hand while moving both feet. If, at any time, both hands touch the ball or the player stops dribbling, the player must only move one foot. The foot that is stationary is called the pivot foot.

2) The basketball player can only take one turn at dribbling. In other words, once a player has stopped dribbling they cannot start another dribble. A player who starts dribbling again is called for a double-dribbling violation and looses the basketball to the other team. A player can only start another dribble after another player from either team touches or gains control of the basketball. This is usually after a shot or pass.

3) The ball must stay in bounds. If the offensive team looses the ball out of bounds the other team gets control of the basketball.

4) The players hand must be on top of the ball while dribbling. If they touch the bottom of the basketball while dribbling and continue to dribble this is called carrying the ball and the player will lose the ball to the other team.

5) Once the offensive team crosses half court, they may not go back into the backcourt. This is called a backcourt violation. If the defensive team knocks the ball into the backcourt, then the offensive team can recover the ball legally.

Defensive Rules

The team on defense is the team without the basketball.

1) The main rule for the defensive player is not to foul. A foul is described as gaining an unfair advantage through physical contact. There is some interpretation that has to be made by the referee, but, in general, the defensive player may not touch the offensive player in a way that causes the offensive player to lose the ball or miss a shot.

Rules for everyone

1) Although the foul rule is described above as a defensive rule, it applies exactly the same to all players on the court including offensive players.

2) Basketball players cannot kick the ball or hit it with their fist.

3) No player can touch the basketball while it is traveling downward towards the basket or if it is on the rim. This is called goaltending. (touching the ball on the rim is legal in some games).

Every player on the court is subject to the same rules regardless of the position they play. The positions in basketball are just for team basketball strategy and there are no positions in the rules.

28. ano ang brief history of basketball?


The history of basketball began with its invention in 1891 in Springfield, Massachusetts by Canadian physical education instructor James Naismith as a less injury-prone sport than football. Naismith was a 31-year old graduate student when he created the indoor sport to keep athletes indoors during the winters.[1] The game became established fairly quickly and grew very popular as the 20th century progressed, first in America and then in other parts of the world. After basketball became established in American colleges, the professional game followed. The American National Basketball Association (NBA), established in 1946, grew to a multibillion-dollar enterprise by the end of the century, and basketball became an integral part of American culture.


29. ano ang rules and regulations of basketball?


Answer:

Ito ang rules and regulations ng basketball:

Dalawang teams na may tig-5 manlalaro sa loob ng court.Nilalaro ito sa rectangular floor na court na may basket sa magkabilang dulo.Ang goal ay maka-shoot sa basket para makapuntos.Hindi puwedeng sipain ang bola.Hindi puwedeng galawin ang bola kapag pababa na ito sa basket o kapag nasa rim na. Violation yun na ang tawag ay "goaltending."Kailangang i-shoot ang bola sa loob ng 24 segundo. Kapag hawak pa rin ang bola sa loob ng 24 sec., tatawagan ng "24 shot lock violation."May 4 na quarter.Kung sino ang mas maraming puntos matapos ang 4 na quarter, sila ang mananalo.Kung pareho ng score matapos ang 4 na quarter, magkakaroon ng overtime na may 5 minutong oras ng paglalaro.Kung pareho pa rin ang puntos sa unang overtime, magkakaroon ng panibagong overtime hanggang sa may manalo.

Offensive Rules:

Team na may possession ng bola.

Kailangang i-dribble ang bola gamit ang isang kamay habang hinahakbang ang 2 paa. Kapag huminto na sa dribbling o 2 kamay na ang may hawak sa bola, kailangang isang paa lang ang ihakbang. "Pivot foot" ang tawag sa di gumagalaw na paa. Kapag di nagawa, tatawagan ng "travelling violation."Kailangang manatili ang bola sa loob ng court. Kapag lumabas ito sa linya, mawawala ang possession sa team at malilipat na sa kabilang team.Habang nagdri-dribble, kailangang nasa ibabaw ng bola ang kamay. Kapag napunta sa ilalim ang kamay habang nagdri-dribble, violation yun ang tawag ay "carrying the ball" at mawawala ang possession ng bola sa team. Kapag na in bound ang bola sa side ng kalaban, kailangang maitawid ang bola lagpas sa half court sa loob ng 8 segundo. Kapag lumagpas sa 8 sec., magkakaroon ng violation at malilipat ang possession sa kalaban.At kapag lagpas na sa half court, bawal nang bumalik sa kabilang side. Magkakaroon ng violation at ang tawag ay "backcourt violation." Pero kapag nahawakan ng defensive team ang bola kaya napunta sa backcourt, legal yun at pwede pa ring makuha ulit ng offensive team ang bola nang walang violation.

Defensive Rules:

Team na wala sa kanila ang possession ng bola.

Ang goal ng defensive team ay mapigilang maka-score ang offensive team.Kailangan huwag i-foul ang kalaban sa pamamagitan ng unnecessary physical contact

Tandaan:

Posible na iba-iba ang rules and regulations ng iba't ibang liga. Tulad na lang ng minuto bawat quarter. Yung iba 12 minuto bawat quarter, yung iba 10 minuto lang.

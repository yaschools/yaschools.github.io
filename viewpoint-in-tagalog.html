19. True or False6. A viewpoint is a person's way of thinking in general or particular things.7. A person may have different viewpoints on a certain issue.8. In presenting viewpoints, it is important to consider the viewpoints of others.9. Coherence is not important in presenting your viewpoint.10. You should not give others a chance to hear their viewpoint.​


Viewpoint

Answer:

6. TRUE

It is true that a viewpoint is a person's way of thinking about a certain topic. Everyone has a viewpoint over current issues. For example, I think that online class is difficult for some students. Many students still need guidance and would prefer a classroom set up than video call over messenger.

7. TRUE

It is also true to have different viewpoints on one issue. Although, it is necessary to align your viewpoints so that they would not be ironic or there would be coherence. For example, I agree that living in the province is good because there are fresh air. However, I also agree that it will be difficult to live in province due to lack of source of income than in city.

8. TRUE

Yes, we must consider the viewpoints of others since sometimes, they have a better viewpoint or perception than us. It would help us be more accepting and open minded about certain issues

9. FALSE

False, there must be coherent in order for other people to understand our viewpoint

10. FALSE

We must be open minded in accepting other viewpoints.

22. did you know animals are stress relievers?explain why animals are stress relievers.​


Answer:

Spending time with animals has been found to have stress-relieving effects on humans for several reasons. One is that animals provide companionship and social support, which can reduce feelings of loneliness and anxiety. Additionally, interacting with animals can increase the production of the hormone oxytocin, which can promote feelings of calm and well-being. Engaging in activities such as petting, grooming, or playing with animals can also be a form of mindfulness, allowing individuals to focus on the present moment and take a break from stress-inducing thoughts. Finally, caring for and being responsible for an animal can provide a sense of purpose and meaning, which can help individuals feel more fulfilled and less stressed.

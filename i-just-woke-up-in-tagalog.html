- Another solution could be to delegate the task to another person if possible. It's important to communicate clearly and express your needs and boundaries while also being considerate of the other person's needs.

- Alternatively, you could also ask if there are any other ways to help around the house, such as running errands, cooking dinner, or doing errands, that would allow you to rest and help out at the same time.

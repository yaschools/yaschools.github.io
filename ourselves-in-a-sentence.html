1. Intensive and reflexive sentences with the pronoun "ourselves"


Reflexive sentence:
We treated ourselves to pizza.

Intensive :
You yourself is responsible to this mess.

see the difference?

Reflexive refer back to the subject, while intensive, words to emphasize the subject.

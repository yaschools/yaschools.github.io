8. All are properties of covalent compounds EXCEPT.A. Soft and squishy.B. Conduct electricity in water.C. Formed between nonmetal elements.D. Have lower boiling and melting points.​


Answer:

B

Explanation:

Covalent compounds (solid, liquid, solution) do not conduct electricity. Metal elements and carbon (graphite) are conductors of electricity but non-metal elements are insulators of electricity.


9. Sign that seafood is bad if it has foul odor,has squishy or mushy texture when poked, has slimy,slippery film on the surface,has bluish or greyish color​


Answers

TRUE

Explanation

Signs that seafood is bad

It has a Foul Odor

Fresh fish should not have a strong ammonia-like smell. The term used for this smell in Filipino is Malansa.

Squishy or Mushy Texture When Poked

Fresh fish have firm meat. When poked, the meat should return to back to its original shape. Spoiled fish, when poked, leaves an imprint of the finger.

Slimy, Slippery Film on the Surface

Fish are naturally slimy to protect themselves from bacteria and other small microorganisms. Spoiled fish have thicker, viscous slime with milk-white flesh.

Bluish, Greyish color, or​ Translucent

Fresh fish have that wet and glisten or shiny looks.

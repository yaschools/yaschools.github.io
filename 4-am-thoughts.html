2. Assignment The Grade 4-Thoughtful took their lunch right after their class at 11:45 am. It' they finished eating, at 12:30 pm how long did they take their lunch​


Step-by-Step Explanation:

The Grade 4-Thoughtful had lunch right after their class at 11:45 am.

They finished eating at 12:30 pm.

To determine how long they took their lunch, you need to subtract the start time (11:45 am) from the end time (12:30 pm).

To do this, you can convert both times to a 24-hour clock, where 11:45 am is equivalent to 11:45 and 12:30 pm is equivalent to 12:30.

Next, subtract the start time from the end time. In this case, 12:30 - 11:45 = 0:45 or 45 minutes.

Therefore, the Grade 4-Thoughtful took 45 minutes to finish their lunch.

then, divide by 7

equals to 2

[2(x + 3) - 4]/7 = 2

compute for x

[2(x + 3) - 4]/7 = 2

2(x + 3) - 4 = 14

2[(x + 3) - 2] = 14

(x + 3) - 2 = 7

x + 3 = 9

x = 6

therefore, the number is 6

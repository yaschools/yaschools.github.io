19. Problem Solving: Directions: Solve the given problem completely and correctly following the four steps of Polya’s method of solving problem. (5 points) Chona and Len are sisters. Len is 36 years less than twice the age of Chona. In 6 years, Len’s age will be 12 years more than half of Chona’s age. How old is each girl?​


Age Word Problem

Analyzation

Let x = Chona's age

Len is 36 years less than twice the age of Chona.

Len's age now = 2x - 36

In 6 years

Chona's age = x + 6

Len's age = 12 + (x + 6) / 2

Then we can say that

In 6 years:

Len's age = (2x - 36) + 6 = 12 + (x + 6) / 2

Solution:

Solve for "x"

(2x - 36) + 6 = 12 + (x + 6) / 2 >>> multiply by 2

4x - 60 = 24 + (x + 6)

4x -x = 24 + 6 + 60

3x = 90

x = 30, Chona's age ✅

2x - 36, Len's age

2(30) - 36 = 60 - 36 = 24

24, Len's age ✅

Answer:

Len is 24 years old and Chona is 30 years old.

25. How much force was applied on the basket of fish which was delivered from the Bagsakan Centre tothe market fish stall with a distance of 20 meters after 100 joules of work was done?​pa ans po salamat


Answer:

50 N

Explanation:

Work = Force × Distance

Derived the formula

W

[tex] \frac{work}{distace} = \frac{force \: \times \: distance}{distance} [/tex]

Cancel distance distance so the formula should be:

[tex] \frac{work}{distance} = \: \: force [/tex]

So then,

[tex] \frac{100}{20} = force[/tex]

And so 50 N is the force.

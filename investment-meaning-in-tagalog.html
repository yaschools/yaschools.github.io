17. In your own words, what is the meaning of Investment risks, Investment return and gains, risk return trade off.


Answer:

When we say "Investment Risks" it means that this is a danger from a result or investment will after what we expected. So, investment risks are a kinda measure of the extent of uncertainty in obtaining their investor's expected returns. It refers to the extent to which unexpected outcomes can be realized.

When we came up with the idea of "Investment Returns or Gains", this is the ratio of a profit or loss earned in a budget year. It shows the percentage of the increase or decrease in the value of the investment during the year and the owner expected profits that are received from the investment of the business.

"Risk-Return trade-off" implies that as the risk level grows, the possible reward climbs as well. In the risk-return trade-off, an investor's money can only generate larger rewards if the investor is willing to endure a higher risk or low.

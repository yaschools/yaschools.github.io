2. 1. What is Stress 2 Give 10 examples of Stress 3. Explain each number on how you can manage istress your 4. Is stress all bad? Why? 5. What Strasses you out?​


1. Stress is a physical, mental, or emotional response to a demand, threat, or challenge that exceeds a person's ability to cope with it. It is a natural reaction that prepares the body to respond to danger or pressure, also known as the "fight or flight" response.

2. Examples of stress include:

Financial problems

Work deadlines

Family conflicts

Relationship issues

Health problems

Traffic jams

Academic pressure

Social anxiety

Performance anxiety

Life transitions (moving, divorce, etc.)

3. Here are some ways to manage stress:

Practice relaxation techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga

Exercise regularly to release tension and boost endorphins

Get enough sleep and establish a regular sleep routine

Eat a healthy diet and avoid excessive caffeine, alcohol, or drugs

Set realistic goals and prioritize tasks to avoid feeling overwhelmed

Seek social support from friends, family, or a therapist

Practice time management and avoid procrastination

Engage in hobbies or activities that bring joy and relaxation

Learn to say "no" to commitments that cause unnecessary stress

Maintain a positive attitude and practice gratitude and self-compassion

4. Stress is not all bad. It can motivate us to take action, achieve goals, and adapt to new situations. However, chronic or excessive stress can have negative effects on our physical and mental health, including increased risk of heart disease, anxiety, depression, and other illnesses.

12. ano ang ibigsabihin ng stress o tagalog ng stress


ANO ANG STRESS?

Ang stress sitwasyon na kung saan nararamdaman mo na ikaw ay nahihirapan o nababahala. Ang stress ay natural na nangyayari sa isang tao, lahat naman tayo ay nakaranas na nito. Pero tandaan, hindi palaging nakakasama ang stress sa isang tao.

Mayroong dalawang uri ng stress. Iyon ay ang Healthy Stress at Unhealthy Stress. Ating talakayin ang dalawang iyon.

Healthy Stress

Ang Healthy Stress ay ang uri ng stress na pangsandalian lang at ito'y nakakabuti pa sa iyo. Halimbawa, na-stress ka kasi exam niyo na. Ang iyong stress ay mag uudyok sa iyo na mag aral ng maigi para hindi ka bumagsak. Sa panandaliang salita, na motivate ka ng iyong stress na mag aral.

Unhealthy Stress

Ang Unhealthy Stress naman ay ang uri ng stress na nakakasama na sa iyo. Ang stress na ito ay pangmatagalan na at ito'y nakakastorbo na sa iyong pang araw-araw na buhay. Malalaman mong ganito na ang iyong stress kapag nawawalan ka nang gana sa lahat pati sa mga bagay na hilig mo at ika'y palagi nang problemado.

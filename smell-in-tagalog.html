18. That soup smells delicious. smells here is a linking verb or action verb? and why?


Linking Verb

Question:

That soup smells delicious. "Smells" here is a linking verb or action verb? and why?

Answer:

“Smells” is a linking verb — because the sentence describes the soup. It connects the subject (soup), with information about that subject (it smells delicious).

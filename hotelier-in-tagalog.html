11. After watching, what can you say about the investigation? As a guest, how do you feel now about staying in hotels? As a future hotelier, what can you do to change the stigma brought about by the investigation to the hotel guests?​


Staying in hotels, it is important to be aware of the safety measures and security protocols in place at the hotel you are planning to stay in. It is also important to read reviews from previous guests to gain an understanding of their experiences at the hotel.

As for changing the stigma brought about by investigations, hotels can take several steps to improve guest safety and security. This can include installing security cameras in public areas, providing safety tips to guests during check-in, and conducting thorough background checks on employees. Additionally, hotels can provide training for their staff to recognize and respond appropriately to any safety concerns or suspicious behavior.

Ultimately, providing a safe and secure environment for guests should be a top priority for all hotels, and implementing measures to ensure this can help to restore trust and confidence among guests.

29. why is it important for a hotelier to understand the different management principles before starting up a hotel explain​


Answer:

to not mess up something you're managing and to know what to do in tough situations

Explanation:

my explanation is in my answer

Answer:

It is essential for a hotelier to understand the different management principles before starting up a hotel because it helps them establish a strong foundation for their business. Effective management principles are critical in achieving organizational goals, ensuring operational efficiency, and providing exceptional customer service. Here are some reasons why understanding management principles is important for hoteliers:

Helps to establish a clear vision: A hotelier needs to have a clear vision of what they want to achieve with their hotel. Understanding management principles can help them define their vision, mission, and values. They can then use this information to create a business plan that outlines their objectives, strategies, and tactics to achieve their goals.

Improves operational efficiency: Management principles help hoteliers to identify the most effective and efficient ways of running their business. They can use these principles to streamline their operations, reduce costs, and improve productivity. This can help them to deliver high-quality services to their guests, which can lead to repeat business and positive reviews.

Ensures exceptional customer service: One of the most critical management principles is customer service. Hoteliers need to understand how to provide exceptional customer service to their guests. They can use various management principles such as employee training, quality management, and continuous improvement to ensure that their staff is providing top-notch services to their guests.

Helps to manage resources: Hoteliers need to manage their resources effectively to ensure that they are using them efficiently. Management principles such as budgeting, financial management, and resource allocation can help hoteliers to manage their resources effectively. This can help them to avoid wastage and maximize their returns.

Ensures compliance with regulations: There are several regulations that hoteliers need to comply with to run their business successfully. Understanding management principles such as legal compliance, risk management, and ethics can help hoteliers to comply with these regulations.

In conclusion, understanding management principles is essential for hoteliers as it helps them establish a strong foundation for their business. It enables them to establish a clear vision, improve operational efficiency, ensure exceptional customer service, manage resources effectively, and comply with regulations.

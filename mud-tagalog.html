Answer:

A colloid is a substance microscopically dispersed evenly throughout another substance. ... The glass is a colloid of solid particles inside the solid glass. Mud is usually considered a suspension because the dirt will settle, but it may be a partial colloid, particularly with very fine-grained clay.

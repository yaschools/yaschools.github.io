To find the length and width of the canvas, we need to factor the expression x²-4x-12 to get the dimensions of the canvas.

Factoring this expression, we get (x-6)(x+2). This tells us that the dimensions of the canvas are x-6 inches by x+2 inches.

a. The length of the canvas is x-6 inches.

29. it takes 25 days for an artist to draw 3 canvas. How long will it take for an artist to finish 6 canvas at the same rate? ​


Answer:

50 days po or 1 month and 20 days

Question:

x varies directly as y, and x = 25 when y = 3, solve for x when y = 6

Solution:

Plugging in our initial statement values of x = 25 when y = 3, we get:

[tex]25=3k[/tex]

Divide each side by 3 to solve for k:

[tex]\frac{25}{3} =\frac{3k}{3}[/tex]

Cancel out same terms

[tex]\frac{25}{3} =k[/tex]

[tex]\frac{25}{3} = 8.3333333333333[/tex]

Solve the second part of the variation equation:

Because we have found our relationship constant k = 8.3333333333333, we form our new variation equation:

[tex]x = 8.3333333333333y[/tex]

Since we were given that y = 6, we have

[tex]x = 8.3333333333333(6)[/tex]

x=50

25. What is Luster and examples?


Answer:

Lustre, in mineralogy, the appearance of a mineral surface in terms of its light-reflective qualities. Luster has two main categories: Metallic and Non-metallic. An example of lustre is the glow of sterling silver.

Luster examples

Dull lustre. Kaolinite.

Greasy lustre. Moss opal.

Metallic lustre. Pyrite.

Pearly lustre. Muscovite.

13. 1/5 of the fishes in the aquarium are goldfish 3/4 of the fishes are swordfish and the rest are guppies there are 20 guppies in the aquarium. How many fishes are there in all in the aquarium


Answer:

All in all, there are 400 fishes in the aquarium.

Step-by-step explanation:

1/5 + 3/4 = 4/20 + 15/20 = 19/20

1 - 19/20 = 1/20 (guppies)

20(4) = 80

20(15) = 300

80 + 300 + 20 = 400

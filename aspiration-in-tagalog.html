5. what is aspirational clutter?


Dear Enriquezmodesta,

Aspirational clutter is when you buy things that you would want to use or make, but you become uninterested, too busy, etc. to do and/or use the things you bought. Also, you have a hard time letting go of the things you bought (so it becomes clutter) because you still have the thought that you might be able to use those things in the future.

16. meaning of suction aspiration​


Answer:

1.)a method of abortion performed in the later half of the first trimester of pregnancy by aspiration of the contents of the uterus through a narrow tube

2.) A surgical procedure in which the cervix is dilated (opened) and vacuum is used to remove tissue from the uterus. Also called suction evacuation and vacuum aspiration

Vacuum or suction aspiration is a procedure that uses a vacuum source to remove an embryo or fetus through the cervix. The procedure is performed to induce abortion, as a treatment for incomplete miscarriage or retained pregnancy tissue, or to obtain a sample of uterine lining (endometrial biopsy).

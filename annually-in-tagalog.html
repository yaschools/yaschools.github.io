1. Ano ang annual motion at precession of equinox. Tagalog po.


Answer:

Ang annual motion ay tumutukoy sa paraan ng paggalaw ng araw sa paligid ng buwan sa loob ng isang taon. Sa pangkalahatan, ang araw ay nagpapakilos sa isang orbit sa paligid ng buwan sa loob ng 365.24 na araw, na nagreresulta sa mga seasons sa lupa. Sa kabilang banda, ang precession ng equinox ay tumutukoy sa paraan ng paggalaw ng mga puntos ng equinox at solstice sa paligid ng ekliptika sa loob ng isang cycle na tumatagal ng mahigit sa 26,000 na taon. Ito ay dulot ng gravitational pull ng ibang mga planeta sa solar system sa buwan at sa lupa. Sa pangkalahatan, ang precession ng equinox ay nagbibigay ng mga subtle shifts sa mga seasons sa lupa at sa mga astrological signs sa Zodiac.

4. Payments of $17,000 made annually for 10 years at 10% compounded annually


Answer:

FV = $44,093.62

I = $27,093.62

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Principal (P) = $17,000

Rate (r) = 10% or 0.1

Time (t) = 10 years

Conversion Period (m) = 1

We will solve for the Future Value (FV):

 [tex]FV = P(1+\frac{r}{m})^{tm}[/tex]

Substitute the values:

[tex]FV = 17,000 (1+\frac{0.1}{1})^{10(1)}[/tex]

Answer:

[tex]FV=44,093.62[/tex]

To find the interest we will use the formula:

[tex]I = FV-P[/tex]

Substitute:

[tex]I = 44,093.62 - 17,000[/tex]

Subtract:

[tex]I = 27,093.62[/tex]

Final Answers:

FV = $44,093.62

I = $27,093.62

29. Problem: A salary loan amounting to ₱15,000 is to be paid annually for 4 years with an interest rate of 6% compounded annually. The annual amortization is ₱4,328. 87.


Answer:

To determine the total amount of interest paid over the 4-year period of the loan, you can use the following formula:

total interest = loan amount x interest rate x number of years

Plugging in the values given in the problem, we get:

total interest = 15,000 x 0.06 x 4

total interest = ₱3,600

To determine the total amount paid over the 4-year period, you can add the total interest to the loan amount:

total amount paid = loan amount + total interest

Plugging in the values, we get:

total amount paid = 15,000 + 3,600

total amount paid = ₱18,600

To determine the total amortization paid over the 4-year period, you can multiply the annual amortization by the number of years:

total amortization = annual amortization x number of years

Plugging in the values, we get:

total amortization = 4,328.87 x 4

total amortization = ₱17,315.48

Therefore, the total amount of interest paid over the 4-year period is ₱3,600 and the total amount paid is ₱18,600. The total amortization paid is ₱17,315.48.

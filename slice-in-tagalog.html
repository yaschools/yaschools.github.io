Step-by-step explanation:

Let...

x be the slices of carrot cakey be the slices of walnut cake

Gotten equation:

y = x - $0.50

Solution(getting the cost of carrot cake:

[tex]7x + 9y = 35.50 \\ 7x + 9(x - 2) = 35.50 \\ 7x + 9x - 18 = 35.50\\ 16x = 35.50 + 18 \\ 16x = 53.5 \\ \frac{16x}{16} = \frac{53.5}{16} \\ x = 3.34[/tex]

Finding the cost of carrot cake:

y = x - $0.50

y = 3.34 -$0.50

y = $2.84

Word Problem

The problem is asking for the total number of whole pi zzas that the vendor sliced. There are two steps to find the answer.

Find the total number of slices of pi zza by adding the number of slices sold and the number of slices left.Find the total number of whole pi zzas by dividing the total number of slices made into one whole pi zza.

Given:

number of slices sold - 57

number of slices left - 7

number of slices into one whole pi zza - 8

Solution:

Step 1: Add.

57 + 7 = 64

There were 64 slices of pi zza.

Step 2: Divide.

64 ÷ 8 = 8

Final Answer:

The vendor sliced 8 whole pi zzas.

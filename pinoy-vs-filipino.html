Answer:

Based on the song, Tayo’y Mga Pinoy, the unique qualities of Filipino are, 1.) proud, not ashamed of its physical appearances (flat nose as being mentioned in the song), 2.) brave, (to speak the origin of Filipinos are coming from the east), 3.) value, (showing the importance of the motherland and connecting or staying to the roots of being a Filipino).

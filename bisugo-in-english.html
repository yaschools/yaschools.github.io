Problem :

A fish vendor in Puerto Princesa City Old Public Market sells different kinds of fish. She has 5 kilos of bangus, 5 kilos of galunggong, 7 kilos of bararawan, and 4 kilos of bisugo. In how many ways can you buy 1 kilo of each kind of fish?

Given :

➪ She has 5 kilos of bangus, 5 kilos of galunggong, 7 kilos of bararawan, and 4 kilos of bisugo

What to Find :

➪ In how many ways can you buy 1 kilo of each kind of fish

Solution :

➪ 5 × 5 × 7 × 4 = 700

Conclusion :

➪ 700 ways you can buy 1 kilo of each kind of fish.

26. 5. Jazvienne's mom is buying hotdogs and hotdog buns for the family barbecue. Hotdogs come in packs of 12 and hotdog buns come in packs of 9. The store does not sell parts of a pack and Jazvienne's mom wants the same number of hotdogs as hotdog buns. What is the smallest total number of hotdogs that Jazvienne's mom can purchase? A. 36 hotdogs B. 21 hotdogs C. 18 hotdogs D. 3 hotdogs​


Answer:

A. 36 hotdogs

Step-by-step explanation:

Because there are 12 hotdogs in a pack and 9 buns in a pack. So in order to have the same number of hotdogs and buns, you must buy 3 packs of hotdogs (12x3=36) and 4 packs of buns (9x4=36). 36 is the smallest divisible number of both 9 and 12.

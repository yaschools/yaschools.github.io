10. jun receives a 5% commission on all sales. What is his commission if his total sales is ₱40,000?PLZ assignment ko po ito pa-pasahin namit ito bukas.. ​


If Jun receives a 5% commission on all sales and his total sales is ₱40,000, then we can calculate his commission as follows:

Commission = 5% of Total Sales

To find 5% of ₱40,000, we can multiply the total sales by 0.05:

Commission = 0.05 x ₱40,000

Commission = ₱2,000

Therefore, Jun's commission on his total sales of ₱40,000 is ₱2,000.

6. Choose the concise sentence below. Select one: a. We have completely eliminated the bugs from this program. b. We have eliminated the bugs. c. We have eliminated the bugs from this program. d. Totally, the bugs are eliminated.


The answer or the concise sentence from the choices given is C: We have eliminated the bugs from this program. Although the some choices have fewer words than option C, they did not give the precise information. A concise sentence should consist of few words yet accurate information. In Choice C, the bugs were described as coming from a program which is a crucial information.

Here's another example of a concise sentence vs wordy sentences: https://brainly.ph/question/1679223

VS. Choice AAlthough Choice A has the same thought or message as Choice C, the word "completely" is not necessary in the sentence. The information remains the same without the word "completely," making Choice C the more concise sentence.

VS. Choice BIn Choice B, it was not said whether actual bugs were eliminated. Although Choice B has fewer words than Choice C, the information is not accurate.

VS. Choice DSame as Choice B, Choice D did not mention on what kind of bugs were eliminated. The word "totally" is also unnecessary in the message the sentence is trying to convey.

Avoid wordiness or unnecessary words Be straightforward or direct to the pointAvoid qualifiersAvoid redundancy

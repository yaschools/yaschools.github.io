2. NaHCO3 meaning? example diba yung HCI ang meaning nun ay Hydrogen Chlorine ngayon ano yung meaning nga NaHCO3???​


Answer:

Sodium Bicarbonate

Explanation:

Na (1) - Sodium

H (1) - Hydrogen

C (1) - Carbon

O (3) - Oxygen

NaHCO3 is the chemical formula for Sodium Bicarbonate. It contains 1 molecule of sodium, 1 molecule of hydrogen, 1 molecule of carbon and 3 molecules of oxygen. This is more commonly known as Baking Soda.

15. Explain why the parts of the body of the woman submerged in water appear fatter and shorter.


Answer:

As you sight at the portion of the body( women's body) that was submerged in the water, light ray changes medium and subsequently undergoes refraction. As a result, the image of the women's body appears to be fatter and shorter. Furthermore, the portion of the woman's body that is submerged in water appears to be wider that is not submerged. These visual distortion are explained by the refraction of light.

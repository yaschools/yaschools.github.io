Directions: Read and analyze the tale entitled "Hudhud Hi Aliguyo" (The tale of Aliguyon).Answer the process questions.

1. Where did the story happen?

The story happened in the mountainous regions of Northen Luzon and the village of Hannanga and Daligdigan.

2. Who were the characters?

Amtalao and Dumulao – the parents of AliguyonAliguyon – an intelligent and brave warrior from HannangaPangaiwan – the enemy of Aliguyon's fatherPumbakhayon – also a brave warrior and the enemy of Aliguyon that later on became his good friend.Bugan – Pumbakhayon's younger sister and the mate of AliguyonAginaya – Aliguyon's younger sister and the wife of Pumbakhayon

3. What is the conflict of the story?

The conflict of the story was when Aliguyon and Pumbakhayon fought for three years. This kind of conflict is man vs. man.

4. Why did the battle between Aliguyon and Pumbakhayon last for three years?

Their battle lasted for three years because they are both equally skilled in the arts of war and magic; no one was better than the other.

5. What kind of warriors were Aliguyon and Pumbakhayon? (characterize)

Both Aliguyon and Pumbakhayon were brave warriors. They were also intelligent and very skilled.

6. If you were Aliguyon? Would you do the same? Why?

If I were Aliguyon, yes, I would do the same. I will just end the battle between my enemy who is just as good as me because we would be wasting time if we continue to fight since no one will win. It is better to be in good terms with other people than be in conflict with them.

7. What particular values of the Filipino being showcase in the tale?

Bravery – the bravery of Filipinos was reflected in the character of Aliguyon and Pumbakhayon. They both showed bravery in battling with each other.Sportsmanship – Aliguyon and Pumbakhayon had enough sportsmanship to be able to accept that no one will win in their battle so they both stopped and became friends instead

8. How did the story end?

The story ended with Aliguyon and Pumbakhayon becoming good friends. Peace in their villages flourished and they became wealthy and respected in all of Ifugao.

9. What is the moral of the story?

The moral of the story is that, pride and aggressiveness won't do anything good. If Aliguyon and Pumbakhayon had high pride and continued to be aggressive, their battle with continue endlessly. It is also important to be in good terms with other people so that we and the people around us will live in peace.

10. What is your realization after reading the tale?

I have realized that it is good to be brave but bravery should be used correctly. Backing up in a battle does not mean we are weak, it means we are strong enough to know how to choose our battles and think of the greater good for everyone.

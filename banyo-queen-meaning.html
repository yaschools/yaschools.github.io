19. Ang banyo ay may habang 8m at lapad na 4m. Anong area ng banyo


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Upang makuha ang area ng parisukat (square) at parihaba (rectangle), kailangang i-multiply ang haba (length) at ang lapad (width).

Ang formula ay Length x Width = Area

Ngayon, i-substitute natin ang given ayon sa formula sa itaas.

L = 8 m

W = 4 m

8 m x  4 m =32 m²

Kung iyong papansinin, hindi natin tinanggal ang unit of measurement o ang m (meter) at mayroon tayong nilagay na  squared sign (²).  Sa tuwing area ang hinahanap, parating dapat nakalagay ay unit of measurement ng given at ng squared sign.

Mathematics: Study of Patterns.

Math is the Queen of Sciences 1. What do you mean by "Mathematics is a study of patterns"?

Mathematics is often considered the study of patterns because it involves the identification and analysis of regularities and structures that can be observed in various phenomena. These patterns can be found in natural phenomena, social structures, and even in abstract concepts.

In mathematics, patterns are identified by recognizing similarities in sets of numbers, shapes, or other objects, and by observing how they relate to one another. By studying these patterns, mathematicians can develop theories, models, and formulas that help explain and predict the behavior of complex systems.

The study of patterns is an essential part of mathematics, as it helps mathematicians identify new relationships and develop new techniques for solving problems. In this way, mathematics provides a powerful tool for understanding the world around us and for making sense of the patterns that we observe in it.

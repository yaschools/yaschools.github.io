20. Mr. Z is not minimum wage earner with the following data in a year salaries 200,000,Benefits 20,000,Mandatory deduction (SSS,etc),Income Tax withheld onbthe compensation income 2,000 Required:Income tax still due or refundable end of the year?​


Answer:

The 2,000 tax withheld (deducted from salaries) is refundable at the end of the year.

Step-by-step explanation:

Annual basis:

Salaries: 200,000

Mandatory deductions of benefits (SSS, Philhealth, Pag-Ibig): 20,000

Solve for taxable income:

Taxable income = Gross salaries - mandatory contributions

Taxable income = 200,000 - 20,000

Taxable income = 180,000

Under the 2018 Philippine Tax schedule, for taxable income 250,000 and below, there is no tax due or exempted from paying income taxes.

The taxable income of Mr Z is 180,000.  

180,000 < 250,000

Therefore:

The 2,000 pesos tax withheld (deducted from salaries) is refundable at the end of the year.

The tax schedule is as follows:

Net income 250,000 and below

Tax due:  0%

Net income over 250,000 to 400,000

Tax due: 20% in excess of 250,000

Net income over 400,000 to 800,000

Tax due: 20,000 + 25% in excess of 400,000

Net income over 800,000 to 2,000,000

Tax Due: 130,000 + 30% in excess of 800,000

Net income over 2,000,000 to 8,000,000

Tax Due: 490,000 + 32% in excess of 2,000,000

Net income over 8,000,000

Tax Due: 2,410,000 + 35% in excess of 8,000.000

29. Mr. Pol is a resident citizen with an income of Php50,000 per month. He has no dependents and is still single and available. His total SSS, Paag-IBIG, PhilHealth and other contributions is Php1,000. What is the income tax withheld?


Answer:

Tax withheld = Php 6,416.75

Step-by-step explanation:

Monthly income: Php 50,000

Contributions, monthly:  Php 1,000

Compute the net taxable income, monthly:

Net taxable income = 50,000 - 1,000

Net taxable income = 49,000

For monthly taxable income for bracket  Php 33,333 to Php 66,000, compute the monthly withholding tax as follows:

Tax Withheld = 2,500 + 25% (x - 33,333)

Where:

x = taxable income above 33,000

x = Php 49,000

Compute the income tax withheld for the month:

Tax withheld = 2,500 + 0.25 (49,000 - 33,333)

Tax withheld = 2,500 + 0.25 (15,667)

Tax withheld = 2,500 + 3,916.75

Tax withheld = Php 6,416.75

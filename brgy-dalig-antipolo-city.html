23. why Antipolo became the most populous city/ municipality in Rizal?


Answer:

On June 19, 2020, President Rodrigo Duterte signed Republic Act 11475, designating Antipolo City as the official capital of Rizal Province. It took effect on July 7, 2020. The city is popular for being a pilgrimage site. It prides itself as the "Pilgrimage Capital of the Philippines".

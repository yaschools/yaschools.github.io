29. instrument classification of shank​


Answer:

The shanks of an instrument are classified as simple (straight) or complex (angled). In general, simple shanks are designed for use in areas of easy access like the anterior teeth. The more complex or angled shanks are designed for areas that are more difficult to access, such as the posterior teeth.

11. Speechless by kyra lee what is the poem all about


Speechless by Kyra Lee

The poem was about two sisters who suffer constant verbal and physical abuse from their father.

The poem was about:

Physical Abuse Verbal Abuse Mental Trauma Violence  Addiction (Drinking)

The poem Speechless by Kyra Lee, was from a perspective of child an elder sister in fact. It was said in the poem that their mother can’t do anything while his father’s home. She shuts quite and they run to their rooms and doesn’t make any sound.

Their father was constantly mad at them for somehow no reason, he drinks and physically abuses them whenever they were in his vision. The two sisters truly shows fear towards their father which is the man of the house.

But one time her sister failed to keep quiet and yelled at their father, expectedly their father got mad and started beating her up. It seems that their father no longer was in the right mind. He took a short break and just watches her till the last of her breath.

The elder sister Sasha got noticed later on by her father, he then yelled at her blaming that it was her fault that-that thing happen. The title Speechless was from the last part of the poem where Sasha, the older sister who watched her sister takes one last breath refuses to speak anymore. For she blames herself for speaking towards her father and angering him to the point she lost her sister.

The characters of the poem Speechless:

Leigh- the younger daughter; sister of Sasha Sasha- the eldest sister Mother- oppressed mother who just keeps quiet when her husband’s home Father- the one who inflicted mental and physical trauma to his own family

16. could this really have happened why speechless


With all the emerging child abuse, it is possible that the story tackled in Speechless by Kyra Lee happened in real life.  Furthermore, the effect of the abuse due to what Sasha witnessed, which is the death of her younger sister, Leigh, is also possible

Too much stress caused by a traumatic event may lead to a person’s inability to speak.  This is a possible response when the trauma is unbearable for the person involved.

According to Jamie Eske, “Stressful situations can trigger anxiety, resulting in more pronounced speech disorder symptoms".

As for Sasha, she grew up in a hostile and toxic familial relationship.  Her mother was always away from home, and did not provide affection and protection for her children.  While Sasha’s father was the sole source of their danger which caused her sister’s death.

18. what is the summary of the poem speechless by kyra lee


Speechless portrays the life of Sasha, aged 6, and her younger sister Leigh, aged 3.

Sasha’s father is abusive.  He has a terrible temperament who would always yell and was always mad.  He was always drunk and alone.  On the other hand, her mother was not always at home with them.  If she was ever at home, she would remain quiet.

One day, when Sasha’s father came home, Sasha and Leigh ran off quickly and hid from their father.  They held hands so tightly, and was trying not to make a sound.  But Leigh could not handle it anymore.  Leigh screamed at her father, and as expected, his father became furious of her.  Her father took Leigh and beat her up until Leigh lost her consciousness, and died.

Sasha never spoke since that day because she thought that it was speaking that killed her sister.

27. what is the affix used in the words speechless and hopeless​


Answer:

SPEECHLESS= the suffix –LESS expresses the same idea as the preposition "without", so "speechless" means literally "without speech", that is, "without words". When you are speechless you can't speak because you're so impressed by something that you don't know what to say

HOPELESS= The suffix "less" in "hopeless" gives the meaning "without." derivations: suffixal (adj.), suffixion (n.)

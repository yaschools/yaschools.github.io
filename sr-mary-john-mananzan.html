30. E. PQRS is a parallelogram. If QP = 3x and SR = 2x + 5, find QP and SR.​


Answer:

5

Step-by-step explanation:

Formula: QP = SR

[tex]3x = 2x + 5[/tex]

[tex]3x - 2x = 5[/tex]

[tex]x = 5[/tex]

Therefore, the value of QP and SR is 5.

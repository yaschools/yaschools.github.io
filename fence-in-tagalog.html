Side: 35 m

[tex] \rm Area = side \times side[/tex]

[tex] \rm A = (35 \: m)(35 \: m)[/tex]

[tex] \underline {\boxed {\rm{A = 1225 \: {m}^{2} }}}[/tex]

The area of a square is expressed by square unit.

21. In a crate of papayas, 12% of the papayas are rotten and 66 are in good condition. Find the total number of papayas in the basket.step by step​


The total number of papayas in the basket is 65.

EXPLANATION:

Given:

12% = rotten papayas

66 = __% = good papayas

Solution:

[tex]100\% - 12\% = 88\% \: or \: 0.88[/tex]

[tex]66 \div 0.88 = 75[/tex]

Checking:

[tex]75 \times 0.88 = 66[/tex]

[tex]75 \times 0.12 = 9[/tex]

[tex]66 + 9 = 75[/tex]

So the total number of papayas is 65.

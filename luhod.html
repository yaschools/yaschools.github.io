1. kahulugan ng "nagbabalik-luhod"​


Answer:

"Nagbabalik-luhod" is a phrase in Tagalog that means "to kneel down in repentance or submission." It can be used to describe someone who is expressing remorse or apology for their actions, or someone who is acknowledging their inferior position or subservience to another person or authority. The phrase can also be used more broadly to describe any act of humbling oneself or showing deference to others.

29. What can you say about the quote "The end justify the means" in tagalog.


Step-by-step explanation:

The quote "The end justify the means" means that the goals or outcomes of an action or situation justify the methods or means used to achieve them. In Tagalog, this quote can be translated as "Ang hangganan ay nagtutustos sa mga paraan." It is a controversial idea because it suggests that it is acceptable to use any means necessary to achieve a desired goal, even if those means are unethical or immoral. Some people believe that the end justifies the means, while others disagree and argue that the means should always be ethical and moral, regardless of the end result.

29. difine the following meaning trendsettter futurist trendspotting cool hunting trend analysis


Answer:

Trendsetter refers to a person or entity that sets a new trend or style. Futurist refers to someone who studies and predicts future trends and developments.

Trendspotting is the act of identifying emerging trends and changes in consumer behavior. Cool hunting refers to the search for new and innovative trends and ideas that are considered desirable or attractive.

Trend analysis involves studying past and present data to identify patterns and forecast future trends in a particular industry or market.

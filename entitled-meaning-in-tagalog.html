21. What is the meaning of a song entitled o caca, o caca?


It is actually a song created by two couple exchanging their views and thoughts about each other. In Kapampangan, the word 'caca' means a polite way to call a brother or a sister of your age or older.

In the 1st stanza, the man thought that for him the beauty of a woman depends on how she can fight for herself and not with how smooth her skin is. He said he would go to a mountain to search instead.

In the 2nd stanza, the woman teased the man and called him Bulatiktik. The word means small and thin. She said she doesn't like a dark brown skin of the man.

In the 3rd stanza, the man advised the woman to get a shot because she will get hurt in the future that she can have a son someday that may adore her yet would live far away from her.

In the 4th stanza, the woman said the opposite description of the man that she thought unacceptable nowadays.

In the 5th stanza, the man said that the woman is attractive but she couldn't hide how bad words came out from her mouth. For him, her lips are still black.

In the last stanza, the woman said that the man said his thougths without choosing his words perfectly so she believes that she could just get a pair of scissors to cut the story.

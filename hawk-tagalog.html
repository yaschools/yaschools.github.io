1. paano laruin ang hawk and chicken (tagalog)​


Answer:

Ang isang manlalaro ay napili bilang lawin at isa pa bilang inahin. Ang natitirang mga manlalaro ay manok. Ang mga manok ay nakaayos sa likod ng isa't isa, may hawak na mga baywang ng bawat isa. Pinamumunuan ng hen ang mga manok at paikot-ikot sa paghahanap ng pagkain.

Nakilala sila ng lawin at sinubukang bumili ng sisiw mula sa hen. Tinanong ng lawin ang inahin, "Papayagan mo ba akong bumili ng isa sa iyong mga manok?" Ang hen ay tumugon, "Oo, pumili ng sinumang gusto mo." Umupo ang lawin at pumili ng mataba. Sa wakas, pagkatapos ng bargaining para sa presyo, binabayaran niya ang hen at dinala ang manok. Pagkatapos ay hinubad niya ang manok at hiniling sa kanya na maghanap ng mga butil ng bigas para sa kanyang hapunan. Kapag nakatulog ang lawin, nakatakas ang manok at bumalik sa hen. Nang matuklasan ng lawin na nakatakas ang manok, hinanap niya ito at hinabol. Pinipigilan ng inahin at ang iba pang mga manok ang lawin sa paghuli ng manok.

Kung ang lawin ay nagtagumpay sa paghuli ng manok, kinuha niya ito muli at parusahan sa pamamagitan ng paghukay sa paligid ng mga butil ng bigas; kung siya ay nabigo, ang laro ay nasimulan muli, at sinubukan niyang bumili ng isa pang manok.

7. Which food chain is in the correct order? grasshopper → grass → snake → frog → hawk grasshopper → frog → hawk → snake → grass grass → grasshopper → frog → snake → hawk grass → grasshopper → snake → frog → hawk


The food chain that is in the correct order is: grass → grasshopper → frog → snake → hawk

Remember that no food chain can start from a consumer since there will be no source of energy for the primary consumers. In this case, the producer is the grass, it produces its own food via photosynthesis; the grasshopper is the primary consumer, it consumes the producer; the secondary consumer is the frog which in turn eats the primary consumer; and finally the tertiary consumer is the hawk, which eats the secondary consumer.

Do note though that energy received per level in the food chain and energy pyramid is decreasing as you ascend. This is because some of the energy is lost by the organisms to the environment as heat while some is used by the organism.

19. what is hawking raddiation?​


Hawking radiation is black-body radiation that is predicted to be released by black holes, due to quantum effects near the black hole event horizon. It is named after the physicist Stephen Hawking, who provided a theoretical argument for its existence in 1974.The requirement that black holes lose energy into the wider universe, and therefore can "evaporate", and the radiated spectrum are both a result of analysing black hole thermal equilibrium combined with extreme redshifting effects very close to the event horizon, with some consideration of quantum entanglement effects.A pair of virtual waves/particles arises just beyond the event horizon due to ordinary quantum effects.

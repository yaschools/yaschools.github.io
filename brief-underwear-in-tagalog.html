25. Is it advisable to borrow underwear to a friend? Why or why not?​


Answer:

It is generally not advisable to borrow underwear to a friend because underwear is considered to be a personal item that is worn close to the body and can contain bacteria, sweat, and other bodily fluids. Sharing underwear can also be considered unhygienic and may lead to the spread of infections or other illnesses. Additionally, people may have different hygiene practices and preferences, which can make it uncomfortable to wear someone else's underwear. It is generally better to buy your own underwear and to avoid sharing it with others.

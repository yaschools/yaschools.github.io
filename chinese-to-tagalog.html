Answer:

Mao Zedong was a Chinese communist revolutionary who played a key role in the establishment of the People's Republic of China in 1949. He served as the Chairman of the Communist Party of China from 1943 until his death in 1976.

Mao Zedong's rise to power in China was similar to Joseph Stalin's rise to power in the Soviet Union in the sense that both leaders were able to consolidate their power through the implementation of strict political and social controls, and the use of propaganda and censorship to maintain their grip on power.

30. what is the use of 得 in Chinese grammar?(Tagalog or Korea explaination)​


Answer:

English:

得as dé When 得is pronounced dé in second tone, it's a verb meaning “to get” or “to acquire”.

Tagalog:

得 bilang dé Kapag ang 得 ay binibigkas dé sa pangalawang tono, ito ay isang pandiwa na nangangahulugang "upang makakuha" o "Makuha"

The Philippines has its belief and faith in God, not because of the Portuguese group. Instead, we Filipinos adopt these values after the expansion and colonization of the Spaniards in the year March 21, 1521. The remarkable person behind the discovery of the Philippines is an expeditioner and navigator born Portuguese Ferdinand Magellan. Therefore Magellan serves and sails for the country of Spain. After Discovering the Island of the Philippines (Las Filipinas), Magellan immediately lay and discussed the idea and approach of catholicism to the Filipinos.

4. 11. Find the 12th term of the geometric sequence 1, 2,4,...pakisama narin po ng computation. thank you​


an = a1 * r^n-1
where: a1 = 1
n = 12
r = 2
a12 = ?

a12 = 1 * (2)^12-1
a12 = 1 (2)^11
a12 = 2048

The 12th term is 2048.Answer:2048Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]a_{1} =1\\a_{2}=2\\\\r = a_{2}\div a_{1}\\r = 2 \div 1\\r = 2[/tex]

Use the general formula: [tex]a_{n} =a_{1} r^{n-1}[/tex]

Substitute:

[tex]a_{n} =a_{1} r^{n-1}\\a_{12} =1 (2)^{12-1}\\a_{12} =1 (2)^{11}\\a_{12} =1 (2048)\\a_{12} =2048\\[/tex]

Then, the value of the 12th term of the sequence 1, 2, 4,... is 2048.

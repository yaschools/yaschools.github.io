12. What does the speaker mean when he said that profession becomes a mission?​.


Answer:

When a person says that their profession has become a mission, they are saying that their work is more than just a job or a source of income for them. It has become a purpose or a calling in their life, something that they feel passionately about and are dedicated to doing to the best of their ability. They may see their profession as a way to make a positive impact on others or to contribute to a cause that they believe in. They may also feel a strong sense of responsibility and commitment to their profession, and take pride in doing it well.

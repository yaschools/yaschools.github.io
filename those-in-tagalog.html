20. Are these dresses in your home which have the materials of the barong Tagalog or are designed after the Barong tagalog? What makes those dresses special? Why are they not worn every day?


Answer:

What makes those dresses special?

Those dresses were special because it plays a big part of us being a Filipinos. It became our characteristic and own style that even other countries knows.

Why are they not worn everyday?

years passed and we are being influenced by the clothing and style of other countries. Specially when we became a 'free country'. Barong tagalog was not worn everyday because it has a special day/ month to be worn. And that was every 'buwan ng wika'. It was made to commemorate or bring back the kinf of past that we, Filipinos had.

Answer:

Marubdob kong hinihingi ang tawad sa lahat ng mga taong aking inapi at pinag-discriminate. Nais kong humingi ng paumanhin sa mga nasaktan ko at ginawa ko silang mababa. Hindi tamang ginawa ang pagpapakita ng ganitong uri ng pag-uugali at alam kong may mas magandang paraan na magpakita ng kabutihan sa ibang tao. Nauunawaan ko kung hindi kayo handang magpatawad sa akin, subalit nais kong malaman ninyo na magbabago na ako at gagawin ko ang lahat ng aking makakaya upang hindi na maulit ang ganitong uri ng pagkakamali. Sana ay magbigay kayo ng pagkakataon na maipakita ko ang aking pagbabago at kung paano ko kayo mamahalin at igagalang. Maraming salamat sa pagbibigay ng panahon na basahin ang liham na ito.

4. Uses Fuel – Butane, Diesel

6. Preservative – Acetic Acid

7. Ripener – Kalburo

Beverage - A liquid meant for human consumption is referred to as a drink or beverage.

Example:

Ethyl Alcohol or Ethanol - Alcoholic beverages contains ethanol when they are diluted.

Food - any edible component that is consumed or ingested by animals, people, or pl4nts in order to sustain life and growth.

Example:

Acetic Acid - Acetic acid is a sour ingredient that is added to vinegar, pickled vegetables, sauce, and utilized as a starting point for spices.

Antiseptic - is a chemical that inhibits or ret4rds microbial growth.

Example:

Ethyl Alcohol or Ethanol - As an antiseptic, ethanol is a component of hand sanitizer and medical wipes. Ethanol kills microorganisms by destroying their cell walls and denaturing their proteins. The majority of bacteria, fungi, and viruses are known to be successfully combatted by ethanol.

Fuel - a material that is used to generate heat or electricity, typically by burning it

Example:

Diesel - diesel fuel refers to the distill4te fuel oil sold for use in automobiles with compression ignition engines.

Butane - is a hydrocarbon that may be quickly liquefied and is a highly fl4mmable, colorless, and odorless gas. It is frequently employed as a fuel for portable stoves and cigarette lighters, as a propell4nt in aerosols, as a heating fuel, as a refrigerant, and in the production of a broad variety of goods.

Cleaner – a substance that is used in cleaning.

Example:

Formaldehyde - is a chemical that is colorless, combustible, and pungent that is present in cleaning supplies.

Preservatives - a compound used to prevent deterioration in food, wood, or other things.

Example:

Acetic Acid - Due to its ability to inhibit bacterial development, acetic acid is also a widely used preservative.

Ripener - any chemical used to enhance ripening

Example:

Kalburo - Actually, Kalburo is calcium carbide. Its primary industrial usage is in the synthesis of calcium cyanamide and acetylene. This substance is also used to ripen fruit synthetically.

25. Pilosopiya Nagtatag Bansang Sinilangan Mga batayang buro at aral Confucianismo Taoism Legalismo​


Answer:

Cunfucianismo - Ito ay itinatag ni Confucius. Ang Confucianismo ay nakapokus sa paraan ng pamumuhay at ethical teachings.

Mahalagang pilosopiya ni Confucius:

"When you know a thing to hold that you know it; and when you do not know a thing, to allow that you know it - this is a knowledge."

Taoism - Itinatag ni Lao Tzu na sumusunod sa tinatawag na tao na ang ibig sabihin ay "ang daan".

Mahalagang pilosopiya ni Lao Tzu:

"If you are depressed, you are living in the past. If you are anxious, you are living in the future. If you are at peace, you are living in the present."

Legalismo - Ito ay umiral sa panahon ng pamumuno ni Shi Huang Ti. Ayon sa legalismo, dapat na palawakin, patibayin, at patatagin ang estado at mahalaga ang istriktong batas ng pamahalaan at estado upang ang lahat ng miyembro ng lipunan ay kumilos at gumawa ng mabuti at wasto.

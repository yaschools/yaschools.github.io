#BrainlyFast

Two cars, one of mass 3 000 kg and one of mass 2 000 kg, collide head on. The car with more  mass experiences greater force and lesser acceleration with respect to the smaller  car.

The second law of motion is also known as the Law of acceleration:

Force causes the object to accelerate. The formula for Force is ( Force = mass × acceleration ). This means that the Force and acceleration when mass is constant is directly proportional. The stronger the force the higher the acceleration. When force is constant, acceleration and mass are inversely proportional. The higher the mass the lesser the acceleration and vise versa.

Force can make the moving object to move faster or stop and it can make the object at rest to move.

16. Burger barn makes dipping sauce by mixing juice bowls of honey with 1/2 spoonful of mustard. Sandwich, Towne stupid sauce by mixing 4 spoonfuls of honey with one spoon full of mustard. Which dipping sauce has a stronger mustard flavor?


None of the sauces has a stronger mustard flavor.

Burger barn's dipping sauce involves 2 spoonfuls of honey (from the juice bowl) for every 1/2 spoonful of mustard.

Sandwich towne's dipping sauce involves 4 spoonfuls of honey for every 1 spoonful of mustard.

Try to multiply Burger barn's 2 spoonfuls of honey by 2, which also multiplies its 1/2 spoonful of mustard by two.This equates to 4 spoonfuls of honey which also denotes to 1 spoonful of mustard, the very same ratio used for Sandwich towne's dipping sauce.

Both have the same ratio for its honey and mustard ingredients.

Thus, the two dipping sauce tastes the same because none of the sauces has a stronger mustard flavor.

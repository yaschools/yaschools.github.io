2. Cedric took an 80-item test, he got 95% of it correctly. How many incorrect answers did Cedric get?​


Answer: He got 4 incorrect answers.

Solution:

Multiply 80 by 95% or .95

80 x .95 = 76

76 is the total amount of correct answers Cedric got.

Subtract 76 from 80.

80 - 76 = 4

4 is the final answer.

8. what is land breeze and sea breez​


Answer:The wind blowing from land towards the sea is Land breeze. The wind blowing from the sea towards the land is Sea breeze.

Explanation:hope it helps pls brainleist

Answer:

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Land breeze and sea breeze are two types of winds that occur near coastal areas due to the difference in temperature between the land and the sea.

A sea breeze is a cooling wind that blows from the sea towards the land during the day. This happens because the land heats up faster than the sea, causing the air above the land to rise and creating a low-pressure zone. The cooler air over the sea then flows in to fill this low-pressure zone, creating a sea breeze. The sea breeze is typically strongest in the afternoon and early evening when the temperature difference between the land and sea is greatest.

A land breeze, on the other hand, is a cooling wind that blows from the land towards the sea at night. This occurs because the land cools down faster than the sea, causing the air above the land to become cooler and denser, creating a high-pressure zone. The warmer air over the sea then flows in to fill this high-pressure zone, creating a land breeze. Land breezes are typically weaker than sea breezes and occur in the late evening and early morning hours.

Both sea and land breezes are important in regulating the temperature and climate of coastal areas. They can also affect activities such as sailing, fishing, and beach-going, making them important to understand for anyone spending time near the coast.

30. how does land breeze breeze and sea breeze affect the air in the surroundings?


Answer:

The cool breeze that comes along is sea breeze. There is also a cool breeze coming from land and that is land breeze.

Explanation:

This usually happens in coastal areas or areas near large water. Water and land have different heating abilities. Water takes a bit more time to warm up and is able to retain the heat longer than land does.

In the day, when the sun is up, the land heats up very quickly and the air above it warms up a lot more than the air over the water. The warm air over the land is less dense and begins to rise. Low pressure is created.  

The air pressure over the water is higher with cold dense air, which moves to occupy the space created over the land. The cool air that comes along is called a sea breeze. At night, it is the land that is cooler than water since it has a faster capability to adapt. The cool breeze that land produce at night is land breeze.

1. Strong earthquakes have caused countless deaths all over the world including our country. Aside from earthquakes, Philippines also experiences this natural phenomenon which causes heavy rains, strong winds and terrible flood. What do you call this phenomenon?

A. TyphoonB. TsunamiC. TornadoD. Hailstorm

Answer: [tex]\large\tt\red{A.}[/tex]Typhoon

Tropical cyclone (also called typhoon or "bagyo", cyclones or hurricane) is a weather disturbance that forms over tropical, warm oceans. It is characterized by low atmospheric pressure, strong winds, heavy rainfall, and storm surge as it approaches a coast. The windspeed ranges from 45 kph to about 300 kph.

Tropical cyclones need water vapor or warm moist air to start. They form only over warm ocean waters near the equator. It develops at latitudes usually greater than 5° from the equator. Scientists says, the temperature of ocean water must be 26.5°C or greater.

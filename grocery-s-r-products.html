4. a 45 kg grocery is riding on a 9kg pushcart which travels at 2.0m/s . what is the total momentum of a grocery and a pushcart​


MOMENTUM

A 45 kg grocery is riding on a 9kg pushcart which travels at 2.0m/s . what is the total momentum of a grocery and a pushcart?

The formula for finding the total momentum of an object is P=MV or Mass times velocity.

Let's list the givens!

P=45kgP=9kgP=2.0m/s

Let's solve it!

P=(45+9)*2P=54*2P=108

The final answer is 108kg•m/s

2. A companys manufacturing overhead is 40% of its total conversion costs if direct albor is 93000


Answer:

Total Conversion Costs = 155,000

Manufacturing Overhead = 62,000

Explanation:

Conversion Costs = Direct Labor + Overhead

CC = 93,000 + OH

100% = 60% DL + 40% OH

93,000/60% = 155,000

155,000 * 40% = 62,000

12. are sections of an email client that are used to store different kinds of email.


An Email Folder is a part or section of an email client or server which can store all types of email ranges from Inbox emails and sent emails. You can create as much folders as long as there is space in your mail server or client. You can also rename, delete, sort and move folders like a typical file folder in your computer. Almost any email client or server supports the creation of email folders. Other known folders are Inbox, Outbox/Sent Folders, Spam and Trash.

27. What do you call anapp used for sending, receiving, and organizing emails. Select one: a. Email server b. Email service c. Email client d. Email address


C. Email Client.
An email address is not an app. The email server and service is where the email is processed and routed to the recipient. The email client is what you have installed on your phone or laptop used to send, receive, and organize emails.

30. ano ang kahulugan ng email tagalog

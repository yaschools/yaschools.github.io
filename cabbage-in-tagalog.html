22. 4. There are 60 pechay and 80 cabbage plants planted in rows at the school garden. Each row has the same number of pkants. How many peachay and cabbage plants planted in each row? A. 6 pechay and 7 cabbgeB. 5 pechy and 6 cabbageC. 4 pechay and 5 cabbageD. 3 pechay and 4 cabbage​


D. 3 pechay and 4 cabbage

step by step explanation:

According to the question assume there are a pechay and b. cabbage in each row.

∴ the number of plants in each row is a+b.

∴ The number of row is (60+80)÷(a+b)

ㅤ= 140÷(a+b)

also the number of rows is 60÷a or 80÷b

∴ 140/a+b = 60/a = 80/b

=> a=3/4b

∴ b is multiple of 4

∴ b's positive value are 4,8,12,...4n.

Thus, The option D. 3 pechay and 4 cabbage is correct.

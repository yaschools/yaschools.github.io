29. 1. Calculate the number of moles of gas contained within a bouncy house with a volume of 20.63 cubic meters, a temperature of 300 Kelvin and a pressure of 101 kPa.​


Given:

V= 20.63 m³

P= 101 kPa

T= 300 K

General Gas Law:

PV = nRT

P = Pressure

V = Volume

n = No. of moles

R = Gas Constant

T = Temperature

Conversion:

P = 101 kPa x 1atm/101.325kPa

= 0.997 atm

Constant:

R = 8.20573 (m³atm)/(mol K)

Derivation:

PV=nRt (Divide both sides by RT)

n=PV/RT

Now, Solve:

n = (0.997atm)(20.63m³)/(8.205m³atm/mol K)(300K)

n = 8.36x10^-3

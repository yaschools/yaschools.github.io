7. how do you call a lip valley flute of cordillera?a. diwdiw-asb. saggeypoc. tongalid. paldong ​


Answer: d. paldong

The paldong is a traditional lip-valley flute of the Kalinga tribes in the Philippines. The paldong is made of bamboo. Its upper edge is cut away obliquely from the backside, and then cut away slightly from the frontside. The player's lower lip is placed against the cut away surface. It is open at both ends.

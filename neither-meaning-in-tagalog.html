28. NOVEL : NEW – Do these words have a.)similar meanings b.)contradictory meanings c.)neither similar it contradictory​


The answer for the question is a. similar meanings. Novel and new has similar meaning.

Because the meaning was not the opposite, the second alternative, contradicting meaning, is incorrect. Because they did not have a different meaning, the final alternative, neither similar nor conflicting, is also incorrect.

Even if they can be employed in many contexts and situations, some words may have similar meanings to one another. According to dictionary.com, a novel is something that is brand-new and unusual; distinct from anything that has been observed or previously known; it is also something that hasn't been previously detected or reported. Meanwhile, new is defined as of recent origin, production, purchase, etc.; having but lately come or been brought into being. The other definition of new is of a kind now existing or appearing for the first time or having but lately or but now come into knowledge. Therefore, the correct answer is a. similar meaning.

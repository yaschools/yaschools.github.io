2. is the meaning of the word kuta​


Answer:

kuta ay tumutukoy sa isng lugar na ginagawang taguan o kampo ng isang tao o grupo ng tao.

Answer:

KUTA means "hideout o Taguan o Kampo"

Explanation:

There are historical renderings of this word into Spanish and English as “cotta” — in fact, there is a Barangay Cotta in Lucena City, Quezon Province, where part of their official history states that the place name is a Spanish form of kuta, meaning “hideout.”

The English word “barricade” has been translated into Tagalog as munting kuta (modest entrenchment). Most Filipinos these days simply say barikada.

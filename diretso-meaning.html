26. Bakit hindi diretso ang linya ng PPF(Production Possibilities Frontier)?​


If the shape of the PPF curve is a straight-line, the opportunity cost is constant as production of different goods is changing. But, opportunity cost usually will vary depending on the start and end points

Its always drawn as a curve and not a straight line because there a cost involved in making a choice i.e when the quantity of one good produced is higher and the quantity of the other is low.

22. 6. If 5 street sweepers can clean a certain street in 3 hours, how long will ittake 7 of them to clean that street?​


Answer:

1 hour and 48 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

180 mins. ÷ 5 = 36 mins. each sweeper

36 + 36 = 72

180 mins. - 72 mins. = 108 mins.

108 - 60 (1 hr.) = 48

= 1 hr. and 48 mins.

11. ang taong walang kibo


Answer:

nasa loob ang kulo

Explanation:

Sa loob ang kulo: Speaking the Unconscious in the Transformations of an old Filipino Proverb

This philosophical  article argues for an elucidation of a concept of the unconscious, kulo (the boiling), that may have been originally enunciated by an old Filipino metaphorical proverb,  Ang taong walang kibo, nasa loob ang kulo, and which continues to be invoked in today’s handy expressions Sa loob ang kulo or kulo sa loob.  The essay draws inspiration from contemporary philosophical suggestions about the ‘metaphorical sedimentation of concepts’ (Derrida), and the critical role of the philosopher in the ‘fabrication of concepts’ (Deleuze and Guattari, 1991)

The main thesis is elaborated in three parts: First, I chart the inception and subsequent development of the concept, both in its semiotic as well as socio-historical  manifestations, and how it makes sense within traditional Filipino worldview;  Second, I demonstrate how the syntax, logical grammar, and tropological qualities of kulo’s enunciations all co-operate in supporting its identification as a concept of the indeterminate unconscious; Third,  I  show how kulo resonates well with metaphors in Western psychoanalysis (Deleuze and Guattari’s ‘lava and water’, and Castoriadis’s ‘magma’) that are meant to signal a more dynamic rethinking of the unconscious, in terms of drives or desire.  In the conclusion, I articulate some suggestions borne out by the study on what a metaphorics/metaphorology of the unconscious might contribute to the re(con)figuring of psychoanalysis.

24. Pagiging umid o walang kibo


Ang pagiging umid o walang kibo ay isa sa mga hadlang sa mabuting komunikasyon. Ibig sabihin ng umid ay ang pagtatago ng saloobin, na tila mahirap pakisamahan. Hirap silang makipagsalamuha sa iba at maghayag ng kanilang damdamin. Sadyang may mga taong hindi makwento, kahit anong saya o lungkot pa ang nararamdaman nito.

Ang mga iba pang hadlang sa mabuting komunikasyon ay ang mali o magkaibang pananaw, pagkainis o ilag sa kausap, at takot na ang sasabihin ay dadamdamin o didibdibin.

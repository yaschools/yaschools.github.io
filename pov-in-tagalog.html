1. what is the POV of avatar's trailer explain why1st pov 2nd pov3rd pov​


Answer:

POV of Avatar Trailer

The trailer for the movie Avatar is presented from the point of view (POV) of the main character, Jake Sully. This POV is used to provide the audience with a sense of immersion and to establish a connection with the character as they experience the world of Pandora alongside him. Additionally, this POV allows for the audience to learn about the story and the world of Pandora at the same pace as the main character, making for a more engaging and dynamic viewing experience.

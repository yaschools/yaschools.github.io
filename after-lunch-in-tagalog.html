11. sally walk a 3/8 of a kilometers before lunch and 4/8 km after lunch how did you walk in all​


PROBLEM

Sally walked 3/8 kilometers before lunch and 4/8 km after lunch. What are the total kilometers Sally had walked?

SOLUTION

[tex]1. \: \displaystyle \frac{3}{8} \: + \: \displaystyle \frac{4}{8} \\ \\ \implies \displaystyle \frac{3 + 4}{8} \\ \\ \implies {\underline{\boxed {\displaystyle \frac{7}{8} }}}[/tex]

Since the two given fractions have the same denominators, we'll just have to add the numerator of the two given distance Sally had walked then copy the given denominator they have in common to get the final answer.

Sally had walked a total distance of [tex] {\underline{\boxed {\displaystyle \frac{7}{8} }}}[/tex]

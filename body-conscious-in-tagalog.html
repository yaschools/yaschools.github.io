Having the freedom to work whenever one wishes is an example of flexibility. Although everyone has a different range of flexibility, it is still important to maintain it in order to keep your joints and entire body healthy. Exercises for flexibility increase a joint's capacity to sustain the range of motion required for performing daily activities and engaging in physical activity.

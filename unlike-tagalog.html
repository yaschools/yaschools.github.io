What are unlike signs in math?

Multiply or divide, if the signs are same (like) the sign of the product or quotient will be positive. Multiply or divide, if the signs are different (unlike) the sign of the product or quotient will be negative.

What is unlike sign?

When the integers have unlike sign: The multiplication of a positive integer and a negative integer is always negative. And in other words when both the numbers have different (unlike) signs, the result of the multiplication is always negative.

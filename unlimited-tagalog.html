8. Discuss unlimited earning opportunities​


Answer:

What jobs have unlimited earning potential?

The only types of jobs that have unlimited earning potentials are asset-based - i.e. you are selling an asset instead of your time.

Some examples of such jobs

authors

musician/actors who earns residuals from the music/movies sold

indie developers who sells software licenses

inventors who earns royalty from their patents

entrepreneurs who create businesses that deals with assets

investors who identify under-valued companies and purchase equity

Jobs that aren't backed by assets, no matter how well paid - accountants, lawyers, professional athletes - are all capped by the numbers of hours you can realistically work.

Fnet = m x a

Given: Fnet= 500N
            mass= 250g
            
500N= 250g x a
500N / 250g= a
2m/s^2= a

Answer:

a. 5:4

Explanation:

We can express ratios in lowest term by finding their greatest common factor. Simply list all the factors of the given numbers in the ratio. An example here is number 1, the real answer in number 1 is 10:8, but we can express 10:8 in lowest term by finding the greatest common factor of both 10 and 8, let's list all the factors of 10 and 8.

Factors of 10: 1, 2, 5, 10

Factors of 8: 1, 2, 4, 8

The underlined digit is the greatest common factor of 10 and 8. Now, divide 10 to 2, the answer is 5, and divide 8 to 2, the answer is 4. Therefore, the ratio will gonna be 5:4.

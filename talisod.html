4. description/function of palisade layer​


Answer:

The palisade mesophyll layer of the leaf is adapted to absorb light efficiently. The cells: are packed with many chloroplasts. Chloroplasts are an important part of plant cells because they are cell structures that allow for photosynthesis. The palisade layer is therefore mainly responsible for producing food and oxygen for the plant through photosynthesis.

17. What is palisade layer​


Answer:

The cuticle and upper epidermis provide protection for the plant. Below that is the palisade layer, which is the location of photosynthesis within the leaf. Below the palisade layer is the spongy layer, which contains cells that are more spread out, allowing for air pockets

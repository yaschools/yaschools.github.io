17. Jane buys a reel of thread for sewing. There are 30 m of thread on the reel. She uses 850 cm. How much is left on the reel in centimeters?


Answer:

2150 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Unit Conversion

Unit conversion is a way of expressing the same quantity or property of a material or substance to be converted in a different unit of measurement. A constant unit conversion factor is given to each type of measurements. For instance, distance with the unit of meter (m) has a unit conversion factor of 100 in order to convert to centimeter (cm).  Measurements are given in two commonly used systems of units namely the English and SI units.

Commonly used unit conversion factors

Here are the widely and most commonly used units of conversion.

1. Length/distance: meter = 100cm

                                kilometer = 1000 meter

2. mass: kilogram = 1000 grams

3. time: hour = 60 minutes

4. volume: Liter = 1000 milliliter

5. Temperature: degree Celcius = 273.15 K

In this problem, we are given 30 meters of total thread that jane bought for sewing and she used 850 cm. We need to calculate the reel in cm left after her sewing.

Solution:

Unit of measurements must be consistent with each other to perform any mathematical operation. Hence, convert the total length of the thread to cm and proceed to subtraction.

30 meter x 100 centimeter/ meter = 3000 centimeters

3000 centimeters - 850 centimeters = 2150 centimeters

Therefore, Jane has 2150 centimeters left on her reel of thread.

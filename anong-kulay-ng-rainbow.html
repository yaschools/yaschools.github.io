12. What is the phenomenon wherein a rainbow is inside the rainbow? A. Primary Rainbow B. Secondary Rainbow C. Supernumerary D. All of the above


B. Secondary rainbow

Caused by light being reflected twice inside the raindrop, this phenomenon is what we call double rainbow. In this phenomenon, a faint, secondary rainbow appears above the primary one.

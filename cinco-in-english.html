Answer:

VICENTE CINCO: Vicente Cinco's objective in educating the people of tomorrow was to promote nationalism and social awareness through education. On the other hand, Benigno Aquino Jr.'s objective was to provide access to quality education for all Filipinos, regardless of their social status.

CONTRADO AGUINO: His legacy has inspired many Filipinos to pursue education and strive for a better future. Today, the Philippines continues to prioritize education as a key factor in national development."

Explanation:

The difference between Vicente Cinco and Contrado Aguino is that while Cinco focused on developing the intellectual and moral faculties of students through a liberal arts education, Aquino emphasized the importance of vocational and technical training to prepare students for practical skills needed in the workforce. Both educators aimed to educate the people of tomorrow but had different approaches to achieving this goal.

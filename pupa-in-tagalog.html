15. what is the description of a pupa


Answer:

A pupa is an intermediate, typically dormant stage of a metamorphosing insect (such as a bee, moth, or beetle) between the larva and the imago. It is typically enclosed in a cocoon or protective covering and goes through internal changes where the structures of the larva are replaced by those characteristic of the imago.

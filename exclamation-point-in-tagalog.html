18. an exclamation point is?​


Answer:

The exclamation mark, !, also sometimes referred to as the exclamation point, especially in American English (another term is ecphoneme, now obsolete) is a punctuation mark usually used after an interjection or exclamation to indicate strong feelings or high volume (shouting), or to show emphasis.

Answer:

The exclamation mark, !, also sometimes referred to as the exclamation point, especially in American English (another term is ecphoneme, now obsolete) is a punctuation mark usually used after an interjection or exclamation to indicate strong feelings or high volume (shouting), or to show emphasis.

19. Why should you keep calm when earthquakes happen


It is important to keep calm when earthquakes happen because remaining calm will help you think clearly and make the right decisions to keep yourself safe.

What to do before an earthquake:

Participate in earthquake drills so you will know what to do and where to go in case of earthquakes and have an emergency plan.Prepare a Go Bag which includes a medical kit, at least 48 to 72 hours supply of food and drinking water, flashlight, and whistle. You can use the medical kit in case you get injured, the supply of food and water if you get stuck and waiting for rescue, and the flashlight and whistle to help you call for help.

What to do during an earthquake:

Stay calm. Panicking will make you lose focus on your emergency plans.Practice "Drop, cover, and hold on".Stay under a sturdy furniture if you are trapped inside a building and cannot get out.Stay away from buildings, electrical posts, trees, or anything that might fall on you if you are outside.

What to do after an earthquake:

Do not get back inside a building immediately after an earthquake. Aftershocks may occur.Check yourself or loved ones for any injury.Stay tuned to the news for more information.

6. Ano ang isosceles triangle, rectangle, rhombus, trapezoid, right triangle Please pwedeng tagalog ​


Answer:

An isosceles triangle is a triangle that has two sides of equal length.

A rectangle is a four-sided shape where every angle is a right angle (90°).

A rhombus is a four-sided shape where all sides have equal length.

A trapezoid is a pair of parallel sides.

A right angle is an angle of 90°, as in a corner of a square or at the intersection of two perpendicular straight lines.

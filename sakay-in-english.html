Answer:

Ang mga salitang "sakay", "naganap", at "magaganap" ay pawang mga pandiwa na nangangahulugang ng pagsasagawa ng isang kilos o gawain. Ang mga salitang ito ay naglalarawan ng mga pangyayari na nangyayari, naganap, o magaganap pa lamang.

"Sakay" ay isang pandiwa na nangangahulugan ng pagpasok o paglulan sa isang sasakyan o anumang uri ng transportasyon. Halimbawa: "Sasakay ako sa eroplano papuntang Paris mamayang gabi."

"Naganap" ay isang pandiwa na nangangahulugan ng pagkakaroon ng isang pangyayari o pagtupad sa isang layunin. Halimbawa: "Naganap ang pagsabak ng ating koponan sa pambansang paligsahan."

"Magaganap" ay isang pandiwa na tumutukoy sa mga pangyayari o gawain na mangyayari sa hinaharap. Halimbawa: "Magaganap ang aming pagtitipon sa Sabado sa bahay ni Anna."

Ang mga pandiwang ito ay mga halimbawa ng mga salita na ginagamit upang ilarawan ang mga pangyayari sa isang maikling panahon, sa nakaraan, o sa hinaharap.

17. How did Macario Sakay die?


Macario Sakay's Death

Macario de Leon Sakay was a infamous Filipino general who was killed in 1907. He died by being hanged after imprisonment in the hands of American soldiers.

Known as the last true Katipunero, he fought for the country's freedom since the late Spanish rule in the 1890s until the American revolution in the 1900s. He was a strategist who showed his determination to win the country over with his strategic fighting and civil war victories. He was perceived as "unstoppable" and impossible to contain.

Such was the resentment, fear and hate of the Spaniards and Americans with his force that they spread false information against him then lured him with a fake deal—that the processing for the Philippine independence would commence should he surrender, and from doing so, he and his comrades were promised to be spared from imprisonment.

In the end, he was betrayed by a Filipino, sent by the American Governor General, who talked to him about the negotiation. He was captured in 1906 then hanged a year later.

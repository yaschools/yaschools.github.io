18. Two friends,Zale and Enzo,run in marathon. Zale finished a 22-km Marathon in dumaguete while Enzo Finished a 16 mile marathon in cebu?show the solution​


Answer:

To find the answer of this question, we need to convert the values to meters.

First we will consider Zale's skill:

He finished a 22 km marathon in Dumaguete.

[tex]\boxed{\sf{1\:km=1000\:m}}[/tex]

This means that, 22 km = 22 x 1000 = 22000 m

Zale covered 22000 m

Now, we can check Enzo's:

He finished a 16 mile race in Cebu.

[tex]\boxed{\sf{1\:mile=1609.34\:m}}[/tex]

This means that, 16 x 1609.34 = 25749.44 m

Enzo covered 25749.44 m.

This means that:

22000 < 25749.44

Enzo covered more distance.

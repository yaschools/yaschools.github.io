2. make a report about flood for four reporters tagalog report​


Answer:

A flood is a condition when an area is fully or partially submerged in water for a period of time due to man-made or natural causes. The natural reasons behind floods can be heavy and continuous rain for an extended time period.

Note:when you are writing a report about floods, you need to mention factors including the reasons that have led to the floods, the areas that have been flooded, the damage that has been caused by the floods, the measures taken to help with the damage, and the people affected.

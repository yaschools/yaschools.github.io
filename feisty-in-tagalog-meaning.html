Cats and dogs have been domesticated throughout the years, making them two of the most common pets. In some cases, owners abandon their pets and let them fend for themselves. There is no perfect pet, so let's take a look at the similarities between cats and dogs.

Cats and dogs are both friendly to humans, they may be aggressive and feisty at times, but affectionate towards their owners, nonetheless. They just need some understanding, if you can provide that for them, you'll be a great pet owner.

Despite having some similarities, cats and dogs are still very different. Most cats hate water, while dogs love taking baths. Cats destroy things with their paws, while dogs chew some objects due to curiosity. Cats steal food right from the table, but dogs wait patiently for you to give them something. They both show affection in different ways, a cat's affection can just be a slow blink, dogs on the other hand, shows affection by wagging their tail, which is more obvious.

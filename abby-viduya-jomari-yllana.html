14. abby's daily allowance is 60 pesos she spends 1/2 of it for snack and 1/3 for transportation.How much was left with abby's allowance?​


Answer:

10 pesos

Step-by-step explanation:

1/2 x 60

Put 1 as a denominator

1/2 x 60/1

Multiply the numerators and the denominators

(1)(60)/(2)(1)

60/2

Simplify

= 30

1/2 of 60 is 30; Abby spent 30 pesos for snacks.

1/3 x 60

Put 1 as a denominator

1/3 x 60/1

Multiply the numerators and denominators

(1)(60)/(3)(1)

60/3

Simplify

= 20

1/3 of 60 is 20; Abby spent 20 pesos for transportation.

60 (allowance) - 30 pesos (snacks) - 20 pesos (transportation)

= 10 pesos

10 pesos is left with Abby's allowance.

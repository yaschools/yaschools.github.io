13. 2. If you have a friend that recently found out that she is diagnosed with an anxiety disorder, what will you do to comfort her to stop her from panicking?


Anxiety is a normal emotion just like anger, fear, happiness, sorrow, and several others that a person can feel. It is just somewhat different because anxiety is more like a multiplied feeling of nervousness, worry, fear and uneasiness.

The best ways to help someone with anxiety are:

1. Listen carefully to what they have to say because they often have a hard time expressing what they really feel inside. It is important to just lend your ears without any judgement.

2. Don’t belittle them, thinking that you’ve gone through worse. Because we all have different coping mechanisms and what may have been easy for you may not have been easy for them.

3. Always be there for them. Push them (not too much) to do things that would improve their health including getting enough sleep, working out, and eating healthy foods.

4. Stay connected with them. What they need the most is care and love, just like almost everyone else when they’re going through a hard time.

5. Help them seek professional help. A mental health professional is vital for a person with mental health issues.

And most importantly, do not rush them to get better. Things take time, and time is the best solution to heal. Just always be by their side during those times, even if you have to wait for a long long time.

Answer:

Memory is a series of electrical signals within the brain which replicates the same pattern over and over. As your body repeats the pattern, it becomes more familiar to the point of being automatic. This is a memory. These patterns and pathways use nerve cells called neurons and the more the connection between neurons is used the stronger the pathway becomes. If the pattern stops being used for a while the brain "loses" the pattern to make way for ones which are used more often.

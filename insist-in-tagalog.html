25. What is the antonym of insist


Answer: The word insist may have different antonyms including the words dispute, negate, disclaim, disprove and disclaim.

Explanation:

For you to understand it better, let me tell you first the meaning of insist. Insist is a verb which means "to take a firm stand ". It can be synonymous to the words assert(v), claim(v) and protest(v). Example: I insist that my answer is correct.

On the other hand, the opposite of the word insist are the following words dispute, negate, disclaim and disprove  which has different uses depending on the context given.

These are the words with corresponding meanings from Merriam-Webster:

dispute- to express different opinions about something often angrilynegate- to think not to be true or realdisclaim-  to declare not to be truedisprove- to prove to be false

To sum up, these words mean "to declare and prove that something isn't true".

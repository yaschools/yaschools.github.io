Answer:

compound means two or more thank you po

Answer:

2. Howling wind and drenching rain rattled the loose windows on the old cabin.

3. The rain beat down on the broken roof and leaked in through the cracks in the door

Explanation:

Number 2 and 3 sentences contains a compound subject because the sentences contains a subject with two or more simple subjects.

For example number 1 there is only 1 simple subject which is The wind while number 2 the compound subjects are Howling wind and drenching rain. When we say a compound subject it should have two or more simple subjects, so basically since number 1 and 2 sentences have 2 simple subjects it is considered as sentences having compound subject.

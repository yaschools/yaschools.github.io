3. what is chuckle one sentence​


Answer:

Chuckle means to laugh quietly or giggle

"I chuckle at the reaction on her face"

Answer:

Chuckle is one of many words for different kinds of laughter. These include giggle, titter, snicker, and a word that is a cross between chuckle and snort — chortle. These words are all imitative. That is, the word itself sounds like the noise it refers to. ... Use chuckle when you mean a quiet, even soundless laugh

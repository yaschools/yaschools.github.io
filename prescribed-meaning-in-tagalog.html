5. Prescribed connector


Answer:

The ISO 80369 and ISO 18250 series of standards were introduced to prevent accidental mis-connections between different medical connectors. The prevention of mis-connection is achieved through the specification of the different types of small-bore connectors and reservoir connectors so that they are purposely dimensionally incompatible. This must be proven through CAD-analysis and/or physical testing.

There are currently eight standards in the ISO 80369 series, of which six are currently published and one is underway. The eighth standard has been in development for urethral and urinary applications for some time, although development of this standard has stalled.

While developing the ISO 80369 series it became apparent that mis-connections on the patient side were not the only ones possible. The ISO 18250 series was therefore developed to cover reservoir connectors and assist in reducing risk of mis-connection between small-bore connectors and reservoir connectors. This series contains five standards, and all are published.

28. Abandonds both the cultural goals and prescribed means to achieve them, but tries to set up new norms or goals.​


Rebels are those who abandon both the cultural goals set the by society and the means to achieve those goals. Instead, a rebel tries to set up new goals or new norms under his or her own terms. Being a rebel is usually associated with those who rise up in armed resistance against the government, pushing their own goals. It may also refer to a teenager who is just starting to get to know himself and going against the wishes of the parents. In both examples, there is the element of rejecting the norm and trying to establish a new normal.  

Resisting authority when the authority is wrong could be seen as good. But rebelling for the sake of rebellion, without just cause can be seen as bad for the society in general. After all, for a society to function, there has to be smooth relations between the government and the people. The same way, in the home, there has to be good relations between parents and children.

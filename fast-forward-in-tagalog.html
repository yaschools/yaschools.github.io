14. It is similar to a fast walk with the exception that the weight is carried forward on the ball of the foot. a. Slide b. Run c. Leap d. Skip​


RUN IS SIMILAR TO A FAST WALK

Running is similar to a fast walk, it is by taking quick steps in which every foot are lifted before the other foot or next foot touches the  ground.

TIPS IN PROPER RUNNING

Look Ahead

Do not stare at your feet. You should look forward to avoid any incidents because you are too careful in executing the skills.

Keep hands  at your waist

It is somehow tiring to put the hands on our waist. But we can adjust as we jog everyday.

Relax your hands

Always remember that when  you are doing jogging, relax your hands because the tension may go up  to our hands, then to our shoulders then goes to our neck.

Check Posture

It is important to maintain good posture while jogging to avoid fatigue.

Relax your shoulders

Always remember that jogging is a form to release stress, thus we should be relaxed.

Keep your arms at your side

avoid side to side arm swinging.

Don't bounce

If we are doing a lot of bounce while jogging, we can be easily tired. The higher we lift ourselves from the ground the greater the shock thus we can experience more fatigue.

Answer:

You have the correct formula:

Volume = (1/3)*(base area)*(height)

The base area is just the base length times its width, so the formula can be written as:

Volume = (1/3)*(base length*width)*(height)

You have the numbers: base length = 8 cm, width = 7 cm, height = 5 cm. Just plug those numbers into the formula to get the answer.

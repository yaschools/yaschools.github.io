9. About 20% of 80 children are malnourished. How many children are malnourished? A. 16 children B. 20 childrenC. 40 childrenD. 60 children​


Answer:

Letter A

Step-by-step explanation:

If 20% of 80 children are malnourished, we can find the number of malnourished children by multiplying the total number of children (80) by the percentage that are malnourished (20% or 0.20).

Number of malnourished children = 80 x 0.20 = 16

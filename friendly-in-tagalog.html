4. is composting environment friendly​


Answer:

It is.

Explanation:

This natural process transforms organic matter into a soil-like product called compost, which then becomes humus in the earth. This process comes from the fermentation of biodegradable waste in contact with microorganisms (bacteria and fungi) and oxygen.  

Bacteria and fungi need to be in close contact with both carbon-rich and dry waste: C and other wet nitrogen-rich waste: N, all in a well-ventilated atmosphere and at a relatively high temperature ranging from 30°C to 60°C.  

The proportion of carbon-rich composting material to nitrogen-rich compost will be critical in terms of the quality and speed with which your compost will be made. Link to the other article.  

Once decomposed, organic matter is transformed into simple elements that can be absorbed by plants, and it will continue to break down in the ground until it ultimately becomes humus. Humus plays a critical role in soil fertility, making composting a vital component in any natural garden.

21. the packaging is environment friendly way or not?explain the friendly way and not​


Answer:

Environment friendly packaging refers to packaging materials and designs that have minimal impact on the environment. There are two types of packaging: eco-friendly and non-eco-friendly.

Eco-friendly packaging - this is designed with an intention to minimize the negative impact to the earth. This may include using biodegradable materials like corn starch, recycled paper, or reusable alternatives like glass, metal, and canvas. Such packaging can be recycled and do not release harmful chemicals to the environment.

Non-eco-friendly packaging - This type of packaging has some negative impact on the environment. It includes materials that release harmful gasses, require huge quantities of energy and water during production, and not easy to reuse or recycle.

Ultimately, it is essential to make use of eco-friendly packaging because it helps to reduce the carbon footprint and environmental impact. Businesses can make sustainable choices in their packaging materials to create a positive impact on the planet.

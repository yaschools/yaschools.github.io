15. how many different ways can the letters of the word PANGASINAN be arranged?​


[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{QUESTION:}}[/tex]

how many different ways can the letters of the word PANGASINAN be arranged?

[tex] \\ [/tex]

[tex] \large\underline \mathcal{{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

Distinguishable permutation:

[tex]P = \frac{ \text{no. of objects}}{ \text{p!q!r!s!...}}[/tex]

Where p,q,r,s, are the times the objects are repeated.

[tex] \\ [/tex]

Given the word: PANGASINAN

The total objects of Pangasinan is 10Times the letters are repeated. A = 3 , N = 3

[tex] \\ [/tex]

P = n! / r!s!

P = 10! / 3!3!

P = 10! / 3×2×1×3×2×1

P = 3,628,800 / 36

P = 100,800

[tex] \\ [/tex]

28. How many permutation can be made with the word PANGASINAN​


Answer:

The answer is 100,800.

Step-by-step explanation:

How many ways can the word Pangasinan be arranged?

The word 'PANGASINAN' has 10 letters where A occurs

3 times, N occurs 3 times.

Therefore, required number of ways = 10!/3!×3! = 100800

Answer:

"How many permutations can be made with the word PANGASINAN​"

First, count the total and the repeated of the letters in the word:

Total of letters: 10Repeated letters: A = 3, N = 3

We'll be using the formula:

[tex]P = \frac{n!}{(n_1!)(n_2!)...(n_r)}[/tex]

Second input:

[tex]P = \frac{10!}{(3!)(3!)}[/tex]

Third, make into a notation:

[tex]P = \frac{10\times9\times8\times7\times6\times5\times4\times3!}{(3\times2\times1)(3!)}[/tex] (Cancel out 3!. Divide 9 to 3, it will be 3. Divide 8 to 2, it will be 4.)P = 10 × 3 × 4 × 7 × 6 × 5 × 4

Lastly, multiply:

P = 10 × 3 × 4 × 7 × 6 × 5 × 4P = 100, 800

Tada!! There are 100, 800 permutations that can be made with the word "PANGASINAN​".

Most Recent Eruption of Volcanoes

Most recent eruption of Mount Pinatubo:

The most recent eruption of Mount Pinatubo took place on November 30, 2021, when a phreatic eruption was detected by satellites and a Filipino airline pilot managed to capture a photo of its steam column.

Most recent eruption of Kanlaon:

The most recent eruption of Kanlaon Volcano took place in 2017. It was a phreatic eruption that sent ash to nearby towns.

Most recent eruption of Mount Bulusan:

The most recent eruption of Mount Bulusan took place in 2017. It was a phreatic eruption from the southeast vent.

Most recent eruption of Biliran:

The most recent eruption of Mount Biliran took place in 1939.

Most recent eruption of Cagua:

The most recent eruption of Mount Cagua took place in October 1860.

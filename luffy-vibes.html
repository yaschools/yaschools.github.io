12. Luffy is planning to build a regular 4 sided polygon table at what angle should luffy cut the wood​


Answer:

45 degree angle

Step-by-step explanation:

To build a regular 4-sided polygon table, Luffy will need to cut the wood at a 45-degree angle. This is because a square can be divided into four congruent right triangles, each with two legs that are equal to the length of the square's sides. In a right triangle, the two legs form a 90-degree angle, and the hypotenuse (the longest side) forms two 45-degree angles with each leg.

Therefore, if Luffy cuts the wood at a 45-degree angle, he will create four right triangles that can be assembled into a regular 4-sided polygon, or a square-shaped table.

21. Suppose a cyborg shoots Monkey D. Luffy with 3g bullets at a rate of 100 bullets/min, with speed of each bullet is 500 m/s. The bullets rebound straight back from Luffy's body with no change in speed. What is the magnitude of the average force on Luffy's body?


Answer:

The magnitude of the average force is 2.5 Newtons

Explanation:

Here, we are going to use the formula for the average force which is equivalent to the product of the mass and the acceleration.

F = ma

where

F     is the force, the unit is in Newton (N)

m    is the mass, the unit is in kg

a     is the acceleration, the unit is in m/s²  

Given information

m = 3g = 0.003 kg

v = 500 m/s     (speed)

a = [tex]\frac{v_f-v_i}{t} = 500 m/s (\frac{100 bullets}{min})(\frac{1min}{60s} )[/tex] = 833.333 m/s²

F = ?

Solving the problem

Let us solve for the average force of the bullet using the given formula for average force

F = ma

Then substitute the given data, we have:

F = (0.003 kg)(833.33 m/s²)

F = 2.5 Newtons

Take note that each bullet has a speed of 500 m/s and there are a total of 100 bullets. All of these bullets moved in 1 minute, that is why we multiply them to solve its acceleration.

Therefore, the average force of the bullets going to Luffy is 2.5N

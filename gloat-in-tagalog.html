1. what is the meaning of gloated?​


Answer:

is to brag or to have a feeling of self-satisfaction

Explanation:

Answer:

verb

\ ˈglōt \

gloated; gloating; gloats

Collegiate Definition (Entry 1 of 2)

intransitive verb

1: to observe or think about something with triumphant and often malicious satisfaction, gratification, or delight

//gloat over an enemy's misfortune

2:obsolete : to look or glance admiringly or amorously

noun

Collegiate Definition (Entry 2 of 2)

: the act or feeling of one who gloats

5. Rey bought 5 dozen bananas. 35% were ripe and the rest were unripe. What is the total number of unripe bananas? ​


Answer:

The total number of unripe bananas is 39.

Step-by-step explanation:

BASE = 5 dozen

1 dozen = 12 pcs

(12)(5) = 60 pcs

RATE = 35% ⇨0.35

Finding the percentage.

BASE x RATE

60pcs x 0.35 = 21 ripe bananas

60 pcs minus the 21 ripe bananas = 39 unripe ones.

23. Stack of telephone pole ha 30 pole in the bottom row. There are 28 pole in the econd row ,26 in the next row, and o on. How many pole are in the if there are 4 pole in the top row?.


Problem:

Stack of telephone pole has 30 poles in the bottom row. There are 28 poles in the second row, 26 in the next row, and so on. How many poles are if there are 4 poles in the top row?

Solution 1:

Arithmetic sequence formula:

aₙ = a₁ + (n - 1)d

a₁ = 30

aₙ = 4

d = 28 - 30 = -2

4 = 30 + (n - 1)(-2)

4 = 30 - 2n + 2

2n = 30 + 2 - 4

2n = 28

n = 14

Sum of an arithmetic sequence:

Sₙ = (n/2)(a₁ + aₙ)

Sₙ = (14/2)(30 + 4)

Sₙ = (7)(34)

Sₙ = 238

Solution 2:

no. of poles = 30 + 38 + 26 + ... + 4

no. of poles = 238

There are 238 poles if there are 4 poles in the top row.

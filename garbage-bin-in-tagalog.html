Special Products and Factors:

Question:

The Punong Barangay in your place noticed that garbage is not properly disposed because the garbage bins available are too small. As the chairman of the health committee, you were tasked to prepare garbage bins which can hold 24 ft³ of garbage. However, the location where the garbage bins will be placed is limited. How will you maximize the area?

a. Find the dimensions of the planned bin according to the capacity given.

b. Make trial and error bins until the desired volume is achieved.

c. Solve for the volume and use it in creating bins.

d. Find the area of the location of the bins.

Answer:

a. Find the dimensions of the planned bin according to the capacity given.

Explanation:

The best way to maximize the area where the garbage bins will placed is to know exactly the dimensions of the bin that will fit the capacity of the garbage that will be disposed. Doing this will minimize the time spent in building the garbage disposal facility.

11. five litters of gasoline cost 220 pesos. How much is the cost of 12 litters


Answer:

528 pesos

Solution:

Let L be the liters and

5L = 220

Divide 5 to both sides

L = [tex]\frac{220}{5} = 44[/tex]

Hence 12L = 44(12) = 528

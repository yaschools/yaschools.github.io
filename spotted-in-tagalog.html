Answer:

Ang Mount Malindig (kilala rin bilang Mount Malindik at Mount Marlanga [3]) ay isang malaking potensyal na aktibong stratovolcano sa isla ng Marinduque sa Pilipinas. Ito ang pinakamataas na rurok sa isla na may taas na 1,157 metro (3,796 ft) sa taas ng dagat. [2]

Ang pangalang Marinduque mismo ay maaaring nagmula sa Marindik o Malindik. Ang kwentong sinabi ng mga taga-Buenavista, ang bayan na nakaupo sa paanan ng Malindig, ay noong mga panahon ng Espanya, nawala ang isang sundalong Kastila. Nadatnan niya ang isang batang babae na nakaupo sa pag-uuri ng bigas. Tinanong niya siya kung ano ang pangalan ng lugar na ito. Tumugon siya sa Tagalog matindig (salin: "Let me stand up") upang makuha niya ang isang mas mahusay na pagtingin sa lugar. Nagkamali ang Kastila sa batang babae at inakala na ang bundok ay tinawag na Matindig, at hindi marunong bumigkas ng Tagalog, tinawag niya itong Malindig.

Ang Malindig ay itinuturing na isang potensyal na aktibong bulkan, [3] sa bahagi dahil sa mga solfataric spring ng Malbog. Ang natatanging lokasyon nito, sa gitna ng Timog Katagalugan, ay lumilikha ng isang panorama na sumasaklaw sa Mindoro, Verde Island, at Timog Luzon hanggang sa Bicol at Burias Island. Mayroong isang military radio outpost sa 900MASL. [4]

Ang bundok ay ang pinakamalaki sa isla ng Marinduque at ang pinakatanyag na pag-akyat at paglalakad sa isla para sa mga taga-bundok at magkamukha, na kilala sa natatanging biodiversity ng kapaligiran. Ito ang tahanan ng iba't ibang mga species ng ligaw na orchid at iba pang endemikong wildlife. Dahil sa taas nito sa itaas ng 1,000 metro (3,300 ft) sa taas ng dagat, ayon sa batas ito ay nakilala bilang isang protektadong lugar. Ang natutulog na bulkan ay may tatlong tuktok, ang Trek at umakyat sa tuktok ay na-rate na madaling mag-moderate. Matapos mapadaan ang base ng Radio Station sa bundok, ito ay kilala na isang mossy at saradong canopy jung trek na may regular na maulap na paligid. Ginagamit din ito ng mga bayan ng Buenavista bilang isang lugar ng pag-aalaga ng baka. [5]

Sa Panahon ng pamamahala ng Amerika sa mga Pulo ng Pilipinas, ang Bundok Malindig ay pinangalanan ng "Mga Bundok Marlanga" ng mga Amerikano. Ang pangalan ay pinalitan pabalik sa orihinal nitong pangalan ng "Mount Malindig".

Mt. Ang Malindig ay nasa paligid ng nasasakupan ng mga baranggay ng Buenavista (Sihi, Bagacay, Bicas-Bicas, Yook, at Tungib-Lipata) at Torrijos (Cawayanan, Malibago at Dampulan).

12. A farmer has brown cows with white spots (genotype BBss) and brown cows with no spots (genotype BBSS). If the farmer crossed a brown spotted bull with a brown spotted cow, what percentage of the cows would be black? 25% 0% 50% 100%


If the farmer crossed a brown spotted bull with a brown spotted cow, the percentage of the cows would be black is 0%.  

To explain this, let us clarify some concepts first.

Having a genotype of BB means that brown is a dominant allele giving black a genotype of bb which is a recessive allele.  

In this question, we are only be concerned with a single allele, the cow color and pattern is already two alleles. Thus, we will not be concerned about the cow coat pattern whether it would be spotted or not since the question is only concerned with cow color.  

Looking at the genotype of the brown cow, we can say that brown cow, having the genotype of BB is dominant over a black cow that should be having the genotype of bb.

Thus if you were going to cross breed a brown spotted bull with a brown spotted cow the probability of having a black is zero percent.  

In this problem, we assume that the cow and the bull has a homozygous dominant allele for their coat color which is brown. In this case, the probability of having black is zero. If we cross them, we have

BB x BB = 4 BB or 4 brown

However, if they were both heterozygous dominant Bb, the chance is 25%. If we cross them, we have

Bb x Bb = 1 BB, 2 Bb, 1 bb (3 brown, 1 black)

13. a. Describe solar spotsb. What is the sun spot cycle?c.Describe solar flares.​


Answer:

A.) Sunspots are storms on the sun's surface that are marked by intense magnetic activity and play host to solar flares and hot gassy ejections from the sun's corona.

B.) sunspot cycle is an 11-year cycle during which the number of sunspots rises to a maximum or falls to a minimum. The periodic change in the number of sunspots; the cycle is taken as the interval between successive minima and is about 11.1 years.

C.) solar flare is a sudden flash of increased brightness on the Sun, usually observed near its surface and in close proximity to a sunspot group. Powerful flares are often, but not always, accompanied by a coronal mass ejection

Answer:

B.The solar cycle or solar magnetic activity cycle is a nearly periodic 11-year change in the Sun's activity measured in terms of variations in the number of observed sunspots on the solar surface.

C.A solar flare is a sudden flash of increased brightness on the Sun, usually observed near its surface and in close proximity to a sunspot group. Powerful flares are often, but not always, accompanied by a coronal mass ejection. Even the most powerful flares are barely detectable in the total solar irradiance.

24. On Fridays, the ratio of occupied spots to empty spots in the parking lot is 10:13. If the number of cars that parked there on that day is 400, find the number of empty spots in the parking


Answer:

520 Empty Spots

Step-by-step explanation:

We start with understanding RATIO.

Ratio = In simple terms, a ratio shows the relationship of two entities. It is written in this format A : B.

Say you have 5 apples and 7 bananas in a basket.

: The ratio of apples to bananas is 5 : 7

: The ratio of bananas to apples is 7 : 5

Example 1:

In a basket, you have a ratio of apples to oranges of 2 : 6, this means that in every 2 apples there are 6 oranges. Now, there are 10 apples inside. Can you find how many oranges are there?

Solution:

Ratio of apples to oranges = 2 : 6

Total number of apples = 10

Total number of oranges = ?

1st Step: Find how many sets of 2 apples are there in 10 apples

= 10/2

= 5 sets of 2 apples

2nd Step: Multiply the the total sets of 2 apples to the equivalent number of oranges as per the given ratio

= 5 x 6

= 30

: there are a total of 30 oranges in the basket

(That's it! Simple isn't it?)

Now let's get it.

List all the given in the problem

* the ratio of occupied spots to the empty spots = 10 : 13

* Total number of cars = 400

* Total number of empty spots = ?

SOLUTION

From the given ratio 10 : 13, this means that for every 10 occupied spots or 10 cars there are 13 empty spots.

Step 1: Find how many sets of 10 cars are there in 400 cars.

= 400/10

= 40

There are 40 sets of 10 in 400 cars.

Step 2: Find the empty spots by multiplying the total sets of 10 to the equivalent empty spots as represented by the given ratio

= 40 x 13

= 520

There are 520 empty spots in the parking lot.

Answer:

Kathleen= 82.5 kg/ms²

Explanation:

FORMULA OF FORCE

F= m×a

where

F= Force

m= mass

a = acceleration

Jolene

F=m×a

F= 45kg × 1.5ms²

= 67.5 kg/ms²

Kathlene

F=m×a

F= 55 × 1.5

= 82.5 kg/ms²

30. Andrew, Jolene and Tommy each draw 3 cards from 9 cards numbered 1, 2, 3, …, 9.   Andrew: The product of all my numbers is 48.   Jolene: The sum of all my numbers is 16.   Tommy: The product of all my numbers is 63.  What is the largest number of Jolene’s cards?​


Answer:

56

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Cards: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9

Andrew: Product is 48

Jolene: Sum is 16

Tommy: Product is 63

Analysis

Each card/number can only appear once.

Available cards: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9

Tommy:

Breakdown 63

63 = 7 × 9

63 = 7 × 3 × 3 (Cannot be since 3 appears twice)

63 = 7 × 9 × 1

Available cards: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8

Andrew:

Breakdown 48

48 = 6 × 8

48 = 2 × 3 × 8 (Remaining numbers will be 4, 5, 6)

Is 4 + 5 + 6 = 16?

No, so Andrew's cards are not 2, 3, and 8.

Breakdown 48 again

48 = 6 × 8

48 = 6 × 4 × 2 (Remaining numbers will be 3, 5, 8)

Is 3 + 5 + 8 = 16?

Yes, so Andrew's cards are 2, 4, and 6.

Available cards: 3, 5, 8

Since these cards are left, these 3 cards are

Jolene's.

Jolene's cards: 3, 5, and 8.

Answer:

The largest number in Jolene's cards is 8.

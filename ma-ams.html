21. Ma. Joan is flying from Manila to Hongkong. She will depart at 7:50 am, Pacific Standard Time (PST), and will arrive at 10:10 am (PST). How long is the flight from Manila to Hongkong?


Answer:

2 hours and 20 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

7:50 + 10 = 8:00, Keep the 10 (mins)

10mins

8:00 + 2:00 = 10:00, Keep the 2 (hrs)

2hrs

10:00 + 10 = 10:10, Keep the 10(mins)

10mins

Mix all the kept numbers together.

2 hours + 10 mins + 10 mins = 2hrs and 20mins

Answer:

2 hours and 20mins

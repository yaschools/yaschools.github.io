2. Literary Devices  A Literary Device is a technique that shapes narrative to produce an effect on the reader.

3. PLOT DEVICE - A plot device is an object, a character or a concept introduced into the story by the author to advance its plot.   A Plot Twist is any unexpected turn of the story that gives a new view on its entire topic. A plot twist at the end of the story is called a twist ending.  A Flashing Arrow is a technique used to focus the reader's, but not the characters', attention on an object or a location that will be important later in the story.

16. What were the different premonitions that President Magsaysay and his family had?​


Answer:

There are several reports of premonitions or strange occurrences surrounding the death of President Ramon Magsaysay of the Philippines. Some of these include:

-President Magsaysay reportedly having a dream about his own death just before the crash.

-His wife reportedly having a dream about the death of her husband and seeing his spirit in the dream.

-The family dog reportedly howled and refused to be quiet the night before the crash.

-President Magsaysay's pilot, Captain Felix Manalo, reportedly had a premonition of the crash and initially refused to fly the plane.

It should be noted that such reports of premonitions and strange occurrences are often difficult to verify and are often the subject of superstition and folklore. Nevertheless, they are part of the legacy of President Magsaysay and the cultural memory of his death in the Philippines.

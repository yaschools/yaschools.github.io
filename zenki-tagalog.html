Lita's money was 45.00

Explanation:
Lita's Money= 5/4 or 1 1/4 Zenny's Money
Zenny's Money= 1/1 or 1
When Jane gave 9.00 to Zenny, Zenny's money became equal to Lita's which means 9.00 is 1/4 of their money.
So Lita has 9.00 x 5=45.00

To check:
Lita: 45
Zenny: 36

Lita: 45/36 or 5/4

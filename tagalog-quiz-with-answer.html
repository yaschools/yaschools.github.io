8. 1-5 quiz about tsunami with answers ​


Answer:

What is a tsunami?

A tsunami is a large ocean wave caused by a seismic event, such as an earthquake, volcanic eruption, or underwater landslide.

How do tsunamis form?

Tsunamis form when there is a sudden displacement of water, such as from an underwater earthquake or volcanic eruption. This displacement creates a series of large ocean waves that can travel long distances and cause significant damage along coastlines.

What are some warning signs of a tsunami?

Some warning signs of a tsunami include a sudden retreat of water from the shoreline, a loud roaring noise coming from the ocean, and strong shaking or ground rumbling.

What should you do if you are in a tsunami warning area?

If you are in a tsunami warning area, you should move to higher ground immediately and stay away from the coast. It is also important to stay informed about the latest weather and safety information from local authorities.

How can communities prepare for a tsunami?

Communities can prepare for a tsunami by creating evacuation plans, building early warning systems, and educating residents about the dangers of tsunamis and how to stay safe. It's also important for communities to develop strategies for protecting key infrastructure and assets, such as hospitals and emergency services.

15. A quiz consist of four multi questions with five possible responses to each question.In how manydifferent ways can the quiz be answered?​


To find the total number of ways to answer the quiz, we need to multiply the number of possible responses for each question together. Since there are four questions with five possible responses for each question, we have:

5 possible responses for the first question × 5 possible responses for the second question × 5 possible responses for the third question × 5 possible responses for the fourth question

= 5 × 5 × 5 × 5

= 625

Therefore, there are 625 different ways that the quiz can be answered.

STRATEGIC MARKETING MANAGEMENT means the process and methods used to identify and target the most desired customers within a given market and to develop and deploy marketing strategies to reach these customers.

1. Identification of the target market

The first step in any market strategy is identification of the target market. This can be done by studying the company’s competition, researching market trends, or by interviewing key customers. Once the target market has been identified, market research must be conducted to determine the target audience and the type of product or service that they want.

2. Marketing strategy

A marketing strategy is the plan that is put into place to reach the target market. This can include target market research, market targeting, and market positioning. The marketing strategy should also include a plan for promoting the product or service to the target market and for measuring the success of the marketing campaign.

3. Distribution

Distribution is the process of getting the product or service to the target market. This can include selling the product or service through traditional marketing channels such as television, radio, print, and the Internet. Distribution also includes creating and managing a marketing campaign to reach the target market.

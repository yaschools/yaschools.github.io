KAHULUGAN NG DEPRESYON

• • Sa tagalog ito ay tinatawag na depresyon. Ito ay isang mental illness o sakit sa utak kung saan lubhang naaapektuhan ang aktibidad at kalooban ng isang tao kabilang na ang kanyang pag-iisip, pag-uugali, damdamin at pakiramdam.

• Ang depresyon ay isang medical na kondisyon na kung saan naaapektuhan ang isip, ng tao na maaring mauwi sa pagka sira ng ulo. Ito ay nangyayari sa sobrang stress at sobrang pag-iisip na kung saan hindi na kayang kontrolin ng isang tao ang kanyang sarili.  

• Ang depresyon ay isang sakit dahil sa sobrang pagdamdam o pag-iisip.

• Ito ay isang sakit sa pag-iisip na may kasamang   sobrang baba ng tingin sa sarili, panlulumo lubhang pagkalungkot, at kawalan ng interes sa mga nakasisiyang gawain.

14. What is the meaning of depressions? and what are the types of depressions?


What is depression?

Depression - is marked especially by sadness, inactivity, difficulty in thinking and concentration, a significant increase or decrease in appetite and time spent sleeping, feelings of dejection and hopelessness, and sometimes suicidal tendencies.

○ Different Types of Depression ○

▪ Major depression

— it has severe symptoms lasting six months or more and has a profound effect on the patient's life.

▪ Bipolar disorder

— it's also known as manic depression . Those with this disorder may experience intense joy and deep sadness.

▪ Dysthymia

— although not as severe as major depression , has depressive symptoms that make it difficult for the patient to move normally. Others may also experience intense grief.

▪ Postpartum depression

— it is a debilitating mood disorder that affects many postpartum mothers.

▪ Seasonal affective disorder or SAD

— is more likely to occur due to lack of sunlight during the fall and winter. It usually disappears in spring and summer.

Answer:

Depression is a constant feeling of sadness and loss of interest, which stops you doing your normal activities. Different types ofdepression exist, with symptoms ranging from relatively minor to severe. Generally, depression does not result from a single event, but from a mix of events and factors.

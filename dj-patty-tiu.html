14. short summary of "Ang Bata Nga Dili Matulog" by Macariu Tiu pahelp pleasee


Answer:

"Ang Bata Nga Dili Matulog" is a Cebuano short story by Macariu Tiu that tells the story of a young boy who struggles to fall asleep at night. Despite his mother's efforts to comfort him, the boy remains wide awake, and his mind begins to wander. He reflects on his day, his fears, and his dreams, until eventually, he drifts off to sleep. The story explores the theme of childhood anxiety and the importance of parental support in helping children overcome their fears.

The essentialist viewpoint holds that some categories, such as women, racial groups, dinosaurs, and original Picasso artwork, have a profound meaning or true nature which can be seen with the bare eyes.

Fundamentally, essentialism is about creating and taking care of oneself. When using purpose, the same amount of energy can bring you much closer to your aim. Determine whatever the right things are for you to do, dismiss everything else, and concentrate your time and energy on making the greatest difference possible where it counts.

1. what is the meaning of stammered


stam·mer

ˈstamər/

verb

past tense: stammered; past participle: stammered

speak with sudden involuntary pauses and a tendency to repeat the initial letters of words.

synonyms:stutter, stumble over one's words, hesitate, falter, pause, halt, splutter

"he began to stammer"

utter (words) with a stammer.

"I stammered out my history"

Origin

￼

late Old English stamerian ; related to stumble. The noun dates from the late 18th century.

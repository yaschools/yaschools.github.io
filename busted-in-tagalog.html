5. a marble bust is 25 years old and a terra-cotta bust is 85 years old.In how many years will the terra-cotta bust be three times as old as the marble bust?


Answer:

In 5 years, the terra-cotta bust will be three times as old as the marble bust.

Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem

The problem above is asking for the number of years that the terra-cotta bust will be three times as old as the marble bust. To find the answer, we need to express our equation in algebraic equation.

Given:

marble bust - 25 years old

terra-cotta bust - 85 years old

Equation:

Let x be the number of years.

85 + x = 3 (25 + x)

Solution:

85 + x = 3 (25 + x)

85 + x = 75 + 3x

85 - 75 = 3x - x

10 = 2x

10/2 = 2x/2

5 = x

Final Answer:

5 years

Checking:

85 + x = 3 (25 + x)

85 + 5 = 3 (25 + 5)

90 = 3 (30)

90 = 90 ✔

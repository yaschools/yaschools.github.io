30. A city is supplied by a reservoir with a capacity of 3.09×108m3 of water. To improve the quality of supply, this reservoir will be deactivated after the construction of two new reservoirs that, together, will have the same water capacity as the reservoir that will be deactivated. The first of these new reservoirs is ready, and its capacity is 1.46×107m3 of water. What should be the water capacity of the second reservoir, in scientific notation, so that, together with the first, they have the same capacity as the reservoir that will be deactivated?a. 3.236×108m3.b 3.09×108m3. c.2.944×108m3. d.1.63×108m3 ​


SCIENTIFIC NOTATION

Scientific notation is a way of representing a very large number, or a very small number, through a quantity that has its value between 1 and 10, where to represent the remaining digits we use a power of base 10.

ANSWER 2.944*10⁸m³ or Letter C

The second tank must have a capacity of 2.944*10⁸m³, with the letter "C" being the correct answer

Explanation

To find the value of the second reservoir, we will have to do a subtraction operation with scientific notation, for this to be possible we have to equate the exponent of base 10 and do the operation with the numbers multiplied. We have:

R = 3.09*10⁸m³ - 1.46*10⁷m³R = 30.9*10⁷m³ - 1.46*10⁷m³R = 29.44*10⁷m³R = 2.944*10⁸m³

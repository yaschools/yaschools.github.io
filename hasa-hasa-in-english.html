Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

2kg galunggong

₱120.00/kg

hasa-hasa ₱165.50/kg

change = ₱263.50

₱1000.00

Solution:

2kg galunggong x ₱120.00/kg = ₱240.00

= ₱1000.00 - ₱240.00 - ₱263.50

= ₱496.50

kg of hasa-hasa = ₱496.50 / ₱165.50/kg

= 3 kgs

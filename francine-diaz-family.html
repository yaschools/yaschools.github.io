[tex]\\ \sf{} 4x + x = 20[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{} x = \frac{20}{5} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \large \boxed{ \sf{} x = 4} \\ \\ \\ [/tex]

Francine's score is 4.

[tex]\\ \sf{} = 4x[/tex]

[tex]\\ \sf{} = 4(4)[/tex]

So, regular price of roller blade is 1850Php.

Francine offers 20% discount, it means, that price becomes 20% less.

The initial price is 100%, so if it becomes 20% less it is 100% – 20% = 80%.

So, the selling price is 80% from the initial price, let's calculate it:

[tex]1850 \times \frac{80\%}{100\%} = 1850 \times 0.08 = Php1480[/tex]

So, the sale price is Php 1,480

29. francine has 50 buns.she sold 9/10 of it and gave 3 of th3 remaining buns to her brother. how many buns were left to francinea. 1b. 2c. 3d. 4with solution please thanks​


Linear Equation and It's Application in Real Life

In the given problem, it is an application of linear equation and solving word problem about mathematical operation. It is a combination of general mathematics and algebra.

The given problem is "Francine has 50 buns.she sold 9/10 of it and gave 3 of th3 remaining buns to her brother. how many buns were left to francine"

Step by step solution and discussion

In the given problem, apply your skills and knowledge in fraction and mathematical operation.

Step 1: List down all the given of the problem

50 buns

9/10 of it was sold

3 of the remaining buns to her brother

Step 2: Identify what is being ask

How many buns were left to Francine?

Step 3: Create mathematical operation

Remember, 1 represents a whole number in fraction and also represents hundred percent. Thus 1 - 9/10 represents for the remaining buns.

[(1 - 9/10) × 50 ] - 3 = N, where N is the number of buns left.

Step 4: Solve for the number of buns left

Since the equation has three operation, apply PEMDAS Or GEMDAS in simplifying the equation.

PEMDAS → Parenthesis, Exponents, Multiplication, Division, Addition, Subtraction

GEMDAS → Group, Exponents, Multiplication, Division, Addition, Subtraction)

It means, you must perform first the grouped equation, followed by exponents, and next is either multiplication or division (it was from left to right), then lastly is addition or subtraction (from left to right).

[(1 - 9/10) × 50 ] - 3 = N

[(10/10 - 9/10) × 50 ] - 3 = N, simplify first inside the parentheses

[(1/10) × 50] - 3 = N, then multiply

50/10 - 3 = N

50/10 is similar with 50 ÷ 10, so it was 5.

5 - 3 = N

N = 2, therefore the number of buns left to Francine is 2.

Final Answer

There 2 buns left to Francine.

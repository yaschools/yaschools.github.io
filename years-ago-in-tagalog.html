19. In 6 years,kate will be thrice as old as she was ten years ago. How old was kate 10 years ago?​


Answer:

8

Step-by-step explanation:

Age problems are math problems that require understanding of the timeline and solving the question using algebra.

The given age problem is solved as follows:

1. Set current age as variable [tex]x[/tex]

2. Consider the given timeline and make equation based on the statements. For this problem, the timeline is ten years ago and six years from now. Therefore:

ten years ago = [tex]x-10[/tex]                   Equation 1six years from now = [tex]x+6[/tex]           Equation 2

3. Consider the statement of the problem that "Kate will be thrice as old as she was ten years ago" and translate into an algebraic expression. Therefore:

ten years ago = [tex]x-10[/tex]                   Equation 1six years from now = [tex]3(x-10)[/tex]      Equation 3

4. From the formulated equation, we can equate Equation 2 and 3 since they are both Kate's age 6 years from now

[tex]x+6=3(x-10)[/tex]                                  Equation 5

5. Solve for current age x from Equation 5

[tex]x+6=3(x-10)\\x+6=3x-30\\6+30=3x-x\\36=2x\\\frac{36}{2} =\frac{2x}{2} \\18=x[/tex]

Kate's current age is 18

6. Finally, to get Kate's age ten years ago, we subtract 10 from the current age.

[tex]18-10=8[/tex]

Now, we solved that Kate's age ten years ago is 8.

[tex]P \: \text{(in atm) = 40 psi} × \frac{\text{1 atm}}{\text{14.696 psi}}[/tex]

5. The pressure of a certain valley below sea level is 200 kPa, what will be the height of the mercury column in a barometer?Solution:

[tex]P \: \text{(in mmHg) = 200 kPa} × \frac{\text{1 atm}}{\text{101.325 kPa}} × \frac{\text{760 mmHg}}{\text{1 atm}}[/tex]

13. Mr. Villamayor rented a construction crane for five hr and a backhoe for seven hr. The total amount he paid is less than Php 9,000. Suppose the hourly rate for the crane is Php 800. What is the maximum amount he paid for the backhoe to the nearest hundreds?


Representation:
Let 7x be the rent paid for backhoe for seven hours.

Inequality equation:
     Keyword: maximum amount, the most
     Inequality symbol:  ≤

     (800 × 5) + 7x ≤ 9,000
      4,000 + 7x ≤ 9,000
      7x ≤ 9,000 - 4,000
      7x ≤ 5,000
      7x ÷ 7  ≤  5,000 ÷ 7
      x ≤ 714.29

To the nearest hundreds:
      x ≤ 700 

ANSWER:  The maximum rental fee per hour  of backhoe is Php 700.00 (rounded off to the nearest hundreds).
 
To check if he paid less than 9,000 for the total rent:
     (800 × 5) + (700 × 7) < 9,000
     4,000 + 4,900 < 9,000
     8,900 < 9,000    (True)

8. 35% of the workforce in Company A is in the engineering department. Given that 105 people work in the engineering department, find the number of people in the workforce.


Let's let "x" represent the total number of people in the workforce.

We know that 35% of the workforce is in the engineering department, which can be represented as:

0.35x = 105

To solve for x, we can divide both sides by 0.35:

x = 105 ÷ 0.35

x = 300

16. for you what is workforce​


For me, workforce is the labour pool in employment. It is generally used to describe those working for a single company or industry, but can also apply to a geographic region like a city, country, state, etc. The term generally excludes the employers or management, and implies those involved in manual labour. It may also mean all those that are available for work. Workers may be unionised, whereby the union conducts negotiations regarding pay and conditions of employment. In the event of industrial unrest, unions provide a co-ordinating role in organising ballots of the workforce, and strike action.

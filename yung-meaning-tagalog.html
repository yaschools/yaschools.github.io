English:

An essay is, generally, a piece of writing that gives the author's own argument, but the definition is vague, overlapping with those of a letter, a paper, an article, a pamphlet, and a short story. Essays have been sub-classified as formal and informal: formal essays are characterized by "serious purpose, dignity, logical organization, length," whereas the informal essay is characterized by "the personal element (self-revelation, individual tastes and experiences, confidential manner), humor, graceful style, rambling structure, unconventionality or novelty of theme," etc.

22. Ano po yung Meaning ng Metaphor explain po sa madaling at paraan tagalog po sana


Metaphor is similar to Simile, nagkukumpara lang ng dalawang bagay (usually, yung isa sa kinukumpara is figurative ang use nya sa sentence). Ang pagkakaiba nga lang ng dalawa is that Simile uses mga palatandaan tulad ng "like" or "as" (in tagalog, maaaring "parang", "tila", "gaya", "tulad ng" at marami pang iba (mas marami kasi sa filipino kaya unti nalang yung binigay kong example).

Halimbawa:

This candy store is like heaven! - Simile

This candy store is heaven! - Metaphor

Answer:

Ang isang suspensyon ay isang magkakaiba-halo na timpla kung saan ang mga solute-tulad ng mga maliit na butil ay tumira sa labas ng isang tulad ng solvent phase ilang oras pagkatapos ng kanilang pagpapakilala.

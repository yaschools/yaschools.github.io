15. The audience thinks 30\%30% of Brandon's jokes are funny. What fraction of Brandon's jokes does the audience think are funny?


Answer:

Fraction

Everyone from the audience thinks that Brandon's jokes are funny.

Step-by-step explanation:

To arrive at the answer, express 30% /30% as a fraction. Such that 30%/ 30% is equivalent to 30/100/30/100 provided that 30% is equivalent to 30/100 in fractions. Then simplify 30/100/30/100, remember that in simplifying fractions, the numerator should be divided by its denominator. Thus, 30/100/30/100 is equal to 30/100 x 100/30 (inverse of 30/100). The answer will be 1 which means that everyone in the audience thinks that Brandon's jokes are funny.



16. The audience thinks 30% of Brandon's jokes are funny. What fraction of Brandon's jokes does the audience think are funny?


Answer:

The audience found [tex]\frac{3}{10}[/tex] of Bradon's jokes funny.

Step-by-step explanation:

1.) We must know that percent means out of a hundred. So by this, we can divide 30 by 100.

[tex]\frac{30}{100}[/tex]

2.) We cannot divide this as the numerator is higher than the denominator, but we do know for a fact that we can SIMPLIFY the fraction. Since the largest factor of 30 and 100 is 10, we must divide both the numerator and the denominator by 10.

[tex]\frac{30/10}{100/10} =\frac{3}{10}[/tex]

The government project War on Drugs of the Duterte administration with the Philippine National Police (PNP) which is Oplan Tokhang which means “knock and plead” gives the Filipinos the advantages for a drug-free country; it reduces the drug users, sellers and drug traffickers; the future of the drug users once they surrender and stop using drugs would change and once they would follow the program of the government, they could start on their own and work decent jobs and contribute positively to the country’s economy; it helps to trace corrupt political leaders or those that sneak and deal illegal drugs; and it reduces the crime rate of the country.

Answer:

The phrase “be fruitful” means be productive, the word “multiply” means to increase in number, “replenish” means to fill or refill, “subdue” means to control and to “have dominion over” means to have authority over. Therefore the meaning of Genesis 1:28 is that humans, starting with Adam and Eve, should live productive lives which includes using the resources of the earth. Humans are also instructed to populate the earth, to reproduce. Mankind is told to fill the earth with their productions as well as with themselves. During the same time making sure the earth is replenished with what has been put to use by man. Humanity is to control the entire planet, its resources and land and whatever is influenced by its natural cycles and seasons. Finally, people of the earth are given the commission to have authority over the creatures of the earth. Humans are caretakers of their home planet.

28. 9. Set of tunes in Peking Opera used to express a subdued mood and deep thought. C. Xipi A. Wuchang B. Wenchang D. Erhuang


Answer:

C. Xipi

Explanation:

When expressing an excited mood, such as happiness, anger, or agitation, the tunes called 'Xipi' are used. When expressing a subdued mood and deep thought, such as a loss, sorrow, or melancholy, the tunes called 'Erhuang' are employed.

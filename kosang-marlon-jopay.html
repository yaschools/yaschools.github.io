W = NRT

W = 1 piece of work

N = 9 men

R = unknown

T = 6 days

\begin{gathered} \\ \end{gathered}

Solving for the Rate:

W = NRT

1 = 54R

1/54 = R

Given that:

N = 12 men

R = 1/54

T = unknown

1 = 12/54T

1 ÷ 12/54 = T

1 × 54/12 = T

1 × 4.5 = T

4.5 = T

5. Tuition fee is increased by 25% since five years. If the tuition fee then was ₱5 000, how much is the tuition fee now?


Answer: ₱6,250


Step-by-step explanation:

To get the answer,

First, you multiply it by 0.25.

It becomes:

5,000 × 0.25 = 1,250.

But, since the tuition is increased by 25%, we add 5000 to 1250.

5000 + 1250 = 6250.

Therefore, tuition today costs ₱6,250.

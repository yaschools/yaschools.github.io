9. what are the Ingredients of Pan de Negro?​


Answer:

Pan de Muertos Recipe

Enrich your Day of the Dead celebration with this rich buttery pastry. Although this is a common recipe in most parts of Mexico on this day, you can add a twist to your Pan de Muertos recipe.

Prep Time: 20

Total time: 65 minutes

Ingredients

One quart cup of margarine

1/4 cup milk

1/4 cup warm water

3 cups all-purpose flour

1 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 teaspoons anise seed

1/4 cup white sugar

2 eggs, beaten

2 teaspoons orange zest

1/4 cup white sugar

1/4 cup orange juice

1 tablespoon orange zest

Instructions

Melt the butter and milk in a saucepan over medium heat. Remove from heat and add warm water.

Combine the flour, yeast, anise, salt, and sugar in a large bowl. Beat in the warm butter-milk mixture. Add eggs and orange zest until well combined.

Place the dough on a lightly flour coated surface and knead until smooth.

Let it dough rise by covering it in a covered grease bowl.

After 2 hours, place it on a baking sheet and let it double in size.

Bake for 45 minutes in a preheated oven set at 450 degrees Fahrenheit.

For the glaze, heat one-quart cup of sugar, orange zest, and orange juice until it thickens. Brush over warm bread

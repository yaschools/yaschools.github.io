6. Is it possible for a human being to be heartless and tender at the same time?


Answer:

People are often let down by those around them. It is not uncommon that a person might be so overwhelmed by betrayal, disappointment, or other negative emotions that they would rather feel no emotion at all. While being truly emotionless isn’t possible for most healthy people, you can sometimes benefit by appearing to be heartless in certain situations. If you detach yourself emotionally, avoid being too friendly, and put yourself first, people are less likely to take advantage of you or hurt you for their own gain.

An alarm clock is a type of clock used to alert a person or group of people at a certain time. These clocks' main purpose is to wake people up from a night's sleep or a little nap; they are occasionally used for other reminders as well. The majority use sound; a few also employ light or vibration.

Certain gadgets have sensors that can determine whether a person is in a light stage of sleep, in order to prevent disturbing someone who is sound asleep, which can lead to weariness even though the person has obtained enough sleep. The sound or light of the clock can be turned off by depressing a button or handle; most clocks turn off the alarm when left unattended for a certain period of time. On a conventional analog alarm clock, the time at which the alarm will sound is indicated by an additional hand or an inset dial. Alarm clocks come as standard equipment in computers, watches, and cellphones.

Alarm clocks frequently come with clock radios, which are radio receivers that may be set to start playing music at specified times. Some alarm clocks allow you to set several alarms. A progressive alarm clock has multiple alarms for different times and can play the user's preferred music. Most modern televisions, computers, cell phones, and smart watches have alarm capabilities that play notifications or trigger at a certain time.

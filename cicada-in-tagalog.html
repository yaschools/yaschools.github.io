3. does cicada undergo complete metamorphosis​


Answer:

There is no pupal stage -- cicadas undergo incomplete metamorphosis. When the nymph is fully grown and the temperature is optimal, the nymph tunnels to the surface and goes through its final molt (shedding its hard outer skin) and emerges as a winged adult

27. 12. Which of the following animals undergo complete metamorphosis?A. CicadaC. CockroachB. MosquitoD. Crickets​


Answer:

(A.) Cicada

Explanation:

The cicada pulses as the tender, green imago emerges from the hard, brown exoskeleton of the last instar nymph. Once the thorax emerges, it does this amazing backward bend and begins to unfold its wings.

B. Mosquito

Mosquitoes undergo complete metamorphosis; they go through four distinct stages of development during a lifetime. The four stages are egg, pupa, larva, and adult. The full life-cycle of a mosquito takes about a month.

Answer:

Ann is 13.33 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

The sum of Ann’s age and Rom’s age is 40.

A + R = 40

Ann was twice as old as Rom.

2A = R

Find Ann’s age.

A + R = 40

A + 2A = 40

3A = 40

A = 40/3

A = 13.33

2. This ritual is performed by female baylans by shuffling around in circles, moving forwards and backwards, spinning and shaking as if in a trance.


It's an ethnic group dance in Davao called Anito Baylan. This is a ritual dance performed in belief the best way to heal a person's sickness. Aside from dancing , they also offer a chicken as a sacrifice for this ritual. This is perfomed by a male spiritual leader and a female who supervises the healing ritual.

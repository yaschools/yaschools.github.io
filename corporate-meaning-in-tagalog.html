12. What is the meaning of net assets of a corporation?


The net worth or net assets of the company is a profit that is fully owned, which is obtained with a completed calculation and there is no dependent burden that must be issued.

Assets or net assets in accounting are commonly referred to as net worth. This is useful as the best way of measuring various kinds of company developments, including along with accounting calculations in the depreciation of business fixed assets.

 

Factors Affecting the Company's Net Assets

Net assets are influenced by several external factors, some of which are quite strong, so that sometimes their influence is beyond the control of the company's management. Some of the strongest factors are inflation, exports and imports, and interest rates.

-Inflation has a strong influence on the net worth of companies related to foreign countries and interest rates. The impact of inflation will be felt when the circulation of money in the community becomes so great that people have a higher willingness to sacrifice to get something.

-Exports and imports are business activities that greatly affect companies, apart from inflation. n addition, interest rates also have an influence on the nominal valuation of the company's net worth.

26. corporation meaning​


A corporation is an organization—usually a group of people or a company—authorized by the state to act as a single entity (a legal entity recognized by private and public law 'born out of statute"; a legal person in legal context) and recognized as such in law for certain purposes.

It means a company or group of people authorized to act as a single entity (legally a person) and recognized as such in law.

27. Ano ang multinational corporation tagalog


Ang isang multinational corporation ay isang kompanya na may kontrol sa paggawa at produksyon ng mga produkto o serbisyo sa ibang mga bansa, maliban pa sa kanyang sariling bansa. Ilang halimbawa nito ay KFC, McDonalds, Concentrix, Nestle, DOLE at iba pa. Ang mga corporation na ito ay may isang central na opisina, o corporate office, na syang may kontrol sa operasyon ng mga branches nito sa iba-ibang mga bansa. Ayon naman sa Black's Law Dictionary, para masabing multinational corporation ang isang kumpanya, dapat ay may 25% ng kita na nagmumula sa mga branches o sangay nito sa ibang bansa.  Pero pwede din na may majority shares ito o 51% na shares ng isang kumpanya.  

Malaking tulong ang nagagawa ng mga multinational corporation sa ekonomiya ng Pilipinas dahil sa tax na binabayaran nila. Sila din ay nagbibigay ng trabaho sa mga local na manggagawa.

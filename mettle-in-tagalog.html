2. Problem # 3: Monkey-Eating Eagle HuntA man plans to go hunting the Philippine monkey-eating eaglebelieving that it is a proof of his mettle. What type of error is this? Explainyour answer.​


Answer:

This is a type II error, where a false Ho is accepted.

Explanation:

The man on the problem believes on himself that monkey-eating eagle exists. He accepted to himself a false belief or information even though it is obvious that there is no such thing as "monkey-eating eagle."

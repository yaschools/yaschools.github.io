Escalation of commitment. It happens when we can’t walk away from something that’s failing because of all of the resources we have invested (e.g. time, money, etc.) in that something. It can happen in any aspect of our lives, including our relationships. The list can go on. It doesn’t only happen with the extreme of breaking up, but it can also be to the extreme of getting married. 18. An escalator has an angle of elevation of 10 degrees and a vertical rise of 6 m. find the length of the escalator.


Answer:

The length of escalator is 35 meters.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Vertical rise of elevator: 6 meters  (opposite of the angle)Angle of elevation: 10°Length of escalator: x  (hypotenuse)The length and vertical rise of elevator and the distance between its foot and base of vertical rise along the ground form a right triangle.

Use trigonometric functions to solve for x, the length of elevator:

Sin θ = opposite/hypotenuse

Find the length of elevator, x:

Sin 10° = 6 meters/x

x = (6 meters) / (Sin 10°)

x = (6 meters) / (0.1736)

x ≈ 34.56 meters

x ≈ 35 meters

The length of escalator is 35 meters.

5. division fraction tutor​


Tutorial:

Dividing a fraction has 2 method.

1) Using Reciprocal

Steps:

Turn the one fraction on its reciprocal then multiply.

Example:

3/4÷4/5

=3/4×5/4

=15/16

2) Using Cross-Multiply

Example:

3/4÷4/5

=3×5 (the numerator of 3/4 and the denominator of 4/5) over 4×4 (the denominator of 3/4 and the numerator of the 4/5)

=15/16

Answer:115 Hours

Step-by-step explanation:

3,187.50:8.5=43,125.00:n

multiply 3,187.50 by n and 8.5 to 43,125.00

3187.50n=366,562.5

divide 366,562.5 by 3187.50 to get n

n=115

Conclusion: The tutor needs to do 115 hours of tutoring to earn P43,125.00

24. Paragraph about 'On Graduation Day' using context clues; synonym and antonym.1 sentence for introduction8 sentence for body1 sentence for conclusion ​


Answer:

Graduation Day is a time of celebration and reflection, the end of one chapter and the start of another. As young adults venture out into the real world, they take with them the lessons and experiences acquired at their alma mater. Graduation Day is a milestone that marks the culmination of years of hard work and sacrifice. It is a day for parents, teachers, and mentors to express their pride and delight in witnessing the young people they have helped nurture and mold achieve this special moment. It is a day for the young people themselves to be recognized for the hard work they have put into their education, and to receive their diplomas and degrees. This is a day when families and friends gather to celebrate and cherish this accomplishment. This is a day for memories to be made and memories to be kept. This is Graduation Day.

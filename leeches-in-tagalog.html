17. compare the reproduction of leech and tape worms


Earthworms are hermaphrodites and have two pairs of testes surrounded by testes sac, ovaries and ovipores. During copulation sperms are exchanged between two worms and stored. Reproduction takes place after copulation.

Leeches are also hermaphrodites with both male and female reproductive organs. Reproduction occurs after copulation and is followed by cocoon formation in which the embryos develop.

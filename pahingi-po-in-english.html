26. Hi, pahingi naman ng natutunan/reflection nyo sa math. English po Sana. G10 math po​


Answer:

Algebra:

Algebra is a fundamental branch of mathematics that deals with the study of mathematical symbols and the rules for manipulating these symbols. In this topic i learn about linear equations, quadratic equations, and systems of equations. Linear equations involve the study of straight lines and their equations, while quadratic equations involve the study of parabolic curves and their equations. Systems of equations involve the study of multiple equations that need to be solved simultaneously.

Geometry:

Geometry is the study of shapes, sizes, and positions of objects in space. In this topic , i learn about angles, lines, circles, polygons, and three-dimensional shapes. I also learn about geometric proofs, which involve using logical reasoning to prove theorems and solve problems.

Trigonometry:

Trigonometry is the study of the relationships between the sides and angles of triangles. In this topic i typically learn about trigonometric functions, inverse trigonometric functions, and the unit circle. Trigonometry is an essential tool for solving problems in many fields, such as engineering, physics, and navigation.

Calculus:

Calculus is the study of rates of change and their applications. For this topic i learn about limits, derivatives, integrals, and their applications in areas such as physics, engineering, and economics. Calculus is an advanced branch of mathematics that provides a powerful tool for modeling and analyzing complex systems.

In conclusion, each of these topics is essential for developing a strong foundation in mathematics.

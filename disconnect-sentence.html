1. sentence of disconnect​


Answer:

Examples of disconnect in a Sentence

The alarm system had been disconnected. Before starting, be sure to disconnect the power supply. Our landlord threatened to disconnect our electricity. We were talking on the phone but suddenly we got disconnected.

Explanation:

Disconnect also means a lack of understanding or connection. So there might be a disconnect between you and your brother, or between what a politician promises to do and what she actually does. Disconnect combines dis-, "the opposite of" with connect, from the Latin conectere, "join together."

26. why are friends are disconnected?​


Answer:

Friends are disconnected for a variety of reasons. This can be a change of location; change of status; arguments or misunderstanding, change of seasons, lack of medium for communication.

Explanation:

As we grow older, we get disconnected with our friends and eventually change our set of friends. One of the reasons why we get disconnected with our friends is when there is a change of location. Sometimes, we move to a new house, transfer to another school or change a job so we move to another company, and thus, we lost our connection to old friends and eventually make new friends in our new environment

The second factor is a change of status. It is usually a fact that "birds with the same feathers, flock together", thus singles hang out with singles and married couples hang out with married people too. This is because once a person change her or his status, his or her responsibilities also become greater and would love to talk more about it to those with the same situation as her or him.

Third reason why friends drift away is argument or misunderstanding. When friends fight because of a person or a situation, they would rather become silent, pull back and either build a wall between their friendship or burn bridges. We sometimes call this a "cold war". They become silent and never talk with each other again.

Fourth factor is the change of season. Teenagers who love fun and excitement go with easy going people but when they become mature, they would go with people who are independent, responsible, diligent and goal-driven. It is really true when you say, "If you want to become a millionaire, then hang out with businessmen or millionaires".

Lastly, friends drift apart because of lack of medium of communication. For example, you have a friend who gave you her phone and email address but when she changed number and email address then you could not contact her anymore. It is a good thing though that facebook was invented, in that way, we can search for our long lost friend and reconnect.

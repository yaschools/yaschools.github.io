29. A thug pushes some 100 kg punk with 300 N of force. How much is the punk accelerated?


Answer:

3 m/s^2

Explanation:

m=100 kg

F=300 N or 300 kg m/s^2

Given the formula for force,F=ma

a=F/m

=(300 kg m/s^2)/100 kg

a=3 m/s^2

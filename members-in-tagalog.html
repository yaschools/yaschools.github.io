8. aActivity 4. Tagalog Folksongs! Directions. Are you familiar with Tagalog folksongs like "Bahay Kubo", "Leron-Leron Sinta", "Sitsiritsit", "Magtanim ay Di Biro", and "Paruparong Bukid"? If not, ask a family member to sing the songs for you. Write the message of each song in the space be- low. Bahay Kubo​


Answer:

Explanation:

ALL songs are Tagalog folksongs.

BAHAY KUBO tells of the typical set up of a nipa hut at the middle of the field surrounded by a garden filled with various vegetable plants.

LERON LERON SINTA is a work song which depicts the activity of harvesting fruits such as the papaya with "Leron" and tamarind with Neneng". The breaking of the branch in the picking of papaya by Leron is regarded as a bad luck by the suitor, and so the suitor decides to find for another lady to court. Neneng in the second stanza is told by her companion to hold lest she will fall off the swaying tamarind branch.

SITSIRITSIT is a folksong about a flirtatious woman who told a store owner that the ant will devour her if she won't give her a credit for cookies. Also it tells of the exchange of the baby for a doll or "bagoong" or fish paste at Malabon.

MAGTANIM AY DI BIRO , Planting Rice is Never Fun reflects the positive hardworking trait of the Filipino rice farmers who endure the whole-day labor with bent position under

the sun and with no tine to sit or stand. Then it also tells the rice farmer to his listener to continue on with the hard work hopeful for a brighter future.

PARUPARONG BUKID is what a lady in the 1900 is likened to - a field butterfly, wearing a glamorous dress with a tall butterfly sleeves with an ornamental comb and a who sways and moves her hips at the middle of the church aisle.

20. ano sa tagalog yung what can you do to help that family member as well​


Pagtulong sa mga miyembro ng pamilya

Ang pagtulong sa mga miyembro ng pamilya ay maaaring gawin sa maraming paraan. Ang pagtulong sa mga miyembro ng pamilya ay maaaring gawin sa pamamagitan ng pagtulong sa mga gawaing bahay at pagsasagawa ng ating tungkulin bilang mga miyembro ng pamilya, lalo na bilang isang bata. Makakatulong tayo sa mga magulang sa paglilinis ng bahay, halimbawa, paghuhugas ng pinggan, pagwawalis ng sahig, paglilinis ng bakuran, at marami pang iba. Ang pagiging masanay sa pagtulong sa paglilinis ng bahay ay nagsasanay din sa ating pagkatao na magustuhan ang kalinisan.

Ang isang halimbawa ng pagtulong sa ibang miyembro ng pamilya ay kung ang isang miyembro ng pamilya ay may sakit, dapat natin silang alagaan at samahan. Dapat din nating subukang hikayatin at aliwin ang mga miyembro ng pamilya na may sakit, upang sila ay maging masaya at magkaroon ng mataas na pag-asa na gumaling.

Ang isa pang bagay sa pagtulong sa mga miyembro ng pamilya ay ang pagtulong sa mga miyembro ng pamilya na may problema. Ito ay kinakailangan para sa amin bilang mga miyembro ng pamilya. Bilang miyembro ng pamilya kung may problema ang isa sa aking pamilya, magbibigay ako ng tulong upang mabilis na malutas ang problema. Ang magagawa ko para matulungan ang mga miyembro ng pamilya ay magbigay ng mga solusyon at solusyon sa mga problemang kasalukuyang nararanasan.

To explain the organization of projects within your Troops/Outfit to other members, you could follow these steps:

1. Start with a brief introduction of the project and its objectives. This will help everyone understand the context of the project and why it is important.

2. Explain the structure of the project team and the roles and responsibilities of each team member. This will help clarify who is responsible for what and how the team will work together.

3. Discuss the timeline and milestones of the project. This will help everyone understand the schedule and what needs to be accomplished at each stage.

4. Describe the resources needed for the project, such as equipment, funding, and personnel. This will help everyone understand what is required to complete the project successfully.

5. Finally, outline the communication plan for the project, including how progress will be reported and how team members will share information and collaborate.

By following these steps, you can help ensure that everyone in your Troops/Outfit understands the organization of the project and their role in its success.

1. Define the purpose of the organization: Explain the main goal or mission of the organization and how it relates to the Troops/Outfit projects. This can help members understand the significance and relevance of the organization.

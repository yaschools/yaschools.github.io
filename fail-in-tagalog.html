Answer:

I agree with this statement. Failing to plan means not taking the time and effort to think about the steps and actions that are necessary to achieve a goal. Without a plan, it is difficult to know what needs to be done, when it needs to be done, and how it needs to be done. As a result, it is likely that important tasks and deadlines will be missed, leading to a failure to achieve the desired outcome. On the other hand, planning involves setting specific goals, identifying the steps needed to achieve those goals, and organizing and prioritizing those steps in a way that maximizes the chances of success.

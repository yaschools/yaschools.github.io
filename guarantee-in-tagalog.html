4. Conditions that guarantee a quadrilateral is a parallelogram


There are different conditions guarantee that a quadrilateral is a parallelogram. If these conditions are met, it guarantees that a quadrilateral is a parallelogram.


- If both pairs of opposite sides of a quadrilateral are parallel, then it’s a parallelogram


- If both pairs of opposite sides of a quadrilateral are congruent, then it’s a parallelogram


- If both pairs of opposite angles of a quadrilateral are congruent, then it’s a parallelogram

This can include using explicit or vulgar language, making inappropriate comments or gestures, or engaging in any other behavior that is considered disrespectful or inappropriate. In the context of MAPEH, it is important for students to understand and respect the boundaries of appropriate behavior in order to create a safe and respectful learning environment for all.

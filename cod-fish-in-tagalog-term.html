7. Which of the following fishes is low in fat?a. cod b. salmon c. trout d. tuna


Subject TLE

Which of the following fishes is low in fat?

a. cod

b. salmon

c. trout  

d. tuna

Answer

The correct answer among the choices is letter a. Cod is a type of fish that has the lowest total fat content. There are different kind of salmon but all of them has higher fat content than cod. Trout fish is considered to have a low-fat or medium-fat content but still has higher fat content than cod. Tuna fish also has a very low-fat content but cod has the very lowest total fat content even considering the saturated fat, polyunsaturated fat and monounsaturated fat content. Cod has also low calorie and cholesterol content.Most kind of fish are considered to have low-fat and low-calorie than meat and poultry.  

2 Classification of Fish in terms of Fat Content Low-fatHigh-fat

Examples of Low-fat Fish Low Fat -Tilapia, Halibut, Mussels, Ocean perch, Oysters, Pacific rock-fish, Salmon  Very Low Fat - Crab, Clams, Cod, Flounder/sole, Haddock, Hake, Lobster, Mahi-mahi, Pollock, Scallops, Shrimp, Tuna

Examples of High-fat Fish High-fat  - Herring, Mackerel, Sardines, Salmon  Medium-fat - Bluefish, Catfish, Rainbow trout, Swordfish

If one scanner is taken from each box, what is the probability that the scanners from the first and second companies are defective and the scanners from the third and fourth companies are not?​


Answer:

For the first company,there are 8 defective scanners P(defective)=8/50=4/25

For the 2nd company,there are 4 defective

scanners P(defective)=4/50=2/25

For the 3rd company,there are 3 defective scanners P(not defective)=50-8/50=47/50

For the 4th company,there are 5 defective scanners P(not defective)=50-5/50=45/50=9/10

9. make a reaction paper about "HUNTER X HUNTER"​


Answer:

A reaction paper is a type of academic writing that requires you to analyze and evaluate a text, film, or other media and express your personal opinion about it. ⁴⁵

To write a good reaction paper, you need to follow these steps:

- Read or watch the assigned material carefully and take notes of your initial impressions, feelings, thoughts, and questions.

- Identify the main purpose, theme, message, and argument of the material. How does it relate to the course topic or objectives?

- Decide whether you agree or disagree with the author's or creator's perspective. Why? What evidence or examples can you use to support your position?

- Make a reaction paper outline. The outline is the backbone of your content, which will serve as your compass during the actual writing process.

- Compose the first draft. Use your outline to form the first draft of your work. Start with an introduction that summarizes the material and states your thesis statement. Then, write several paragraphs that explain and analyze your reaction, using specific details and examples from the material. Finally, write a conclusion that restates your main points and gives a final evaluation of the material.

- Revise and edit your draft. Check your draft for clarity, coherence, grammar, spelling, punctuation, and formatting. Make sure you follow the citation style required by your instructor (e.g., APA, MLA, Chicago). You can also ask someone else to proofread your paper and give you feedback.

I hope this guide helps you write a great reaction paper.

22. what makes the Pinoy Christmas different/unique from the rest of the world? ​


Answer:

Filipinos welcome the season uniquely with different flavors and traditions that make Christmas extra special. ... The Filipino's strong sense of family and preserving ties make Christmas the perfect time to share love and blessings with each other. Christmas in the Philippines is all about families.

Christmas customs in the Philippines are a mixture of western and native Filipino traditions. ... Many people stay awake all night into Christmas day! During Christmas Eve evening, Christians go to church to hear the last 'simbang gabi' or the Christmas Eve mass. This is followed by a midnight feast, called Noche Buena.

6. If ∠JAL and ∠LAY form a linear pair and m∠JAL = 84o, then which is the m∠LAY?


Answer:

m∠LAY = 96 degrees

Step-by-step explanation:

The sum of the measurements of a linear pair is equal to 180 degrees. So if one angle is equal to 84 degrees, then the other angle must be 96 degrees.

180 = x + y
180 = 84 + y
180 - 84 = y

y = 96 degrees

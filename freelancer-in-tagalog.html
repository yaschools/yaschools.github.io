22. If you could give someone new to freelancing advice on managing expectations with a client so that everyone is pleased with the experience and outcome, what would it be?*


As a freelancer, it is important to set clear expectations with your clients from the outset of a project. This can help ensure that both parties are on the same page and can help prevent misunderstandings or disappointments later on. Here are a few tips for managing expectations with clients:

1. Communicate clearly: Make sure to clearly communicate your expectations and the scope of the project to your client. This may include setting deadlines, outlining the deliverables, and discussing any potential roadblocks or challenges that may arise.

2. Be transparent: Be open and transparent with your clients about your availability, your process, and any potential challenges you may face. This can help build trust and establish a positive working relationship.

3. Be responsive: It is important to be responsive to your clients' needs and concerns. If a client has a question or concern, make sure to address it in a timely manner.

4. Set boundaries: It is important to set boundaries with your clients to ensure that you are able to work effectively and maintain a healthy work-life balance. This may include setting limits on the number of hours you are available to work, or establishing clear guidelines for communication.

5. Manage your time effectively: Time management is key to meeting deadlines and delivering high-quality work. Make sure to set aside time for each project and prioritize your tasks to ensure that everything is completed on time.

By following these tips, you can help manage expectations with your clients and ensure that everyone is pleased with the experience and outcome of the project.

30. A suitcase measures 24 inches long and the diagonal is 30 inches long. How much material is needed to cover one side of the suitcase?


Answer:

540 square inches

Step by step explanation:

What is asked? the amount of material needed to cover one side of the suitcase

What are given? 24 inches - length of the suitcase30 inches - diagonal

How to solve? The problem is asking the amount needed to cover one side of the suitcase which is a hint that we should find the area to be covered. Using the formula:

A= length x width

This is the formula for finding the area of the rectangle. But the width is unknown. We should be able to solve it using the diagonal length. With the formula:

d^2=L^2 x W^2

d is the diagonal, L is length and W is width.

Solution 1. (finding width)

d^2= L^2 + W^2

30 ^2 = 24^2 + W^2

900 = 576 +W^2

W^2= 900-576

W^2 = 324

W= 18

Therefore the width is 18 inches.

Solution 2. (finding area)

A= L x W

A= 30 inches x 18 inches

A= 540 square inches

Therefore, the material needed to cover one side is 540 square inches.

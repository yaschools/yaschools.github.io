1. Example po ng panalangin [5-10 sentences]


Answer:

1. Deal of the day

O my lady! Oh my goodness!

I dedicate myself completely to you; and as proof of my love for the child I commit to you this day my eyes, my ears, my tongue, my heart; in a word, my whole being. And since I am already yours, oh Mother of goodness, keep me and defend me as your object and property until the end of my life. Amen.

2. Morning prayer

Blessed Father Almighty, I thank you for this new day, because with the rising of the sun, my awakening and my journey here, I have the opportunity to be closer to you, to be a better servant than yesterday .

I thank you for the family where you put me, for my friends who guide me for good and everything that leads the way to you, which represents something positive in my life. Glorify by your Holy Spirit, Lord, each of my steps, so that it may be an example of your good heart to all I find on the path.

You will glorify through your Holy Spirit, Lord, my tongue, my lips and my voice, so that they will be the defender of your word and its deliverer. Melt your holy blood on my hands, Lord, may they be filled with your holy obedience, that my work may be blessed.

May your joy touch my heart, and may it become a universal chain to know that I am your faithful servant, and in this way be an instrument of your divine peace. I give into your hands all that I am now and what I will be, that you may shape me in your image and will, in such a way that I am like you, for the good of your people, and that your name may be glorified in every place.let it pass

I ask this in the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit. Amen.

3. Prayer at the Beginning of the Day

Lord God almighty, I thank you for this new day, because in this new day I have the opportunity to be close to you, and serve you better than yesterday.

I thank you for my family, my friends and for all the things you have put in front of me for my good. Sanctify, Lord, through your Holy Spirit, in every step I take, so that through them I can show your glory and power to those I find along the way.

Lord Jesus Christ bless my lips so that they may testify to your mercy and love; Lord Jesus Christ anoint my hands with the perfume of your Divine Obedience to the Law, that they may bless my work; open my eyes so they can see your splendor and thus make you a light that gives clarity to all the decisions I make today.

By your grace let my heart rejoice in such a way that the whole universe knows that I am your servant, and thus humbly serve as an instrument of your Divine Peace. I give you my heart, my mind and my whole being so that you can change them in Your Image, so that I can be more like you for the good of your people, and for the glory of your Holy One that Name.

We ask this in the Holy Name of Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.

4. Prayer for Today

Dear Heavenly Father, grant us your Spirit here on earth to be united with Christ Jesus, the Savior. May the truth shine upon us with light, bringing joy no matter what happens to us.

May all the pain in our life be the pain of the birth of a new life and may we rejoice as the people you created, people prepared to fight on earth, called to battle and lead to victory.

Grant that the darkness that surrounds us does not prevent us from seeing. Give bright light to the new life to come. May we recognize that this has already happened with the coming of Jesus Christ who is still here on earth, and through him, the Savior, we will see what is yet to come.

O God of wonders, keep us alert to the wonders that surround us and increase even more, until all the diseases on earth are finally overcome and we people glorify your love and your great goodness. Amen.

5. Week of thanksgiving prayer

Lord, this Sunday, the day of rest that You sanctified, I want to thank you again because you are always by my side, for the faith you gave me so that, in difficult times, I can walk on the path of truth firm in your love..

Thank you my God for giving me a week full of blessings and for helping me understand that the trials that come to me are just to make me strong.

Thank you for my health, for my family, for my job, for my home, for my finances, thank you Lord for allowing me to love you more every day.

Create in me a heart full of kindness, humility and justice to always act in honor of you. I ask you Lord. Amen.

Answer:

Panalangin na may layunin na pasalamatan ang Diyos

Dahilan: para sakin ito ang pinakamahalagang parte ng ating panalangin dahil dito nakikita ang pagpapahalaga mo sa lahat ng biyaya na ipinagkaloob sayo ng diyos.

Panginoon, ako'y nagagalak at nagpapasalamat sa lahat ng biyayang iyong ipinagkaloob maging sa araw araw na buhay na iyong ibinibigay. ipinagpapasalamat ko rin po ang bawat kaalaman na inyong ipinababatid sa akin. maraming salamat din po sa paggabay sa lahat ng aking gagawin at salamat din po sa patuloy na pagproprotekta sa akin mula sa kasamaan at kapahakan. Maraming salamat din po sa araw araw kong paggising at pagbibigay sakin ng bagong pag asa. lubos po akong nagpapasalamat sa walang hanggan niyong pagmamahal sa akin sa kabila ng aking kahinaan at kasalanan na nagagawa. Ako po'y labis na napapasalamat sa mga taong nariyan at mga taong darating na siyang ibinigay mo po upang ako'y suportahan, mahalin at mag iingat sa akin. Ang lahat po ng ito ay aking pinasasalamatan sa dakilang pangalan ni Hesus. Amen.

28. Pambungad na panalangin?


Answer:

Pambungad na Panalangin

Diyos Amang makapangyarihan sa lahat, pinupuri po namin ang Iyong maluwalhating pangalan. Taos-puso po kaming nagpapasalamat sawalang sawa Ninyong paggabay at pagprotekta sa amin. Maraming salamatpo sa aming pamilya, mga kaibigan, at mga guro na nagsisilbi namingkasiyahan at inspirasyon. Lubos din po naming ikinagagalak ang biyaya ngedukasyon kung saan kami ay nahahasa sa lahat ng aspeto. Humihingi pokami ng kapatawaran sa lahat ng aming pagkukulang at pagkakamali.Patnubayan Niyo po kami upang mapagtagumpayan ang mga pagsubokbilang mga mag-aaral. Gawaran Niyo rin po kami ng matalas na paningin,pandinig, at pag-iisip upang maunawaan ang mga aralin. Gabayan Niyo posana ang aming guro nang sa gayon ay maipaliwanag niya ng maayos angmga impormasyong aming kailangan. Nawa’y ilayo Niyo po kami at ang mgamahal namin sa buhay sa anumang kapahamakan. Ito po ang aming dalanginsa ngalan ng Iyong anak na si Hesus,

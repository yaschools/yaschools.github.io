Kasi siya ay tumotulong sa nangangailangan at pawang tama Lang ang kanyang tinotulungan

Answer:

Edit

Captain Barbell is a fictional Filipino comics superhero created by writer, Mars Ravelo and artist Jim Fernandez. His characteristics are similar to American fictional superheroes Superman, DC Comics' Captain Marvel, and Thor but Ravelo based his backstory on Captain America.[1] He first appeared in Pinoy Komiks #5 on May 23, 1963. Like Darna, he also had an alter-ego named Tenteng, a thin, weakling and asthmatic person whose only dream was to become strong and muscular. He also appeared in Kampeon Komiks.[2][3]

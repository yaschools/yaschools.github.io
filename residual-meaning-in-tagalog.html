7. Find the meaning of the following words. Resilience, Retrofitting, Residual risk, Risk transfer, Rehabilitation ,Reconstruction, structural and non-structural measures, Underlying disaster risk drivers, Vulnerability​


Answer:

Resilience - the ability of a system, community, or individual to recover quickly from difficulties, or to efficiently adjust to changing circumstances.

Retrofitting - the process of modifying an existing structure to improve its performance, often to meet new safety or building codes, or to improve energy efficiency.

Residual risk - the risk that remains after all mitigation measures have been implemented.

Risk transfer - the transfer of risk from one party to another, through methods such as insurance or risk management strategies.

Rehabilitation - the process of restoring or improving a building or structure that has been damaged or is in disrepair.

Reconstruction - the process of rebuilding a damaged or destroyed building or structure, often with the aim of restoring it to its original condition.

Structural and non-structural measures - measures taken to reduce the risk of disasters that are related to the physical structure of a building, or to the people and systems within it. Structural measures include improvements to the building structure, while non-structural measures may include disaster preparedness training, early warning systems, and evacuation plans.

Underlying disaster risk drivers - factors that contribute to the vulnerability of communities or systems to the impacts of disasters, such as population growth, poverty, land use changes, and climate change.

Vulnerability - the degree to which a system or community is susceptible to harm from a hazard, often related to factors such as the presence of physical or social hazards, poverty, and the ability to access resources for mitigation and recovery.

Answer:

Here is your answer!

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's solve the problem step by step:

Given:

- Residual interest = 125% of economic obligations

- Economic resources = 180% of residual interest

- Economic obligations = Php 320,000

To find:

- Total economic resources and residual interest of the business

First, let's find the residual interest:

Residual interest = 125% of economic obligations

Residual interest = 125% x Php 320,000

Residual interest = Php 400,000

Next, let's find the economic resources:

Economic resources = 180% of residual interest

Economic resources = 180% x Php 400,000

Economic resources = Php 720,000

Therefore, the total economic resources and residual interest of the business are Php 720,000 and Php 400,000, respectively, when its economic obligations is Php 320,000.

In chemistry residue is whatever remains or acts as a contaminant after a given class of events.

Residue may refer to an atom or a group of atoms that forms part of a molecule, such as a methyl group.

30. the economic resources of the business is 180% of it's residual interest and it's residual interest is 125% of it's economic obligations.How much are the total economic resources and residual interest of the business if it's economic obligations is 320,000?​


Answer:

To solve this problem, we can use algebra. Let X be the total economic resources and Y be the residual interest. We can translate the given information into equations:

X = 1.8Y (the economic resources are 180% of the residual interest)

Y = 1.25(320,000) (the residual interest is 125% of the economic obligations)

Simplifying the second equation:

Y = 400,000

Substituting Y into the first equation:

X = 1.8(400,000)

X = 720,000

Therefore, the total economic resources of the business are 720,000 and the residual interest is 400,000.

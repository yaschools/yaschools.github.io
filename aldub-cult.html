21. describe the fandom culture that the Aldub phenomenon has created


DESCRIBING THE FANDOM CULTURE THAT THE ALDUB PHENOMENON HAS CREATED

The AlDub or Alden and Yaya Dub love team undeniably rocked not only the Philippines but also the world. It became the talk in the entertainment industry of the country for a while. The AlDub love team is then considered as a phenomenal love team. The success of the AlDub love team cannot only be credited to Alden Richards and Maine Mendoza (Yaya Dub). Part of the success are the so-called fans.

The AlDub fans have established a great fandom all over the world. The fandom that was created were not only established to become a plain supporter of the love team but it was made to create an impact to the country. The fandom initiated different cause to extend help, raise awareness and propagate good values.

The fandom culture that the AlDub phenomenon has created and exemplified is truly inspiring. It inspired every Filipinos to be of purpose and the same time not forgetting to be happy. It showed the modern world the modern bayanihan spirit of the Filipinos.

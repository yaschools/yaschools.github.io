10. Is polished marble is Lustrous /Dull?


Polished marble is lustrous.

Since they are glossy, they are attractive to look at. They are commonly used for furniture tops and kitchen countertops. With those occurences, the marble is still very prone to staining and soiling, which causes its dullness at times. Therefore, it is important to have a yearly maintenance of cleaning and removing its stains to keep them shiny as new.

Cleaning them requires extra care inorder to avoid scratches on the surface. Wet the surface with hot water first before applying a mild detergent such as a dishwashing soap to thoroughly clean the stains.

13. A lustrous lace-like luxury textiles traditionally worn by filipino men ​


Answer:

Piña

Explanation:

Piña (Tagalog pronunciation: [pɪˈnja] pi-NYAH) is a traditional Philippine fiber made from the leaves of the pineapple plant. Pineapples were widely cultivated in the Philippines since the 17th century for weaving lustrous lace-like luxury textiles known as nipis fabric.

It is a Barong Tagalog

Explanation:

Barong tagalog is a formal shirt usually made of sheer lightweight but stiff fabric known as nipis (usually woven from piña or abacá fibers). When using sheer fabrics, it is worn over an undershirt known as the camisón or camiseta, which can have short or long sleeves. The term camisa de chino is also used for collar-less and cuff-less shirts, named after its resemblance to shirts worn by Chinese laborers. It is worn with belted trousers and dress shoes. Headgear, when worn, is either a salakot or a buntal hat (and historically also top hats or bowler hats). The ensemble mixes elements of both native and Spanish traditions

25. when a mineral is not shiny or lustrous what could this possibly be ?


Answer:

Dull (or earthy) minerals exhibit little to no lustre, due to coarse granulations which scatter light in all directions, approximating a Lambertian reflector. An example is kaolinite. A distinction is sometimes drawn between dull minerals and earthy minerals, with the latter being coarser, and having even less lustre.

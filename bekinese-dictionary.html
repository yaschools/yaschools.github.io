7. What is a Technical Dictionary?What is a Personal Dictionary?​


Answer:

A Technical Dictionary is a reference book that contains terms and definitions related to a particular field, such as technology, engineering, medicine, or law.

A Personal Dictionary is a reference book that contains words and definitions that are specific to an individual's personal vocabulary. It may include words and definitions related to hobbies, interests, and other topics that the individual finds important.

Explanation:

A technical dictionary is a reference work that contains definitions of technical terms and jargon related to a specific subject or field, such as engineering, medicine, computer science, or law. It is designed to help people understand technical language and concepts that may be unfamiliar or difficult to comprehend. Technical dictionaries often provide examples, illustrations, and usage notes to help readers understand how technical terms are used in context.

On the other hand, a personal dictionary is a collection of words that an individual has learned or encountered, along with their meanings and other information. It is usually created by the individual for their own reference, to help them remember and learn new words. Personal dictionaries can include words from any language, and may also include idioms, expressions, and slang. Personal dictionaries can be kept in a physical notebook or digitally in a software program, and can be organized in any way the individual chooses.

19. what kind of dictionary is the general dictionary​


Answer:

General-purpose dictionaries

Although one may speak of a “general-purpose” dictionary, it must be realized that every dictionary is compiled with a particular set of users in mind. In turn, the public has come to expect certain conventional features (see below Features and problems), and a publisher departs from the conventions at his peril. One of the chief demands is that a dictionary should be “authoritative,” but the word authoritative is ambiguous. It can refer to the quality of scholarship and the employment of the soundest information available, or it can describe a prescriptive demand for compliance to particular standards. Many people ask for arbitrary decisions in usage choices, but most linguists feel that, when a dictionary goes beyond its function of recording accurate information on the state of the language, it becomes a bad dictionary.

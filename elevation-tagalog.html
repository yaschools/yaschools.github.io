10. A building has two elevators P and Q. The time needed for elevator P to rise one floor is (3x+8) seconds. Elevator Q is (x-4) seconds faster than elevator P. How long will elevator Q to reach 3rd floor?​


Answer:  

(12x+12) seconds

Step-by-step explanation:

Elevator Q = Elevator P + (x-4)

Elevator Q = (3x+8) + (x-4)

Elevator Q = 4x+4

Now , Elevator Q will took 4x+4 seconds to reach one floor. So , if it reaches the third floor , we will just multiply it by 3

Time = 3 foors × Elevator Q

Time = 3 × (4x+4)

Time = 12x+12

Therefore , the time reaches the 3rd floor is (12x+12) seconds

15. The term for Italian operaa. OPERA b. COMIC OPERA c. OPERA BUFFA d. OPERA SERIA


Music

The term for Italian opera

a. OPERA

b. COMIC OPERA

c. OPERA BUFFA

d. OPERA SERIA

Answer

The correct answer is letter d. Opera Seria is an Italian musical term which means serious opera. It is an opera that has usually classical or mythological theme. Opera seria is also called drama per musical or drama through music. Some examples of opera seria are Hasse, Vinci, Jommelli, George Frideric Handel and Piccinni.

Definition of TermsOpera is a genre of classical music for singers and instrumentalists with dramatic compositionComic Opera is an opera displayed by humorous words and actions of the charactersOpera Buffa is an entertaining musical comedy typeOpera Seria is a serious musical opera which refers to the noble and serious style of Italian opera from the 1710s to about 1770 in Europe.

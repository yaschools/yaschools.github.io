17. a stone is dropped into a deep well and is here to hit the water 2.6 seconds after being dropped determine the deep of the well​


Answer:

the well has a depth of -33.1 meters.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Explanation:

time (t) = 2.6 s

acceleration (a) (earth's grav. force) =  -9.8 m/s^2

initial velocity (i) = 0 m/s

d = ?

d = (i ⋅ t) + (0.5 ⋅ a ⋅ t)^2

d = (0 m/s) (2.6 s) + 0.5 (-9.8 m/s^2) (2.6 s)^2

d = 0 m/s + 0.5 (-9.8 m/s^2) (6.76 s^2)

d = -33.124m  (round off)

d = -33.1m

22. A stone is dropped into a deep well,heard to hit the water 3.41s after being dropped.Determine the depth of the well


Assuming 340 m/s for the speed of sound (which varies with temperature), depth is 52 meters. 

The 3.41 seconds is the sum of two times. 

1. time for the rock to fall, and that is 
d = ½gt² or  
t = √(2d/g)  

2. time for the sound to return up the well, and that is 
t = d/340  

so the equation is 
√(2d/9.8) + d/340 = 3.41 
solve for d  
answer = 52 m

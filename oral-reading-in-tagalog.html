Reading Money Orally:

This amount is read as "one thousand four hundred fifty pesos and ninety centavos". In  reading numbers orally, you are actually spelling out the number in words including the symbols that the number contains such as the peso sign (₱), negative sign (-), decimal point (.) and others.

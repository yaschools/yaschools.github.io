10. A basket contains 8 mangoes, 6 star apples, and 10 dragon fruits. what is the probability of picking mangoes or dragon fruit


Answer:

Hence the correct answer is [tex] \frac{9}{12} [/tex]

Explanation:

Given-A basket contains 8mangoes,6 star apples,and 10 dragon fruits

To find-what is the probability mangoes and dragon fruit

Solution-total fruit=8+6+10=24

probability of picking mangoes=

[tex] \frac{8}{24} = \frac{1}{3} [/tex]

probability of picking dragon fruits=

[tex] \frac{10}{24} = \frac{5}{12} [/tex]

Add the probability =

[tex] \frac{1}{3} + \frac{5}{12} = \frac{4 + 5}{12} = \frac{9}{12} [/tex]

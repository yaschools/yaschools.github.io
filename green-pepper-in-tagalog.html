10. The chef has 32 ounces of red peppers and 8 ounces of green peppers how many pounds of peppers does he have altogether?


Answer:

To convert ounces to pounds, we need to divide the number of ounces by 16, since there are 16 ounces in 1 pound.

So, the chef has a total of (32 + 8) = 40 ounces of peppers.

To convert this to pounds, we divide by 16:

40 ounces ÷ 16 ounces/pound = 2.5 pounds

Therefore, the chef has a total of 2.5 pounds of peppers.

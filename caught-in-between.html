5. 9. The philippines is caught between two major things tectonic plates of the earth, the Pacific Plate an the _________. A. Earthquakes C. Eurasian Plate B. Plate Tectonics D. Philippine Mobile Belt


Answer:

C

Explanation:

The Philippine Mobile Belt (also called as Taiwan-Luzon-Mindoro Mobile Belt) is a complex tectonic zone which sits at the convergence zone of the Eurasian Plate, Philippine Sea Plate and Indo-Australian Plate. It covers the whole Philippine archipelago and extends southwards to the Molucca Sea and eastern Indonesia.

20. much? Miguel has 6 boxes of chalks having 100 pieces each and 7 boxes of colored chalk with 20 piece each. He shared equally these chalks to his 4 friends. How many chalks does each friend have?​


Answer: 185 pieces of chalk.

Step-by-step explanation:Miguel has a total of 6 x 100 = <<6*100=600>>600 pieces of chalk in the boxes of chalks.

He also has 7 x 20 = <<7*20=140>>140 pieces of colored chalk.

Thus, Miguel has a total of 600 + 140 = <<600+140=740>>740 pieces of chalk.

Miguel shared the chalks equally among his 4 friends. Therefore, each friend will have:

740 / 4 = <<740/4=185>>185 pieces of chalk.

PARTNERSHIP OPERATION

1

Partnership dissolution is synonymous with partnership liquidation. FALSE

2.

When a newly admitted partner pays a bonus to the existing partners, the new partner's capital

account is debited to record bonus to old partners. TRUE

3. Partner Lagrimas has a 15,000 credit balance in her capital account in the firm of Lagrimas and

Villon. If Ballado purchased Lagrimas' equity interest in the firm for 20,000, the entry on the

partnership books to record the transfer would credit Ballado's capital account. FALSE

4. The dissolution of the partnership discharges the existing liability of any partner. FALSE

5. When a new partner invests more than the proportionate share he receives in the partnership, a

bonus is recorded to his account. FALSE

6. There is no impact on the statement of comprehensive income of a partnership when a partner

withdraws from the business. TRUE

7. Total contributed capital is the sum of capital balances of the old partners and the actual

investment of the new partner. It should always equal the new partnership capital. FALSE

8. If a partner withdraws by selling his equity interest to the partnership in exchange for an amount

greater than the balance in his capital account, the excess payment will be treated as a bonus to

the continuing partners. FALSE

9. On dissolution, the partnership is not terminated but continues until winding up of partnership

affairs is completed. TRUE

10. In admission by investment, the bonus method is used when the total agreed capital is more

than the total agreed capital. FALSE

11. A partner who withdraws from a partnership is always entitled to the balance in his capital

account. FALSE

12. It is possible to invest no intangible assets into a partnership. yet be given a positive opening

capital balance. TRUE

13. Edward Santos directly purchased Ferdinand Romero's 250,000 partnership interest for 300,000.

The entry to record the transaction is for 300,000. FALSE

14. The retirement of a partner by payment from a partnership assets may cause the other partner's

capital accounts to decrease. TRUE

29. ano ang pagkakaiba ng round songs at partner songs sa tagalog​


Answer:

Rounds are when two (or more) groups sing the same song at different times to create harmony.

Partner songs are when two different songs are sung at the same time to create harmony.

Explanation:

CARRY ON LEARNING :)

Answer:

English Explanation:

Since some of the rounds have longer lines between parts, they require two systems. ... In partner songs, the systems (groups of staves) are bracketed together, indicating that, after the initial singing of each part separately, the parts each start at the beginning of their own line and at the same time.

Tagalog Explanation:

Dahil ang ilan sa mga pag-ikot ay may mas mahahabang linya sa pagitan ng mga bahagi, nangangailangan sila ng dalawang mga system. ... Sa mga kasosyo na kanta, ang mga system (mga grupo ng mga staves) ay magkakabit, na nagpapahiwatig na, pagkatapos ng paunang pag-awit ng bawat bahagi nang magkahiwalay, ang mga bahagi ay nagsisimula bawat isa sa simula ng kanilang sariling linya at sa parehong oras.

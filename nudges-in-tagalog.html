Kick one of the tennis balls really hard and just nudge the other, which ball have a greater force applied to it? Which will have greater acceleration?


Answer:

1. The tennis ball has a greater mass

2. With the same amount of force, the ping-pong has greater acceleration.

3. With the same amount of mass, the tennis ball with the greater force has the greater acceleration.

Explanation:

In this problem, we will the concept of Newton's law of motion where the force is directly proportional to the product of mass and its acceleration.

F = ma

For the first scenario, a tennis ball has a greater mass than a ping-pong ball since it is heavier where its mass ranges from 54 - 56 grams while the ping-pong ball is only 2.7 grams.

For the second scenario, the tennis, and ping-pong ball was hit with the same amount of force. According to Newton's law of motion, the ball with the lesser mass has the greater acceleration, if the force of the two balls is equal. Where a = F/m, since the balls have the same amount, the force divided by a small value of mass will result in a greater amount of acceleration.

For the third scenario, two tennis balls (meaning, they have the same amount of mass), the other was kicked (with greater force) and the other was nudged (with lesser force). Where a = F/m, the tennis ball with the greater force (kicked) will have a greater acceleration if the balls have the same amount of mass.

12. saan ginagamit ang megaphone


Answer:

Para saan ang megaphone?

Ang isang megaphone, pagsasalita-trumpeta, bullhorn, blowhorn, o loudhailer ay karaniwang isang portable o hand-hand, hugis-kono na acoustic sungay na ginagamit upang palakasin ang boses ng isang tao o iba pang mga tunog at idirekta ito sa isang ibinigay na direksyon.

invention

Parehong nag-imbento ang scholar na sina Samuel Morland at Aleman na Heswita na si Athanasius Kircher ng mga krudo na megaphone noong ika-17 siglo noong 1655. Si Thomas Edison, halos 200 taon na ang lumipas noong 1878, ay may pangalan na "megaphone" nang ginamit niya ang hugis sungay na "nagsasalita ng trumpeta" upang matulungan mga taong mahirap pakinggan upang makinig ng mas mahusay.

16. how megaphone produce sound energy​


Answer:

A megaphone increases the volume of sound by increasing the acoustic impedance seen by the vocal cords, matching the impedance of the vocal cords to the air, so that more sound power is radiated. It also serves to direct the sound waves in the direction the horn is pointing.

The shekere (from Yoruba Ṣẹ̀kẹ̀rẹ̀) is a West African percussion instrument consisting of a dried gourd with beads or cowries woven into a net covering the gourd. The instrument is common in West African and Latin American folkloric traditions as well as some of the popular music styles.

22. - song analysis Song Title: Para-paraan; Artist: Soulstice meaning​


Answer:

"Para-paraan" is a song by the Filipino band Soulstice, and the song is about a person who is trying to find ways to get the attention of the one they love. The title "Para-paraan" is a Filipino phrase that roughly translates to "finding a way" or "making a way," which reflects the theme of the song.

The lyrics of the song describe the narrator's attempts to impress and attract the person they love, even if it means taking unconventional and creative approaches. The narrator is willing to do whatever it takes to win the heart of the person they desire, whether it means dressing up nicely, learning new skills, or even resorting to magic and supernatural means.

The chorus of the song repeats the phrase "para-paraan" several times, emphasizing the idea of finding a way to reach the desired outcome. The song's upbeat tempo and catchy melody give it a playful and lighthearted tone, which matches the song's theme of pursuing someone with a sense of fun and creativity.

Overall, "Para-paraan" is a fun and lively song about the lengths people will go to pursue love and affection. The song's lyrics and upbeat tone make it a popular track among fans of Filipino pop music.

30. Ano po yung Meaning ng Metaphor explain po sa madaling at paraan tagalog po sana


Metaphor is similar to Simile, nagkukumpara lang ng dalawang bagay (usually, yung isa sa kinukumpara is figurative ang use nya sa sentence). Ang pagkakaiba nga lang ng dalawa is that Simile uses mga palatandaan tulad ng "like" or "as" (in tagalog, maaaring "parang", "tila", "gaya", "tulad ng" at marami pang iba (mas marami kasi sa filipino kaya unti nalang yung binigay kong example).

Halimbawa:

This candy store is like heaven! - Simile

This candy store is heaven! - Metaphor

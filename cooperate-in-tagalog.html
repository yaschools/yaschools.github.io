8. how many moles of Cooper are in 6000000atoms of cooper​


There will be 9.9632 × 10⁻¹⁸ moles of Copper in 6,000,000 atoms of Copper.

Explanation:

This problem is just an application of conversion of units. For us to be able to know the number of moles in an atom, we will use the Avogadro's number.

Avogadro's number or Avogadro's constant is the number of constituent particles that are contained in one mole. The units may be electrons per mole, atoms per mole, ions per mole, or molecules per mole. Determining which unit you'll have to use depends on the nature and character of the substance and of the reaction (if there's any involved reaction). Its value is approximately 6.022142 × 10²³.

In our case, we are given with the number of atoms, hence, the unit of Avogadro's constant we are going to use is atom per mole (atom/mol).

Now solving for the number of moles we have:

number of moles = 6,000,000 atoms ÷ 6.022142 × 10²³ atom/mol

number of moles = \frac{6,000,000 atom}{6.022142 x 10^2^3 atom/mol}

number of moles = 9.9632 × 10⁻¹⁸ moles (9.9632×10^-18 moles)

Notice that I've made the second line in terms of fraction so that the cancellation of the unit atom can be visible. The unit atom will be cancelled and the unit mole will be remain as it is what we are looking for.

Therefore, there are 9.9632 × 10⁻¹⁸ moles of copper in 6,000,000 atoms of copper.

11. Why is there a need for cooperative to integrate with other cooperatives?​


Definition of a Cooperative

A cooperative is an association of persons (organization) that is owned and controlled by the people to meet their common economic, social, and/or cultural needs and aspirations through a jointly-owned and democratically controlled business (enterprise).  The people of the cooperative are those who use its products, supplies, and/or services. Profits are also often returned back to the members of the cooperative, however, cooperatives are often more focused on services for members than for investments.

Cooperatives can be created for a number of different reasons or to fulfill a number of different needs: jointly process goods, split costs, split control over work, purchasing power (bulk buys), shared employees, shared wages, etc.

14. Philippine cooperative code of 2008 tagalog version


Ang Batas Republika 9520 o mas kilala bilang Philippine Cooperative Code of 2008 ay nagbibigay daan sa pagtatayo ng mga kooperatibong nakapagiisa o may otonomiya.

4. When Peter misbehaved, his father spanked him using a small stick as his way of disciplining his child. When Peter’s teacher discovered it, she advised the father that what he did was corporal punishment which is prohibited by the law. Is Peter’s teacher CORRECT or INCORRECT? Why?


CORRECT

what Peter's father did is one of the acts that we consider as corporal punishment. Republic Act 7610 is an act the protect the children of the Philippines from being hurt no matter who or how are they related to the one who did it t them. Like any other countries and states in United States of America, protecting children is one of their main goals.In the Philippines, anyone caught doing corporal punishment will have to face a case.

On the other hand and in my personal opinion, the father is a parent and has the right to discipline the child whatever way he wants as long as it is not too much or it doesn’t leave any wound or mark in the child’s body. Unless the stick is spanked to Peter continuously for many times and Peter until Peter becomes helpless, the spanking shall be taken to the authority and have the father punished.

WHAT IS CORPORAL PUNISHMENT?

Corporal punishment is an act of hurting a child physically as an act of punishment. It has been practiced by Filipinos even a long time ago and is known to have copied it from the kind of punishment that Spaniards give to people who go against their rules during their era of colonizing the Philippines.

DISADVANTAGES OF RA7610The description is not too specific. Any act that hurts the child physically even leaving without a trace is considered punishable by law.When children know about this act, they come to abuse it by doing unlikely things because they know that they are protected by law.No one dares to punish a child especially in school that even a sharp look towards a mischievous child is illegal. Children grow irresponsible and proud.

10 ways to prevent illness

1. Eat healthy food

By eating healthy foods, the body will get nutrients and vitamins that increase immunity so that the body can fight viruses or bacteria that cause illnesses that may attack

2. Maintain the hygiene

Illness-causing bacteria are abundant in dirty environments because it supports their reproduction. That's why it's important to keep the environment around us clean, especially the house we always live in.

3. Keep your distance from sick people

Some bacteria or viruses that cause illness can be contagious. If any of our family or friends are sick, try to keep your distance so you don't get infected

4. Wash your hands before eating

Even if you use a spoon when you eat, make sure to wash your hands. Because our hands are one of the parts of the body that have the most physical contact, such as shaking hands, moving things, and other activities that may make some bacteria stick to the hands.

5. Get the vaccines you need

One of the causes of illness is a virus that can be prevented by vaccination. Pay attention to what vaccines you must get and also what is needed to prevent virus infections.

6. Routine doing health checks

If possible, routine health check-ups can help us find out our health condition and also detect if there is a disease that has no symptoms

7. Eat clean food

Food that is clean from bacteria can prevent us from being infected with disease.

8. Take supplements and vitamins

This can help meet the body's needs for nutrients that may not be fully obtained from the food we consume. By ensuring your body gets the nutrients it needs, your immune system will also increase. But still pay attention to the amount of safe consumption of vitamins or supplements, if necessary, consult a doctor first

9. Avoid stress

Prolonged stress can lower the body's immunity so that it is easy to be infected with bacteria or viruses that cause disease

10. Avoid junk foods

Consuming too much junk food can cause an imbalance in body fat and reduce the quality of health

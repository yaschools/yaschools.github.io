11. Meaning of engaged and effective measuring analytics​


Answer:

"Engaged" in the context of measuring analytics refers to the level of participation and involvement of the stakeholders or users in the data collection and analysis process. It means that the stakeholders are actively involved in the process of defining the metrics, collecting the data, and interpreting the results.

"Effective" measuring analytics refers to the ability of the analytics to accurately and efficiently measure the performance and progress towards the goals and objectives. It means that the metrics and data collected are relevant, reliable and provide actionable insights that can be used to improve performance and achieve desired outcomes.

In summary, engaged and effective measuring analytics refers to a process where stakeholders are actively involved in the data collection, analysis, and interpretation and the metrics used are relevant, reliable, and provide actionable insights that can be used to improve performance and achieve desired outcomes.

30. 1. Describe the three types of analytics?2. Where does Analytics apply?3. When is analytics not practical?​


Answer:

1. There are three types of analytics that businesses use to drive their decision making; descriptive analytics, which tell us what has already happened; predictive analytics, which show us what could happen, and finally, prescriptive analytics, which inform us what should happen in the future.

2. It can be valuable in areas rich with recorded information; analytics relies on the simultaneous application of statistics, computer programming and operations research to quantify performance. Organizations may apply analytics to business data to describe, predict, and improve business performance.

3. For example, analytics will not likely be helpful for decision-makers (1) when there is no time for gathering, processing, and interpreting data, (2) when there is no history or precedent related to the decision or when historical data may be misleading, (3) when the decision-makers have legitimate expert experience.

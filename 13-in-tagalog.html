18. PA EXPLAIN PO NG ARTICLE 3 SECTION 13 SA TAGALOG PO


Lahat ng indibidwal, maliban sa mga napatawan ng kaparusahang reclusion perpetua nang may matibay na ebidensiya, bago pa man ang pagpapatunay ng pagkakasala, ang mabibigyan kalayaang makapagpiyansa na iginawad ng batas. Ang karapatang makapagpiyansa ay di mapahihina kapag ang prebilehiyo sa kasulatang habeas corpus ay pinawalang bisa. Ang labis na pagpiyapiyansa ay mahigpit na ipinagbabawal.



Article 3, Section 13

All persons, except those charged with offenses punishable by reclusion perpetua when evidence of guilt is strong, shall, before conviction, be bailable by sufficient sureties, or be released on recognizance as may be provided by law. The right to bail shall not be impaired even when the privilege of the writ of habeas corpus is suspended. Excessive bail shall not be required.

Graciano López y Jaena, commonly known as Graciano López Jaena, was a Filipino journalist, orator, reformist and national hero who is well known for his newspaper, La Solidaridad. Philippine historians regard López Jaena, along with Marcelo H. del Pilar and José Rizal, as the triumvirate of Filipino propagandists.

30. 13. Which of the following attire/costume is NOT worn by the dancers of Tinikling? A. Balintawak or patadyong B. Barong tagalog paired with red trousers C. Colored t-shirt paired with jeans ​


Answer:

C.

Explanation:

When dancers wear proper clothing, it helps their team members and teachers better see how their bodies move. Cohesiveness can help dancers better visualize what the dance will look like and can create stronger bonds between dancers as they realize they are all working towards the same goals.

14. Wilhelm has 3 different Filipino books and 5 different geometry books in how many ways can the books be arranged if the Filipino books are on the left and geometry on the right?


Answer:

240 ways

Step-by-step explanation:

This question is answerable by permutation.

Let's define first what is permutation:

Permutation is (https://brainly.ph/question/2021664)

To solve this problem let us first list the given.

5 Filipino books5 geometry books

The order of arrangement that should be satisfied is that the Filipino books should be on the left, and geometry on the right.

Hence, we need to list down the permutation of the Filipino books on the left, and geometry books on the right.

Permutation of Filipino books on the left:

n1! = 5!

n1! = 120

Permutation of Geometry books on the right:

n2! = 5!

n2! = 120

n1! + n2! = 240 ways

8. 15 kitchen tools untensils and equipment for cooking menudo. ​


Answer:

Stock Pot – The best and more crucial tool is the stock pot. This recipe calls for a lot of ingredients and this pot much hold them all. Cooking menudo involves long cooking, which calls for a thick bottom to disperse the heat evenly. Having a thin bottom could result in burning which will adversely affect the taste of your broth.

Ladle – Serving menudo easily will involve and large ladle. It makes the process of stirring and serving easier.

Large mixing spoon – A large cooking spoon is used early on to mixing ingredients through the different phases in cooking.

Cooking shears – Cutting tripe and honey comb tripe with a knife can be time consuming. Being able to scissor through pounds of honeycomb tripe is vastly easier than using a chef’s knife.

Chopping knife – Used to chop aromatics that will be presented table-side as options to flavoring one menudo.

Apron – Chili powder stains, so wear an apron to minimize the staining of your clothes.

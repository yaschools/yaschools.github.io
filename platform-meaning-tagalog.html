20. Enumerate types of social media platforms and their meaning ​


1. Social Networking

   -  A social networking site is a jack of all crafts. Users can share their opinions, contribute material, submit photographs and videos, create interest-based groups, and engage in vibrant debates. They're designed around the user and everything they care about, including their social connections.

2. Image Sharing

  -  Images, infographics, and pictures, for example, capture our hearts, eyes, and imaginations.

3. Video Hosting

  -  YouTube and Vimeo are video hosting sites that assist creators in putting together content and sharing it on a platform that is suited for streaming. YouTube changed the way we watch, make, and think about video forever.

4. Community Blogs

  -  People can express their opinions and connect with followers on shared blogging sites like Medium and Tumblr. These community blog platforms provide an audience while also allowing you a lot of personalization and expression.

5. Discussion Sites

  -  Anyone can ask a question or make a statement, which draws individuals together who have similar interests and curiosities.

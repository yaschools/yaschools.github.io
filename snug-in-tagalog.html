29. Give the meaning of the following words.(2PTS. EACH)1.vise 2.re iterate 3.bent 4.tweak 5. Snug​


Answer:

1. Vise is a metal tool with movable jaws that are used to hold an object firmly in place while work is done on it, typically attached to a workbench.

2. Reiterate - say something again or a number of times, typically for emphasis or clarity.

3. Bent - changed by bending out of an originally straight or even condition.

4. Tweak - twist or pull something sharply.

5. Snug - When used as verbs, snug means to make secure. When used as adjectives, snug means warm and comfortable.

WHAT IS DURIAN?

-The durian is the fruit of several tree species belonging to the genus Durio. There are 30 recognised Durio species, at least nine of which produce edible fruit, with over 300 named varieties in Thailand and 100 in Malaysia, as of 1987.

21. d.A durian with 1 kg mass is on the floor. If the durian is lifted from the floor to the top of thetable which is 2 meters from the floor, how much work is done on the durian?​


Answer:

19.6 J

Explanation:

Work done to lift an object of mass m a height h: W = Fgh = mgh.

Work = mgh; where g is acceleration due to gravity on Earth (9.8 m/s^2)

Given:

m = 1kg

g = 9.8 m/s^2

h = 2 m

Solution:

W = mgh

W = (1kg)(9.8 m/s^2)(2m)

W = (1kg)(19.6 m^2/s^2)

W = 19.6 kg.m^2/s^2

(1 kg.m^2/s^2 = 1 J)

W = 19.6 J

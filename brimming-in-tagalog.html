4. what will happen a plastic bottle with water up to the brim and cover it observe


Answer:

If a plastic bottle with water up to the brim is covered, the water inside the bottle will remain at the same level. However, if the bottle is squeezed, the pressure inside the bottle will increase, and the volume of the air trapped inside will decrease. This increase in pressure will cause the water level to rise slightly, but it will not overflow as long as the bottle is not squeezed too hard or the lid is not completely airtight. When the pressure is released, the water level will return to its original position.

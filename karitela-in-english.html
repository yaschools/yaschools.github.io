1. Pagkakaiba ng kalesa at karitela


Ang kalesa at karitela ay sasakyan na parehong hinihila ng kabayo.  Ang kanilang pagkakaiba ay ito  : ang kalesa ay mayroong isang upuan na para sa dalawang tao samantalang ang karitela ay mayroong dalawang upuan na maaring upuan ng apat hanggang anim na pasahero.

3. karitela car that weighs 55 kg is rolling down a hill with a velocity of 6.5 m/s. what is the kinetic energy of the karitela?Given: m=____kg v=_____m/sRequired:Solution:Answer:______ joule​


KINETIC ENERGY

Problem:

» Karitela car that weighs 55 kg is rolling down a hill with a velocity of 6.5 m/s. what is the kinetic energy of the karitela?

Given:m (mass) = 55 kgv (velocity) = 6.5 m/s

Required:Kinetic Energy

Solution: KE = ½ mv²KE = ½ × 55 × 6.5²KE = ½ × 55 × 42.25KE = 1161.875 J

Answer:1161.875 J (joules)

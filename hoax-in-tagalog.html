4. why is it code of kalantiaw proved a hoax by William Henry scott?​


Answer:

The Code of Rajah Kalantiaw was a supposed legal code in the epic history Maragtas that is said to have been written in 1433 by Datu Kalantiaw, a chief on the island of Negros in the Philippines. The code is now believed by many historians to have been a hoax and that it had actually been written in 1913 by Jose E. Marco as a part of his historical fiction Las antiguas leyendas de la Isla de Negros (English: The Ancient Legends of the Island of Negros), which he attributed to a priest named Jose Maria Pavon.

7. why did la loba negra consider as a hoax?


La Loba Negra o The Black She-Wolf , a novel written by Father Jose Burgos came out on October 1719 during the World War II. During that time Governor General Fernando Manuel De Bustillo was assasinated. The novel was about the Governor's widow who transforms into a wolf at night to retaliate the culprit of her husband's death.

The novel was considered as a hoax because it turned out that the novel wasn't really Fr. Burgos's handwriting. Historian William Henry Scott found out that the novel was actually a fabrication of a con man by the name of Jose E. Marco.

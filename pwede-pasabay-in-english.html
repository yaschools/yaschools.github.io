5. What's New Directions: Read and analyze the text below. Then write the information needed. Bayanihan of the People by Mary Rose DJ. Mallari Bayanihan is known as one of our traditions which portrays the values of unity, camaraderie, harmony, cooperation and teamwork in a community. In our modern times, this is still practiced. Tested by COVID-19, this Bayanihan serves as one of the solutions, when a member of a family can't go out because of restrictions, pasabuy (originally pasabay) made it possible of having your needs from the grocery store or market. Simply have the list of items and the amount it will require. That willingness to offer help within or outside the community proves how we value this tradition. It's notable to mention the kind of heroism and the helping ability of our frontliners: doctors, nurses, health workers, government officials, employees and the ordinary people. The conduct of relief operations coming from public and private sectors, rich or poor people also become visible. Prayer to our Almighty God also proves to be one of our major weapons to surpass fear and depression. Inculcated by our elders, Bayanihan is by heart still one of the traditions, an enduring value we, Filipinos do possess.. 7​


Answer:

Bayanihan is a tradition in the Philippines that reflects values of unity, cooperation, and teamwork in a community. It is still practiced in modern times, such as during the COVID-19 pandemic when people are unable to leave their homes because of restrictions. Pasabuy, originally pasabay, is a practice in which someone helps another person by buying items from the grocery store or market for them. The willingness to help others within and outside the community shows the value placed on this tradition. The heroism and helping ability of frontliners, such as doctors and nurses, is also notable. Relief operations from public and private sectors and from people of all socio-economic backgrounds are visible. Prayer to God is also seen as an important weapon to overcome fear and depression. Bayanihan is a tradition that is deeply ingrained in Filipino culture.

20. what are the difference between furry and anthro?​


Answer:

The difference between Anthro and Furry

When used as nouns, anthro means anthropology, whereas furry means an animal character with human characteristics. When used as adjectives, anthro means anthropomorphic, whereas furry means covered with fur, or with something resembling fur. ... An anthropomorphic character

23. What do you know and thoughts about the Furry Fandom in the Philippines?​


Explanation:

FurryPinas is the Philippine's furry convention that is held each year in Metro Manila, Philippines. Currently, it is Asia's largest furry convention with an attendance of over 1,700 in 2019[1]. FurryPinas was envisioned to serve as the annual hub for furry contents with the belief that the furry hobby is for anyone who wants to be interested in it, making it accessible and relatable to anyone - in the form of fursuiting, arts, cosplaying, music, games, toys, anime, movies, shows, literature, and any existing types of medium.

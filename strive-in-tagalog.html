27. how will you strive for holiness?


Striving for holiness is not an easy task. It will not be finished for a week of year for it is a lifelong journey where your patience and temptation is tested. Striving for holiness require great commitment and love for the Lord. We can be holy in our own ways by dedicating a part of our time to the Lord in our everyday life. Think of each day as another day of suffering for us to eases the poor lost souls in purgatory and also for us to express our love to God.

How to strive for holiness:

We could start having a rule of life. By having fixed times for rising, praying, and retiring.

Meditation. Conversing with God interiorly or by heart. This may vary from 15 to 30 minutes a day.

Spiritual reading. To spur us on in the spiritual life. Books like the CCC, lives of saints, etc. are being read.

Daily examination of conscience. Reviewing our thoughts, words and actions for this day.

Attention to daily duties. Stay focused on the duty of the moment.

Penance and Mortification. 3-4 penances daily to keep the will in control. But I truly suggest you to suffer greatly!

Practice of the presence of God. Remembering God's presence and uniting to him throughout the day.

Frequent confessions. Confess once a week.

Devotion to the eucharist. Receive Christ reverently and in state of grace. Also make a thanksgiving prayer by heart as an opportunity to thank him.

Devotion to our lady. Say the rosary.

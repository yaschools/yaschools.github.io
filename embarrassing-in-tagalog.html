6. humiliated embarrassed​


Answer:

Embarrassment is awkward at the time, but it can be laughed off and joked about pretty quickly. Humiliation, on the other hand, is something that resonates deeper. Its a stronger emotion that can result in hurt feelings and usually cannot be brushed off easily.

Embarrassment is something that is felt in a group of friends or people who pose no threat. Humiliation is a feeling that resulted in something someone had purposefully done toward you, or the result of something that happened in front of people you're not sure about or feel threatened by.

14. Nida bought siopao for Php80.00 and siomai for Php126.00 how much change did she receive if she gave the cashier Php300.00pa help pls .​


Answer: The change she received is Php94

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Add all the price of the food she bought. She bought siopao for 80 and siomai for 126.

80 + 126 = 206.

2. Subtract the total price of the food she bought which is 206 from 300.

300 - 206 = 94

Therefore, her change is Php94

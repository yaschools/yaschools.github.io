paano kunin ang fraction?

una gagawin mo muna syang fraction halimbawa 65/100 tapos kukunin mo ang lowest term so yung 65÷5=13 tapos yung 100÷5=20 so ang magiging fraction nya na ay 13/20

paano naman kunin ang percent ng lowesst term na fraction?

para makkuha natin ang percentage nito ay ididivide lng natin ang numerator sa denominator halimbawa ay 13÷20=0.65 so magiging 65% na sya

So ilagay ko na kung paano nakuha yung mga sagot sa bawat bilang

fraction:

65. ÷5 =13

100. ÷5 = 20

fraction is 13/20

percent:

13÷20= 0.65 / 65%

percent is 65%

100 ÷5= 20

fraction is 3/20

percent:

Wayang kulit is a traditional form of puppet-shadow play originally found in the cultures of Java, Bali, and Lombok in Indonesia. In a wayang kulit performance, the puppet figures are rear-projected on a taut linen screen with a coconut-oil (or electric) light. AGAWAN BASE

Agawan base is played with two (2) teams with equal number of players and players have base on both team.

Agawan base is called an invasion game. Agawan base, also known as capturing base, is played by two teams composed of four or more members. It can be played on grassy ground, by the seashore, or even in the neighborhood. It involves running, tagging, ang chasing. Thus it needs to be played within a safe place to avoid accidents. Each team has a base to protect from the opponents.

NUMBER OF PLAYERS:

2 groups with 4 or more members.

MATERIALS:

bases (tree, chair, or anything available in the area)

DIRECTIONS:

1. All players step on their own bases to indicate they are home.

2. The player whi first steps out of the base will become the prey or target. It could be just a play to lure players of the other team to attack.

3. Once the opponents come nearer, the player runs back to his or her own base, steps on it, and runs to chase and tag an attacker as he or she becomes the prey.

4. The first player to be tagged will become a prisoner of the other team.

What is meaning of suffix and prefix?

A suffix is a word part added to the end of a word (for example, -ful). If you add the suffix -ful to the base word, help, the word is helpful.

A prefix is a word part added to the beginning of a word or base word (for example, un-).

Answer:

SUFFIX

A suffix is a letter or group of letters, for example '-ly' or '- ness', which is added to the end of a word in order to form a different word, often of a different word class. For example, the suffix '-ly' is added to ' quick' to form 'quickly'. Compare affix and , prefix.

PREFIX

A prefix is a letter or group of letters, for example 'un-' or 'multi-', which is added to the beginning of a word in order to form a different word. For example, the prefix 'un-' is added to ' happy' to form 'unhappy'. Compare affix and , suffix.

28. anu tagalog ng suffix​


Answer:

Panlapi

Sa lingguwistika, ang isang panlapi ay isang morpema na nakakabit sa isang salitang stem upang makabuo ng isang bagong salita o form ng salita. Ang mga affix ay maaaring nagmula, tulad ng English -ness at pre-, o inflectional, tulad ng English plural -s at past tense -ed. Nakatali ang mga ito sa mga morpema ng kahulugan; ang mga unlapi at panlapi ay maaaring magkakahiwalay na mga panlapi.


29. I have learned that suffixes are ______________Suffix -ful means ________ -ness means ________ -less means _________​


Suffix

I have learned that suffixes are added at the end of the root word. There are many suffixes to add such as -ful, -ness, -less and many more.

Suffix -ful means being ful or many in feature or quality. Example is hopeful which means full of hope.

Suffix -ness means showing off the quality of something. An example is darkness which means quality of being dark.

Suffix -less means the opposite of -ful. It means being less in quality or feature. Example is painless which means less in pain or no pain.

8. A triangular landscapehas abase of 24 meters and a height of 12 meters. Finds its area


Solution:

The formula used to find the area of a triangle is

A = (1/2)bh

where:

A = area

b = length of the base

h = height

Listing the given values

b = 24 m

h = 12 m

Starting with the formula

A = (1/2)bh

Substituting all the values

A = (1/2)(24 m)(12 m)

Therefore, the area is

A = 144 m²

9. the compound figure is composed of a rectangle that measures 12cm by 8cm and a triangle with abase of 8cm and a height of 7cm. Find the area of the composite figure


Answer:

124 cm².

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the total area of the composite figure, we need to find the area of the rectangle and the area of the triangle, and then add them together.

Area of rectangle = length x width = 12 cm x 8 cm = 96 cm²

Area of triangle = (base x height) / 2 = (8 cm x 7 cm) / 2 = 28 cm²

Total area of composite figure = area of rectangle + area of triangle = 96 cm² + 28 cm² = 124 cm²

Therefore, the area of the composite figure is 124 cm².

Area of the triangular landscape.

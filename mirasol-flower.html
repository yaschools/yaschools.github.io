QUESTION:

Mirasol saved PHP 10.00 on the first day of January, php 12.00 on the second day, php 14. 00 on Thursday, and so on, up to the last day of the month. How much did mirasol save at the end of January?

SOLUTION:

[sequence]

an = a1 + (n - 1)d

d = 2

a1 = 10

n = 31

a31 = 10 + (31 - 1)2

= 10 + (30)2

= 10 + 60

a31 = 70 -> the amount she saved at the last day of the month

[series]

Sn = n/2 (a1 + an)

S31 = 31/2 (10 + 70)

= 31(80)/2

= 2480/2

ANSWER:

S31 = 1240 -> the amount she saved for the whole month

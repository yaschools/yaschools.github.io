29. Make a list .Tamia is building a rectangular kennel for her dog.She wants the kennek to have an area of 24 square yardr.What is the least amount of fencing Tamia needs to enclose the kennel use the make a list​


Answer:

Tamia needs at least 20 yards of fencing to enclose the kennel.

Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Recall that the area of a rectangle is equal to its length times its width: Area = Length x Width.

Use the given area of 24 square yards to find possible combinations of length and width that would give us an area of 24. For example, we could have a kennel that is 4 yards by 6 yards, or 3 yards by 8 yards, or 2 yards by 12 yards, and so on. There are many possible combinations, but we need to find the one that requires the least amount of fencing.

Recall that the perimeter of a rectangle is equal to the sum of its four sides. To find the amount of fencing needed, we need to add up the lengths of all four sides of the rectangle.

For each possible combination of length and width, calculate the perimeter of the kennel by adding up the lengths of all four sides. The combination that gives us the smallest perimeter is the one that requires the least amount of fencing.

Once you have found the combination of length and width that gives you the smallest perimeter, calculate the perimeter to find the amount of fencing needed.

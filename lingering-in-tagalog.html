13. What is meant by the statement that the 1987 Philippine constitution has a ' Lingering shadow of martial law?​


The 1987 Philippine Constitution is the constitution duly debated and discussed by members of the Constitutional Convention duly convened to adopt a new Philippine Constitution and was duly ratified by the Filipino people in a plebiscite for the adoption of the 1987 Philippine Constitution. The provisions of the martial law declaration by the President of the Philippines were still incorporated in the 1987 Philippine Constitution but with limited purposes and only with the approval of Congress by two third votes.

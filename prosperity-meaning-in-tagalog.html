29. what is the meaning of science and math for the people: innovation for collective prosperity


Science and Mathematics are two of the strongest foundations of life improvement. Science is the main reason why human beings  are able to procreate and thrive. More specifically, Medical Science  is responsible for the prevention and treatment of diseases. It is highly based  upon exhaustive series of trials and errors in order to acquire perfection.  Biological Science covers every division of natural sciences. This branch  is accountable for the survival and improvement of all living organisms.  When living beings die, Mortuary Science deals with the proper disposal of deceased bodies. This branch extensively prevents contamination of dangerous  bacteria found on contaminated bodies. These are just a few branches of science  but they are ultimately significant in the continuity of our lives.  

Mathematics also helps us in a number of ways. Whether we like it or not, we do have to deal with numerical operations everyday. From basic computations like addition and subtraction to the more complex ones like calculus and  statistics. Businesses won't survive without the help of accounting. Buildings  and infrastructures would collapse without in-depth calculations and measurements.

We always have the need to improve and develop new ways to make the future better. The sustainability of human beings substantially depends upon the complex processes of Mathematics and Science. Therefore it follows that we need these two branches  in order to have collective prosperity in the future.

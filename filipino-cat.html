There are various kinds of animals that become pets. However, according to a survey, the majority of people in Asian countries prefer to have dogs and cats. Many people like to take care of pets in their homes. The reason is, these animals can be friends when tired and at the same time hone themselves to be responsible.

There are various kinds of animals that become pets. However, according to surveys, the majority of people in Asian countries prefer to have dogs and cats. After that there are Vietnam and Thailand with percentages of 53% and 47%. Then, there are 34% of Indian respondents who also have dogs. Meanwhile, the country with the most residents keeping cats in Asia is Indonesia. It is noted that there are 47% of respondents in Indonesia who admit to keeping cats.

His position is followed by the Philippines with 43% of respondents who keep cats. Then, the percentage of respondents who keep cats in Thailand and Vietnam is 42% and 35% respectively. 16. some dishes that Filipino's consider as taboo is that;any dish that have dog and cat. In what act in the Philippines constitution prohibits this law? ​


Answer:

Animal Welfare Act (Republic Act No. 8485)

Answer:

The Philippines has an objective of eliminating rabies by 2020, a target that cannot be achieved unless the dog meat trade is eradicated—a fact that lawmakers understood when they included a prohibition in the trade of dog meat in the 2007 Rabies Act.

Explanation:

The killing and selling of dogs for food was banned in the capital city of Manila in 1982. A similar ban was enacted nationally in 1998, with the Animal Welfare Act (Republic Act No. 8485). The Act prohibits killing dogs for food, with minimum penalties set at 1,000 pesos (equivalent to about $US22 at the time) and not less than six months in prison.

25. ano po yong ibig sabihin ng salitang propriety At Sensory?​


Answer:

Ang "propriety" ay tumutukoy sa pagiging nararapat o angkop sa mga pangangailangan ng lipunan, moralidad, o kultura. Halimbawa, ang isang pag-uugali o kilos ay maaaring hindi itinuturing na "proper" kung ito ay labag sa mga pangangailangan ng kultura o moralidad ng isang lugar.

Ang "sensory" naman ay tumutukoy sa mga bagay na may kaugnayan sa pandama o sensory experience. Ito ay naglalarawan ng mga karanasan na konektado sa mga pandama tulad ng paningin, pandinig, pang-amoy, atbp. Halimbawa, ang mga bagay na nakikita, naririnig, naamoy, at nadarama ng isang tao ay mga sensory stimuli na maaaring magdulot ng iba't-ibang karanasan sa kanya.

26. Pls explain what a fungi proprieties and sickness​


Answer:

Fungi - A fungus is any member of the group of eukaryotic organisms that includes microorganisms such as yeasts and molds, as well as the more familiar mushrooms.

Properties:

Fungi are eukaryotic, non-vascular, non-motile and heterotrophic organisms.

They may be unicellular or filamentous.

They reproduce by means of spores.

Fungi exhibit the phenomenon of alternation of generation.

Fungi lack chlorophyll and hence cannot perform photosynthesis.

Sickness:

Mild fungal skin diseases can look like a rash and are very common.

Fungal diseases in the lungs are often similar to other illnesses such as bacterial or viral pneumonia. Some fungal diseases like fungal meningitis and bloodstream infections are less common than skin and lung infections but can be deadly.

Answer:

Fungi are eukaryotic organisms means they have true nucleus which are enclosed in membranes. They are non-vascular organisms. They do not have vascular system. ... Fungi have cell walls (plants also have cell walls, but animals have no cell walls). Fungi cause three different types of human illness: poisonings, parasitic infections, and allergies.

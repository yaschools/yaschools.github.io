20. convert the energy that travel by electromagnetic waves visible light microwave and infrared light radiation from the sun strike the atoms in your body and transfer eenergy potàngina mo module


Answer:

An electromagnetic wave has a frequency and a wavelength associated with it and travels at the speed of light, or c. The relationship among these wave characteristics can be described by vW = fλ, where vW is the propagation speed of the wave, f is the frequency, and λ is the wavelength. Here vW = c, so that for all electromagnetic waves, c = fλ.

30. Through how many degrees does a 33 rpm turntable rotate in 0.27 s?


Solution:

Convert the rotational speed from rpm (revolutions per minute) to rad/s.

[tex]33\:\dfrac{\text{rev}}{\text{min}} \times\dfrac{1\text{ min}}{60\text{ s}} \times\dfrac{2\pi\text{ rad}}{1\text{ rev}} =3.456\text{ rad/s}[/tex]

The angular speed is

[tex]\omega=\dfrac{\Delta\theta}{t}[/tex]

Solving the angular rotation and converting it from radians to degrees, we get

[tex]\Delta\theta=\omega t=(3.456\text{ rad/s})(0.27\text{ s})=0.933\text{ rad}\\\\=0.933\text{ rad}\times\dfrac{360^\circ}{2\pi\text{ rad}} =\boxed{\bold{53.4^\circ}}[/tex]

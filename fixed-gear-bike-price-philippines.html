Q: The price of a bike is ₱2,400. A discount of 12% is offered on the sale of the bike. Find its selling price.

= ₱2,112

Step-by-step explanation:

Discount= 12% of 2,400

              = [tex]\frac{12}{100}[/tex] × 2,400= ₱288

Sale price= ₱2,400 - ₱288= ₱2,112

= ₱2,112

Markdown Price = Sticker Price - Deduction

where

Markdown Price = Php 18,675.00

Sticker Price = ?

Deduction = Php 875.00

solve for Sticker Price,

Markdown Price = Sticker Price - Deduction

18675 = Sticker Price - 875

Sticker Price = 18675 + 875

Sticker Price = 19550

[tex]\therefore \large\boxed{\text{The sticker price is Php 19,550.00}}[/tex]

3. what is the meaning of cotour line cross haching at cotour shading


Answer:

Cross-hatching refers to a softer, woven application often used to build up tonal areas of shadow. Cross contour uses line to map the curved surface of depicted forms in a drawing, but instead of dealing with the profile of the form, it moves within the outer boundaries and maps the surface facing the viewer.

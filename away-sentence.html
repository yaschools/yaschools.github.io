17. what type of sentence? don't scare the birds away.​


Answer:

The given sentence is an imperative sentence. It is a type of sentence that gives a command or makes a request, and it usually ends with a period or an exclamation mark. In this case, the sentence "Don't scare the birds away" is a command, instructing someone not to do something.

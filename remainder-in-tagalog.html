[tex] \therefore [/tex] The remainder after dividing polynomial into (x - 1) is -12.

[tex]P \big( \text - \frac{2}{3} \big) = \big( \text - \frac{2}{3} \big)^{3} - \big( \text - \frac{2}{3} \big)^{2} - 8 \big(\text - \frac{2}{3} \big) - 4[/tex]

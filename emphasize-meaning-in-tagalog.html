11. How do adverbs emphasize the meaning of the sentence?​


Adverbs can emphasize the meaning of a sentence by providing additional information about the action or the verb in the sentence. They can describe how, when, where, or to what extent the action is happening. By using adverbs, writers can create more vivid and precise descriptions of the action, and can convey a stronger tone or mood in their writing. For example, the sentence "She walked quickly to the store" is more descriptive than "She walked to the store," and conveys a sense of urgency or excitement.

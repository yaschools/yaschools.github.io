23. Describe steal the bacon​


Answer:

Steal the bacon (Steal the Flingsock or Steal the Handkerchief) is a tag-based traditional children's game, in which teams try to steal a flag or other item (the "bacon") from the field without being tagged.

Steal the bacon (Steal the Flingsock or Steal the Handkerchief) is a tag-based traditional children's game, in which teams try to steal a flag or other item (the "bacon") from the field without being tagged.

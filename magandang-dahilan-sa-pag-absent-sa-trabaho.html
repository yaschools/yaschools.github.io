To find the percentage of students absent in a class of 45 pupils with 3 absent, you can use the formula:

Percentage = (3/45)×100

Simplifying the calculation, we get:

Percentage = 6.66667

Just round of then turn into percent

Percentage = 6.67%

Therefore, the percentage of students absent in the class is 6.67%.

#BrainlyFast

Having bigger mass like the sumo wrestler will have higher inertia. Inertia can also be referred as resisting in the change of motion. The bigger the mass the more it is difficult the person to move (resist change in motion or position). Mass and inertia is directly proporitonal.

Inertia depends on the mass of an object. The higher the mass the higher the inertia. Whereas momentum depends on mass time the velocity of an object.

Law of Inertia states that:

An object at rest will remain at rest unless acted upon by a net force. Net force is an unbalanced force which causes the object at rest to move. An object in motion will remain in motion unless acted upon by a net force. Net force or unbalanced force causes the object to stop moving.

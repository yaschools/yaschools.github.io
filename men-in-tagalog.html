3. WHITE MENS BURDEN poem in tagalog (summary)


Tagalog: "Hiling sa Pagsasalin"

"White Man's Burden" is a poem by the English poet Rudyard Kipling, written in 1899. It is a controversial poem that reflects the imperialist attitudes of the time. The poem essentially argues that it is the duty of white, European nations to civilize and uplift the "savage" peoples of the world, particularly those in Asia and Africa.

A Tagalog summary of the poem would convey the main idea that the white man has a responsibility to bring civilization and progress to other peoples who are seen as backward or uncivilized. However, the poem's message is often criticized for promoting a colonialist and racist worldview that justifies the subjugation of other cultures and the imposition of European values on non-European peoples.

16. LIKE HANDSHAKES, GREETINGS RITUAL ALSO VARY FROM CULTURE TO CULTURE. JAPANESE WOMAN BOW DIFFERENTLY FROM JAPANESE MENgawan nang explanation please kahit tagalog nalang​


Answer:

For Japanese men, the bow is similar to that of women, but the hands are placed at the sides or on the thighs. The feet are together or slightly apart, and the bow is made by bending forward at the waist. The head is kept level and the eyes are directed downward. The depth and duration of the bow depend on the situation and the person being greeted.

In German culture, handshakes are more common than bows. However, bows can be used in formal situations, such as greeting royalty or high-ranking officials. The German bow is a slight nod of the head with eye contact. The hands are kept at the sides or can be placed behind the back. The feet are together or slightly apart, and the body remains upright. The depth and duration of the bow are less important than in Japanese culture.


17. ano ang woman are promoted less often than men Tagalog explanation po sana​


Answer:

Ang "woman are promoted less often than men" ay nangangahulugang mas kadalasang hindi pinapataas sa puwesto o hindi binibigyan ng mas mataas na tungkulin ang mga kababaihan kumpara sa mga lalaki. Sa iba't ibang mga lugar sa mundo, mas karaniwang nakikita na mayroong diskriminasyon sa pagpapataas sa puwesto at sa pagbibigay ng oportunidad sa mga kababaihan kumpara sa mga lalaki, kahit na mayroong mga kababaihan na mas kwalipikado o may mas mataas na antas ng kompetensya kumpara sa mga lalaki. Maraming mga dahilan kung bakit nangyayari ito, kabilang ang mga patakaran sa trabaho at kultura ng lipunan na nagpapabor sa mga lalaki at nagpapahirap sa mga kababaihan.

7. What do you mean by please be guided accordingly?


The meaning of “Please be guided accordingly” is if ever you are to answer or just follow someone, you are precisely restricted or bound to their rules and regulations in doing that specific thing. It is also used in official documents and other official writings like test papers.

Examples of Please be Guided Accordingly as used in sentences: Every violation done to that exam paper will cause you to retake a new one so please be guided accordingly. Please be guided accordingly by the recently involved rules from the supervisor starting next week. I have sent you the articles for the March and April sales please be guided accordingly

It is often used in: Memos Emails Test Papers Questionnaires

All these examples are formal documents recognized by their perspective company and other government. And like any other professional document, it requires professional and formal language and grammar which allows the phrase to be highly fitted to the description.

17. please be guided accordingly


PLEASE BE GUIDED ACCORDINGLY

The Phrase "Please be guided accordingly" refers to a reminder which means if ever you do, perform thing or follow something or someone, you are prohibited and is expected to watch out for the rules and regulations of performing such things.

Please be guided accordingly, which means, there are other indications on what is the adequate and expected thing to do.

Examples

1.) I already signed all the documents for the entire month please be guided accordingly.

2.) The University's announcement of the entrance exam passers for the year 2019-2020 will be postponed indefinitely, please be guided accordingly.

3.) The company changed their schedule for the month of May please be guided accordingly.

3. What is the meaning of lonta?


Lonta is a Filipino term that is considered to be of a balbal classification, which means the word is an informal or slang term being used by Filipinos. Lonta directly means “pantalon” or jeans in English. To note, Filipinos have a habit of transferring syllables of a traditional word into a fashionable popular term. For example, erpat means father while ermat means mother.

2. what's the meaning of confusion​


Answer:

The term "confusion" generally refers to a state of being bewildered, uncertain, or disoriented. It can be used to describe a mental state in which a person is struggling to understand or make sense of something, or a situation where there is a lack of clarity or understanding.

Confusion can arise from various sources, such as a lack of information or understanding, conflicting information, or an overload of information. It can also be caused by emotional or psychological factors, such as anxiety, stress, or exhaustion.

In some cases, confusion can be a normal and temporary response to new or complex situations, and can be resolved with time, additional information, or support from others. However, in other cases, confusion may be a sign of an underlying medical or mental health condition, and may require professional evaluation and treatment.

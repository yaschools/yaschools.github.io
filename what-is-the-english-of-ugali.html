18. ugaliing magtanim sapat na nutrisyon ang aanihin english translation


“Let us plant our own food, to reap better nutrition for our own good.” This is the english translation of the theme for this year’s nutrition month: “Ugaliing magtanim, sapat na nutrisyon ang aanihin.” It aims to encourage Filipino families to try planting their own fruits and vegetables at home. It has a lot of benefits like healthier produce, lesser expenses and budget cuts. Instead of buying from supermarkets, access to chemical-free food has become easier.

22. ugaliing magtanim sapat na nutrisyon ang aanihin in english


Ugaliing magtanim in english is, "Make it a habit to sow or plant," while sapat na nutrisyon ang aanihin in english is, "right nutrition will be harvested or reaped." Putting them together in english, "Make it a habit to sow or plant so right nutrition will be reaped or harvested." As individuals, let us choose to make our bodies and surroundings healthier by living right. When there are more plants growing, more organic and natural food can be eaten. as a result, life will be greener and more nutritious. Parents can also teach their children this habit if it is modeled properly.

29. point by point comparing the junior high school and senior high school​


Answer:

The grading system in Junior High school goes like this 20% for the final examinations, 40% for written tests and 40% for the performance task, some subjects in Junior High School have a different grading system like for example, 20% final examinations, 35% written works then 45% for the performance tasks. . There are some subjects in Junior High School that have a different grading system like for example, 20% final examinations, 35% written works then 45% for the performance tasks As you can see in Junior High School, the performance task would always hold the highest percentage in every subject, while in Senior High the grading system differs from the kind of subject, per each strand they have this what you call the specialized subjects, applied subjects and the core subjects. In Senior High, written works can have the highest percentage depending on the requirements of the subject.

For the core subjects like Statistics, the grading system is like this 35% for written works, 35% for performance task and 30% for final exams (prelims, midterms and finals).The specialized subjects such as business ethics, the grading system is like this 25% for written works, 45% for performance task and 30% for final exams. While for applied subjects like Pagsulat, the grading system is like this 30% for both written works and final exams then for performance task its 40%. From all the grading systems mentioned above, we conclude that in Junior High School, performance tasks would always hold the highest percentage. In Junior High School they only have one exam under term exams which is the finals, while for Senior High School we have final exams and under this are the prelims, midterms and finals. In Senior High School the grading system would depends on the kind of subject while in Junior High School they almost have the same grading systems and the last conclusion we would like to make is that in Junior High School they focus more on performance while in Senior High School, they consider some things in a subject.

To a boy his age, the world is strange, hostile, terrifying. He needs a home, he needs you, he needs me– not a huge house like this.

From the context, this shows that the boy being talked about is still a child–young and defenseless from the snares of worldly things and reality.

What he needs are people to guide him and help him grow inorder to face the future. What he needs isn't a big house but people who will love and support him from his growth–a place he can belong to, a home.

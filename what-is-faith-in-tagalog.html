15. What is the difference between human faith and divine faith?​


The formal object of human faith is the acceptance of the credibility of revelation based on an informal reasoning process, while for divine faith it is the “acceptance of the revealed truths as true through grace on the authority of the Word of God revealing.”

26. What is the difference between Saving Faith and Walking Faith ?​


WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN SAVING FAITH AND WALKING FAITH?

Saving Faith

- This is the salvation given to us by Jesus Christ who died on the cross of Calvary that whoever believes will have eternal life this was proved by Jesus when a thief who was crucified with him believed in him. Please read the bible in the book of John 3:16 & Luke 23:42-43.

Walking Faith

After we have received the salvation he has given us, we who are alive must walk by faith in him. Not because we are saved does not mean that we are able to do what we want. The fruit of our received salvation is walking by faith in him. Those who walk by faith are active on church, Prayerful, reading and following God's word, avoid doing bad things.

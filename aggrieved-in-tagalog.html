1. Which of the following agencies provides and effective redress mechanism to financial consumer's who feel have been aggrieved with conduct, products, and service?

Answer:

b. Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas

Explanation:

Bangko Sentral's Financial Consumer Protection Department (FCPD) is mandated to provide an effective redress mechanism to financial consumers who feel they have been aggrieved by a BSFI's conduct, products, and services.

========================================

2. Which agency advances consumer rights and welfare?

Answer:

a. Department of Trade and Industry

Explanation:

Bureau of Trade Regulation & Consumer Protection - Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) The Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) is a prime mover of consumer welfare.

========================================

3.What government agency is tasked to ensure laws and regulations relating to food?

Answer:

c. Bureau of Food and Drugs

Explanation:

FDA has jurisdiction over domestic and imported foods that are marketed in interstate commerce, except for meat and poultry products. FDA's Center for Food and drugs Safety and Applied Nutrition (CFSAN) seeks to ensure that these foods are safe, sanitary, nutritious, wholesome, and honestly and adequately labeled.

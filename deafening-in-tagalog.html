The best way to understand this quote is by knowing what being deaf is. A deaf person is someone who is NOT ABLE TO HEAR properly or not hear at all. Silence can't be considered a sound because silence is the COMPLETE absence of sound.

Noise is a type of sound that is loud, unpleasant and causes disturbance. Hence, silence is definitely NOT a form of noise.

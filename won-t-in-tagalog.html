14. The golf team won 5 out of 20 games .The track team won 4 out of 10 games and the Badminton team won 4 out of 12 games . Which team won the greatest fraction and why?


Answer:

Track team

Explanation:

Golf team = 5/20

Track team = 4/10

Badminton team = 4/12

First, we must reduce the fraction in lowest terms:

5/20 = 1/4

4/10 = 2/5

4/12 = 1/3

Next, multiply the denomators to get the LCD.

3 x 5 x 3 = 60

Convert dissimilar fractions into similar fractions guided by the formula:

(LCD/Denominator) x numerator

First fraction: 1/4

(60/4) x 1

15 x 1 = 15

1/4 or 5/20 is equivalent to 15/60

Second fraction: 2/5

(60/5) x 2

12 x 2 = 24

2/5 or 4/10 is equivalent to 24/60

Third fraction: 1/3

(60/3) x 1

20 x 1 = 20

1/3 or 4/12 is equivalent to 20/60.

It would be:

Golf team = 15/60

Track team = 24/60

Badminton team = 20/60

Last, compare all the fractions. We can see that the Track team has the greatest fraction therefore we can say that they won the greatest games compared to other teams.

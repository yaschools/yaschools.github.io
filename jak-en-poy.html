30. describe the energy changes that occur for this jak in the box​


Answer:

Two kinds: “potential” and “kinetic”. When the spring is tight, before the toy clown is released, there is “potential” energy stored within the toy's mechanical mechanism. The source of this potential energy is the person who set the toy. When the toy is sprung the potential energy is converted to “kinetic” energy.

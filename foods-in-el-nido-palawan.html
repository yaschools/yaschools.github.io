How many joined the El Nido trip only?

there are 10 students who joined the three tourist spots, 1 who joined both Coron and El Nido and 5 who joined both Tubbataha Reef and El Nido.

10+1+5+n=20

16+n=20

n=4

23. Manila is 820 km from El Nido Palawan. If it will take about 27 hours to travel from Manila to Palawan, what is the average speed?


Let speed is equal to the distance over time.

r = [tex]\frac{d}{t}[/tex]

where:

r is the average speed

d is the distance travelled

time is the total time interval


Solution:

d = 820 km

t = 27 hrs


[tex]r = \frac{d}{t}[/tex]

[tex]r = \frac{820 km}{27 hr}[/tex]

[tex]r = 30.370370... \frac{km}{hr}[/tex]

[tex]r ≈ 30.4 \frac{km}{hr}[/tex]

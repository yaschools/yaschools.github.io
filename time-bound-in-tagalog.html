9. A time-bound undertaking that is carried out to create a unique service.


A time-bound undertaking that is carried out to create a unique service is project

A project is carried out in an effort to achieve certain goals that have been planned well in advance in order to produce a desired goal or benefit (output). Of course, it takes time and money to implement the project, which is its main foundation. Therefore, a project is said to be successful if it succeeds in achieving the desired results and objectives within the timeframe and budget as agreed upon in the initial planning.

Some examples of projects are the manufacture of products or services, software development, construction of public facilities, e-commerce, building construction, public infrastructure development, and others.

Projects are different from programs. Indeed, many equate it with the program, but in fact, there are important differences to know between the two.

Projects are carried out to be able to produce certain desired results in the most effective and efficient way. Meanwhile, the program is an activity that focuses more on coordination and cooperation carried out with several projects and other activities that are interconnected to achieve a greater and wider benefit.

24. kahulugan ng time bound


Ang ibig ipakakahulugan ng Time bound ay ang  pagbibigay ng limit o deadline para tapusin mo ang isang bagay.  

Tunay na mahirap ang magtrabao at kumilos na alam mo na meron kay time bound maari kang mataranta at mawala sa pokus, pero kung ikaw ay isang magaling at propesyonal na tao na marunong mag manage ng oras ay hindi mo iisipin ang salitang time bound, sapagkat bilang isang mahusay at propesyonal na tao ay kaya mong magplano kung papaano mo matatapos ng maayos ang trabahong ibinigay sa iyo sa tamang oras. Dahil iniisip mo muna ang mga hakbang na gagawin bago magsimula ang isang trabaho, pokus at disiplina sa sarili upang matapos sa tamang oras ang iniatang na trabaho sa iyo.

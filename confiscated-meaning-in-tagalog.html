7. Who are authorized to confiscate driver’s licenses? Explain.​


Answer:

LTO

Explanation:

In the Philippines, only the Land Transportation Office (LTO) can confiscate a driver's license. Since the LTO is the only one that has the authority to issue a student's permit, non-professional and professional license, this means that only the LTO can confiscate it as well. However, when caught violating traffic rules, there are also officials who are deputized by the LTO to issue tickets or confiscate licenses.

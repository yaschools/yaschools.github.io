12. how can you say that someone has a firm stand on his opinion or thought?​


Answer:

There are a few signs that someone has a firm stand on their opinion or thought:

- Clarity and consistency: They are able to articulate their opinion or thought clearly and consistently, without wavering or contradicting themselves.

- Confidence: They speak with conviction and confidence, and are not easily swayed by counterarguments or opposition.

- Openness to discussion: While they may be confident in their opinion or thought, they are still open to hearing other perspectives and engaging in productive discussions.

- Knowledgeable: They have a deep understanding of the subject matter and are able to provide evidence to support their position.

- Willingness to take action: They are willing to act on their beliefs and opinions, even if it means taking a stand against others or facing opposition.

- Overall, someone with a firm stand on their opinion or thought is confident, knowledgeable, open to discussion, and willing to take action.

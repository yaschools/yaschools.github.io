1. love letter for your crush​


Dear [Crush's Name],

I hope this letter finds you well. I've been wanting to tell you something for a while now, but I've never had the courage to say it in person. So here goes: I have a crush on you. From the moment I met you, I knew there was something special about you. You have this way of lighting up a room with your smile, and your laughter affects everyone around you. Every time I'm around you, I feel like I'm on top of the world.

I've been trying to figure out the best way to express my feelings to you, and I realized that the best way for me to do that is through this letter. I want you to know that I care about you deeply, and I admire you for who you are. You are kind, intelligent, and beautiful inside and out. I love the way you make me feel when I'm with you, and I can't stop thinking about you when we're apart.

I know that we've only known each other for a short time, but I hope that we can get to know each other better. I would love to take you out on a date, just the two of us, and see where things go from there. I understand if you don't feel the same way, but I had to take this chance and tell you how I feel.

Thank you for taking the time to read this letter. I hope that we can talk soon and maybe even go out on that date.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

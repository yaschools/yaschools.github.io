6. I don't chase i attract what belongs to me will find me in tagalog


Answer:

I think the key to the phrase is the word "chase." You shouldn't ever have to chase the people you want in your life. And you shouldn't expect people to chase you.

In an ideal world, we would find those people who we enjoyed and who enjoyed us without much trouble, but reality is that it is hard to find people you really like and want to be with. Sometimes those people aren't as enamored of us as we are of them.

I don't think you can sit back and expect the "right people to come to you" but I do think that when you come across the right people, they will stick with you.

That doesn't mean relationships are effortless we all have to put ourselves out there and be friendly and interactive. Sometimes relationships are hard, but worth it.

15. which of the following words has the same meaning with the word legend .a. chase b.immigrant c myth​


Answer:

C. Myth

Explanation:

The closest answer is the letter C, they are both traditional stories.

        A myth is a folktale that describes a people's views on the natural and human world. Gods or supernatural heroes are frequently the protagonists in mythology. The stories are set in a far-off time.

       A legend, on the other hand, is a conventional narrative about the past that was recounted by individuals who thought the stories were true. King or hero figures are frequently featured. Legends like Odysseus from Ancient Greece, Beowulf from Norse countries, and King Arthur from Old England are only a few instances of well-known legends. Legends were believed to be factual, much like myths.

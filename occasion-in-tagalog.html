20. Differentiate informative speech and speech for special occasionInformative speech - Speech for special occasion-​


Answer:

Informative speech -

An informative speech is one that intends to educate the audience on a particular topic. There are many different types of informative speeches, including speeches that describe the conditions of a subject and speeches that instruct the audience on how to perform an action.

Speech for special occasion-

In broad terms, a special occasion speech is a speech designed to designed to address and engage the context and audience's emotions on a specific occasion. Like informative or persuasive speeches, special occasion speeches should communicate a clear message, but the manner of speaking used is typically different.

10. A Latin word for an unacceptable person


The answer is Persona non grata, plural:  Persona non gratae.

From the Latin words persona, person; + non, not; + grata, feminine: gratus, acceptable. Literally means person not appreciated, not acceptable. A person who is not welcome.

In diplomacy, a persona no grata is a person prohibited by country’s government to enter or remain in a particular country.

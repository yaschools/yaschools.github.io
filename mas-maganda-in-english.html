Answer:

Unfinished Dreams

My grandfather was very helpful; he always lent a hand when someone needed it. He loves his family, and he shows it by being caring, lovable, and supportive. Although sometimes he's strict, he is still understanding.

My late memories of my grandfather were the best. I remember climbing up into the mountains to visit him and my lola. There's no trouble there; all you could see and feel was nature and peace.

Those were the moments I realized how pure and unconditional my grandfather's love was. His care for us, his grandchildren, was very genuine. He taught us the right things, and he corrects us when we're wrong. He even told us to keep on dreaming and continue pursuing our dreams in the future.

He's strict, but he just wants what's best for us. He just wants us to be disciplined, and his strictness doesn't mean that he doesn't love us. Instead, his strictness means that he loves us, cares for us, and wants the best for us.

But everything has an end. It was very sad that, in the end, he needed to leave us. I badly miss my grandfather, particularly whenever I am at my grandma's house. Seeing her long for my grandfather broke me into pieces.

It was very hard to accept; my grandfather was one of my biggest supporters. He motivated me to do better and to be better. He's there when I'm dreaming, and he made me realize that my dreams could turn into reality.He's one of the reasons I'm striving to achieve my dreams.

Every time I remember the time when my grandfather was almost chasing for his breath, it breaks my heart. I was very scared that time because I knew what that meant. And the things I was scared of happened, but I know everything happens for a reason.

I know that my grandfather has a lot of unfinished dreams. And I am certain that when the moment comes that I'm finally holding my diploma, I will be the one to finish those dreams. I will pursue our dreams and make my grandfather proud.

When Ge finally bid his farewell, I told myself that I would not let our dreams slip through my fingers. I will strive for my goals and reach for my dreams. I don't want to say that you're not here because you own a very special place in my heart. I will make you proud, lolo.

*for my be loving Grandfather Elisio Santiago*

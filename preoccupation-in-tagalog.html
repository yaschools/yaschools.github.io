21. If the findings were gathered from the minority of the population, it is impossible to establish its reliability and validity. Why do you think so? What preoccupation of quantitative research is involved here? Write 3-5 sentences .


If the findings were gathered from only a minority of the population, it is impossible to establish its reliability and validity because the sample may not be representative of the entire population. If the sample is not representative, the findings cannot be generalized to the entire population, and there may be a bias in the results due to the group studied. This is related to the preoccupation of quantitative research with obtaining representative and random samples to increase the generalizability and accuracy of research findings. Therefore, quantitative research often uses statistical analysis to ensure that the sample is representative of the population and that the results are reliable and valid.

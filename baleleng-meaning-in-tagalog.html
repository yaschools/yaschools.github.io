Answer:

T'Bolis

The T'Bolis are people of medium build. They are light in complexion. Some of them are square-jawed. Their hair may be curly or straight.

Sahaya

Individualistic and independent, showing leadership and drive. The 1 is masculine, focused, an originator and self-starter; it is also progressive, strong-willed, courageous, self-reliant and rebellious (in a constructive way).

Baleleng is one of the famous Filipino folk song which is Sama Dilaut and/or Tausug in origin[1][2] that has been passed through generations. The composer or the writer of the song was not credited and was unknown.[3] The tune was passed by mouth from province to province and the original lyrics of the song was altered. Versions of Filipino local artists have made the song popular both in Visayan and Tagalog languages.[4]

18. mahahalagang detalye sa baleleng song? ​


Answer:

Baleleng is one of the famous Filipino folk song which is Sama Dilaut and/or Tausug in origin[1][2] that has been passed through generations. The composer or the writer of the song was not credited and was unknown.[3] The tune was passed by mouth from province to province and the original lyrics of the song was altered. Versions of Filipino local artists have made the song popular both in Visayan and Tagalog languages.[4]

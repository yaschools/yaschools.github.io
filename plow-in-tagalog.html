7. instead of plowing up and down,farmers should plow across the slope to form ridges


Answer:

Contour Farming

Explanation:

https://brainly.ph/question/115182

Contour Farming is done by many farmers in many regions of the world. It is mostly practiced in lands with agriculturally-dependent areas like the Philippines and the rest of Southeast Asia. It is the type of soil preservation wherein instead of plowing up and down farmers form ridges to prevent soil erosion. Erosion is the number one enemy of farmers. It is a phenomenon in which topsoil (which is rich in minerals and nutrients) is washed away or displaced. Now, what happens with contour farming is that it protects the plants and soil from being washed away by water ( during rainy seasons), wind and other factors that contribute to erosion (https://brainly.ph/question/115182).

27. what is the the category of disc plowAnd why is the uses/function of disc plow​


Answer:

disk plow or disk, farm implement employing a row or rows of concave circular steel disks that cut and pitch the soil in a way somewhat similar to a moldboard plow. It can be used in many situations where the latter will not work, such as dry crusty soil, rough ground, or sticky gumbo.

Preventing Pilferage

Pilferage by definition is considered the partial theft of contents from a package. The thief may also steal the contents, but leave behind the resealed package filled with bogus contents. Broader aspects of theft may include taking the entire package, pallet load, truck load, shoplifting, etc. There are two types of Pilferers; the Casual Pilferer and the Systematic Pilferer. 14. What is pilferage what makes it unethical?


Pilferage is an act of stealing small items or small amount.  Sometimes it is called "petty theft" or "petty crime".  This kind of stealing is hard to notice because of its rather scrupulous and subtle means of action by a person or persons involved.  Oftentimes, pilferage is planned, organized act of individuals or groups of professionals that work together, and hide what they are doing.

This is unethical and punishable by law of man.  More so to God because this is an act of stealing.  Stealing is a crime.  In Bible times, when a man or woman is caught with stealing having a strong evidence against the wrongdoing, he/she must be stone to death.  This capital offense shows the gravity of a crime.

Petty crimes such as pilferage often leads to big and more organized form of stealing in the long run.

16. Before you are hired as the security manager in the company, there were lot of pilferage happened before. Upon hired, you noticed that the fence surrounding the plant/installation is made of wire. In order to stop theses pilferages, what are the security measures do you recommend to the management?​


Answer:

I will propose the removal of the wire fence and replace it with something sturdier and more opaque. It is a good idea to install a cement wall there instead. This will prevent more pilferages from happening in the future since it will be harder for people to sneak in and out of the building.

Aside from that, I also recommend the installation of CCTV cameras in different areas of the building so that we will know who is behind these acts. This is going to be costly, but it will be worth it. It is always good to know what is going on in the area when there is no one around.

Lastly, I would suggest to the management that they invest in a better lighting system. This is going to ensure that criminals will find it harder to sneak around the area in the nighttime. It will make things harder for these people since they will be visible regardless of the time of day.

By putting these security measures in place, it is guaranteed that there will be less pilferages in the future.

2. I love you so much that it hurts meaning


I love you so much that it hurts means lovesick. It brings a negative state of mind and heartbreak. It happens when the person you love is not a part of your life anymore. Our body chemistry is also a reason when love hurts. Our brains release pain hormones when we feel sad. However, true love doesn't hurt, it never fails.

Love Hurts

These are the common reasons why love hurts:

Unrealistic expectation. Uncertainty of the future in a relationship. Unsolved issues from past relationships. Chemical crashes in our body. Abusive partner in a relationship.

Ways to Let Go

These are the ways to let go of past hurts in love:

Decide to let it go. Express your sorrow. Stop playing the victim and blaming. Focus on the present. Forgive them and yourself.

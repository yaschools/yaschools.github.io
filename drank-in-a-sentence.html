26. during a contest, arman drank 1 9/5 liters of lemonade and frank drank 1 1/2 liters. who drank more lemonade and by how many liters?​


Answer:

Arman drank more lemonade by 13/10 liters or

1 3/10 liters.

Step-by-step explanation:

Subtract the whole numbers, 1-1 = 0

Subtract the fractions, 9/5 - 1/2

To subtract look for the least common denominator(LCD). In this fraction the LCD is 10.

Therefore 9/5 = 18/10 , 1/2 = 5/10.

18/10 - 5/10 = 13/10 or 1 3/10

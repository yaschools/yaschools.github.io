22. English-Make a slogan on the importance of variation in plant and animal breeding.Tagalog-Gumawa ng isang slogan sa kahalagahan ng pagkakaiba-iba sa pag-aanak ng halaman at hayop. Nyawang​


Answer:

Generally, genetic variation is important for the survival of all species so that they will not become extinct. Genetic variation is the differences in alleles in each individual.

Through genetic variation, some animals have better immune system, or longer limbs, faster runners compared to the other animals. The same concept holds true for plants.

In plants for example, if a pest was to attack a rice field with no genetic variation then all the plants would die.

However, due to genetic variation, some plants could be immune to the pest. These "immune" plants will survive and reproduce, producing plants with stronger immune system.

23. a breed or cause to breed withanother of a different race or species

Hybrid Biology

Explanation:

Basically, In biology, a hybrid is the offspring resulting from combining the qualities of two organisms of different breeds, varieties, species, or genera through sexual reproduction. The concept of a hybrid is interpreted differently in animal and plant breeding, where there is interest in the individual parentage.

In reproductive biology, a hybrid is an offspring produced from a cross between parents of different species or sub-species. An example of an animal hybrid is a mule. The animal is produced by a cross between a horse and a donkey. Liger, the offspring of a tiger and a lion, is another animal hybrid.

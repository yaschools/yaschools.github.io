8. where is the center of mass for the fork and toothpick system?​


Answer:

The system prefers to be in the lowest energy state, and so the centre of mass remains directly below the pivot. Here, the centre of mass of the rigid combination of two forks and a toothpick lies somewhere along the second toothpick (that forms the pivot) probably just above the thumb.


9. There are 700 toothpicks in a​ regular-sized box. If a jumbo box is made by doubling all the dimensions of the​ regular-sized box, how many toothpicks will the jumbo box​ hold? The jumbo box holds nothing toothpicks.


Step-by-step explanation:

Referring to the problem, there are 700 toothpicks inside the regular box. This means that the volume occupied by the 700 toothpicks would be the volume of the box. Further, it says that a jumbo box is made by doubling the dimensions of the regular box. Say we have x length, y width, and z height of the regular box as its dimensions. Doubling the dimensions would give us 2x length, 2y width, and 2z height as the jumbo box' dimensions

Solving the volume of the regular box, we get xyz, since volume is just length x width x height

Thus, the volume of the jumbo box is (2x)(2y)(2z) = 8xyz

If a volume xyz can contain 700 toothpicks, then the volume 8xyz would contain

(8)(700 toothpicks) = 5600 toothpicks

17. there are 750 toothpicks in a regular-sized box. if a jumboo box is made by doubling all the dimensions of the regular-sized box, how many toothpicks will the jumboo box hold?


We use the formula: V = 750 for the volume of the regular-sized box. Since the volume of a box is Length × Width × Height, we assume that the volume of the jumbo box is (2L)(2W)(2H) = V --> 8 LWH = V. Therefore, 8 times the volume. So, the jumbo box can hold 6000 toothpicks.

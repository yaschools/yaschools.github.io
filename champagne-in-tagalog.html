Problem SolvingQUESTION

How many liters of champagne would be needed for a wedding reception if 80 people are expected, and each drinks 400mL of champagne?

ANSWER

Find first the amount of champagne the wedding reception needs to have then, convert the unit to liters.

[tex][Sol]\\80\ \times\ 400\ mL=\underline{32,000\ mL}\\\rm{Converting,}\\\dfrac{32,000\ mL}{1}\ \times\ \dfrac{1\ L}{1000\ mL}\\=\dfrac{32,000\ L}{1000}\\=\boxed{\bold{32\ L}}[/tex]

Hence, 32 Liters of champagne are needed for the wedding reception.

27. why does a champagne cork explode out of a shaken bottle​


Answer:

The pressure builds to several atmospheres, so as soon as the cork is removed, the pressure above the liquid drops to one atmosphere. At this pressure, the liquid cannot hold as much dissolved carbon dioxide and the excess now explodes out of the bottle bringing the liquid with it.

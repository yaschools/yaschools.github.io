17. What is technical smuggling​


TECHNICAL SMUGGLING = IS AN ACT OF FRAUDULENTLY, FALSIFIYING OR ERRONEOUSLY DECLAIRING IMPORTED GOODS ENTERING A COUNTRY.


Sana maka tulong pa brainliest poTECHNICAL SMUGGLINGIs an act of fraudulently, falsifying or erroneously declaring imported goods entering a country

Three types of technical smuggling?

“MISDECLARATION”

WHEN THE DISCREPANCY PERTAINS QUANTITY, QUALITY, DESCRIPTION, WEIGHT, OR MEASUREMENT OF THE IMPORTED GOODS.

“MISCLASSIFICATION”

ON OTHER HAND, EXISTS WHEN INSUFFICIENT OR WRONG DESCRIPTION OF THE GOODS OR USE OF WRONG DESCRIPTION OF THE GOODS OR USE OF WRONG TARIFF HEADING WAS DECLARED RESULTING IN DISCREPANCY.

“UNDERVALUATION”

IT IS PRESENT WHEN:- THE DECLARED FAILS TO DISCLOSE IN FULL THE PRICE ACTUALLYPAID OR PAYABLE OR ANY DUTIABLE ADJUSTMENT TO THE PRICE; OR WHEN AN INCORRECT VALUATION METHOD IS USED; OR THE VALUATION RULES ARE NOT PROPERLY OBSERVED.

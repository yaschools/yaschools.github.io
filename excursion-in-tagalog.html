11. 50 children went on an excursion.2/5 of them were girls. How many girls were there? How many boys were there? Show your solution. ​


Answer:

No. of girls: 20 Girls

No. of boys: 30 Boys

Explanation:

Given:

50 Children

2/5 are girls

Find:

no. of Girls = ?

no. of Boys = ?

Solution:

Bring 50 to lowest term.

[tex] \frac{2}{5} \times 50[/tex]

Times 20 from 10.

[tex]2 \times 10[/tex]

There you will get the number of girls.

[tex]20 \: girls[/tex]

Then, to find the number of boys, Minus 50 from 20 girls from what we have got.

[tex]50 - 20 = 30[/tex]

There you will arrive wherein 20 are girls, and 30 are boys in 50 children who went on an excursion.

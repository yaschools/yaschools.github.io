10. This theory states that inheritance only takes place by means of germ cell, other cells do not function as agents of heredity


Answer:germ plasm theory

In the germ plasm theory, inheritance in a multicellular organism only takes place by means of the germ cells: the gametes, such as egg cells and sperm cells. Other cells of the body do not function as agents of heredity. ... Genetic information cannot pass from soma to germ plasm and on to the next generation.

14. What is SIGLAKAS? Hint: It is a ____


Answer:

SIGLAKAS is a word that is derived from the Filipino words “Sigla” (vigor) and “Lakas” (strength). It is a two day sport fest where both students and their parents participate in various games from tug of rope to basketball. Its purpose is to develop sportsmanship, cooperation, and bring the whole family together!

17. ano ibig sabihin ng sawa


sawa •

n.

1. sawa, boa constrictor

2. sawa'

adj.

satiated, sated, satisfied, tired

» synonyms and related words:

tired

adj.

1. weary, exhausted: pagod, pagal, hapo, nahahapo

2. tired of waiting: inip

3. tired from standing, kneeling, etc.: ngawit, nangangawit, mitig, namimitig

4. tired by noise or a monotonous attitude or condition: bagot, nababagot

5. fed up: sawa na, nagsasawa na, nanghihinawa na, suya na

boa

n.

a very large snake that is not poisonous: sawa

fed-up

adj.

tired of, disgusted, annoyed: suyang-suya, sawang-sawa, yamot na yamot

bantad

adj.

sated, satiated [syn. sawa', suya']

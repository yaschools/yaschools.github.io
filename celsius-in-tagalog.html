19. solve for the average temperature of 29 celsius 30 celsius 28 celsius celsius[tex]i will brainliest the verified answer[/tex]​


Answer:

Average Temperature (Mean) = 29.5°C

Formula:

[tex]M = \dfrac{\text{Sum of all Data Points}}{\text{Number of Data Points}}[/tex]

Given:29 °C 30 °C 28 °C31 °C​

Solution:

[tex]M = \dfrac{\text{Sum of all Data Points}}{\text{Number of Data Points}}\\\\\\M = \dfrac{29 \ ^\circ \ \bold{C} + 30 \ ^\circ \bold{C} + 28 \ ^\circ \bold{C} + 31 \ ^\circ \bold{C} }{\text{4}}\\\\\\M = \dfrac{29 + 30 + 28 + 31 }{\text{4}}\\\\\\M = \dfrac{118}{\text{4}}\\\\\\M = \dfrac{59}{2}\\\\\\\boxed{M = \dfrac{59}{2} \ \ \ \text{or} \ \ \ 29.5}[/tex]

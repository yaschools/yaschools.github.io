[tex] \sqrt[6]{2 {}^{6} } [/tex]

= 2

2. Write 16 in exponential form;

[tex] \sqrt[4]{ - 2} {}^{4} [/tex]

then reduce the index of the radical and the exponent 4

= 2i where i represents imaginary number

then reduce the index of the radical and the exponent 5

= 5i where i represents imaginary number

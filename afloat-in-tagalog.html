1. differentiate bobbing control and staying afloat​


Answer:

Bobbing control means quickly moving the head up and down in water, while staying afloat means keeping the whole body up in water. Bobbing helps to keep the head above water, but doesn't keep the whole body up, while staying afloat allows the swimmer to keep the entire body up or move through the water without sinking.

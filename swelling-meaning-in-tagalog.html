2. what does "the outcry swells across the world" mean?​


Answer:

"The outcry swells across the world" means that there is a growing and widespread expression of strong public protest or demand for action, which is being heard and felt in many different parts of the world. This can refer to a range of issues or events that have triggered a significant and passionate response from people across different countries and cultures, such as political crises, social injustices, environmental disasters, or humanitarian emergencies. The use of the word "swells" suggests that the outcry is increasing in intensity and momentum, as more and more people become aware of the issue and add their voices to the chorus of protest.

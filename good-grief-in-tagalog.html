The use of the words "hears the rain and the noise of battle" implies that good prose should be able to transport the reader to the scene and make them feel as if they are a part of it.

The quote also suggests that good prose has the power to evoke a range of emotions, from grief to universality, which lends it a youthful beauty. This means that good prose can make the reader feel a variety of emotions, and that this emotional depth is what makes it beautiful.

Furthermore, the quote implies that good prose has the ability to convey a sense of universality, which allows the reader to identify with the emotions and experiences described in the story, regardless of their own background or experiences.

11. ano ang ibig sabihin ng low grief death at high grief death? ​


Answer:

Ang grief o pagluluksya ay isang masidhing emosyon dahil sa pagkamatay ng minamahal

Explanation:

Ang pagluluksa o grief ay isang matinding emosyon ng kalungkutan dahil sa pagkamatay ng isang minamahal sa buhay. Sinasabing ang pagluluiksya ay maaring maging low-grief o high-grief depende sa kung sino ang namatay:

low-grief death - maaring ang namatay ay isang kamag-anak ngunit hindi naman kayo masyado nagkikita o nagkakausap. Bagamat may lungkot hindi ito kasing tindi kung mas malapit sayo ang namatay.

high-grief death - eto naman ay kung ang namatay ay malapit sa iyo. Iilan sa mga halimbawa nito ay kung ang lola, nanay, tatay o kapatid ang namatay. Para sa isang magulang, ang pagkamatay ng isang anak ay isang halimbawa ng high-grief death.

Pero, eto may ay low or high-grief, kailangang harapin ang emosyon ng pagkamatay at maintindihan bakit ito nararamdaman. Kailangan din magkaroon ng acceptance pagkatapos na makapagluksa. Sa mga panahon na ito, kailang ang pakikipag usap sa mga taong pinagkakatiwalaan upang maproseso ang nararamdaman. Kapag hindi, maaring magresulta ito sa depresyon.

28. Which stage of loss and grief is most closely associated with good mental and emotional health? A. Depression B. Anger C. Acceptance D. Denial


The stage of loss and grief that is most closely associated with good mental and emotional health is C. Acceptance. This is the last stage of the Kübler-Ross model, otherwise known as the Five Stages of Grief (trademarked) where it posits that a person who lost someone, undergoing grief, goes through five steps famously known as DABDA. This is described in a bit more detail below:

Stages of Grief

Denial - one does not accept that a loved one has died.

Anger - anger is shown, whether towards the self, others or a higher being

Bargaining - usually negotiating with God that the death does not happen

Depression - feelings of despair

Acceptance - finally coming to terms with the death

When undergoing grief, it is important that people seek help if help is needed, especially if depression is very evident as this could cause deeper mental problems later on.

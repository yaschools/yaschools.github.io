Importance of Rhyme Scheme in a Sonnet

Rhyme Scheme is a pattern of rhyming lines used in writing poem or sonnet. It establishes the form of a poem or a sonnet. Further, rhyme scheme is helpful in making a poem musical. It plays an important role in creating an artistic sonnet pleasing to the ears. Aside from that, rhyme helps in memorizing the lines of a sonnet for recitation.

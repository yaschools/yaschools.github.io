Common and Abstract Nouns

Common nouns are used for general items rather specific ones. Example of Common Noun is cellphone. Proper nouns are nouns that are specific such as Samsung, Harry Potter. Abstract nouns are nouns that you can't see, can't taste, can't smell, can't hear or touch. Example of abstract noun is love and anger.

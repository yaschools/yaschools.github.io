6. cita is six years older than bona,and bona is twice as old as albert.four years from now,cita age will be 8 years less than the sum of albert and bona age.how old is each now


Let 'x' be the age of Cita at present
      'y' be the age of Bona at present
      'z' be the age of Albert at present
------------------------------------------------
Four years from now:
x+4  ---Cita's age
y+4  ---Bona's age
z+4  ---Albert's age
-------------------------------------------------
Translating the statements into mathematical expressions you'll have:
x = y + 6     ----equation 1
y = 2z         ----equation 2
(x+4) = (y+4) +(z+4) - 8     -----equation 3
From equation 3
(x+4) = (y+4) +(z+4) - 8
x + 4 = y + 4 + z + 4 - 8
x + 4 = y + z     -----equation 3'
Express x and z in terms of y
From equation 2
y = 2z
z = y/2   ----equation 2'
Substitute equations 2' and equation 1 to equation 3'
x + 4 = y + z
(y+6) + 4 = y + y/2
y + 6 + 4 = y + y/2
Combine like terms
y - y + y/2 = 10
y/2 = 10
Cross multiply
y = 20 yrs old ----Bona's age
Substitute y=20 to equation 2
y = 2z
20 = 2z
z = 20/2
z = 10  ----Albert's age
substitute y=20 to equation 1
x = y + 6
x = 20 + 6
x = 26 yrs old ---Cita's age

9. Cita is 6 years older than Bona, and Bona is twice as old as Albert. 4 years from now, Cita's age will be 8 years less than the sum of Albert's and Bona's age. How old is each now?Show your solution.


Answer:

Present ages:Albert = 10 years oldBona = 20 years oldCita = 26 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Represent each age.

Present Ages:

Albert: x

Bona: 2x

Cita: 2x+6

Ages in 4 years (add 4 to present age):

Albert: x+4

Bona: 2x+4

Cita: 2x+6 + 4  or  2x+10

Derive the equation from ages in 4 years:

Cita's age = [Albert's age + Bona's age] - 8

2x + 10 = [(x+4) + (2x+4)] - 8

Solve for x, Albert's present age:

2x + 10 = [3x + 8] - 8

2x + 10 = 3x

3x - 2x = 10

x = 10  ⇒  Albert's present age

2x ⇒ 2(10) = 20  ⇒ Bona's present age

2x+6  ⇒ 2(10)+6 = 26  ⇒  Cita's present age

Check:

Ages in 4 years:

Cita's age = [Albert's age + Bona's age] - 8

26 + 4 =  [(10+4) + (20+4)] - 8

30 = 38 - 8

30 = 30  (True)

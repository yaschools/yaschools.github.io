_______ 3. Slide a tiny bead on the threading material, then the crimping bead, then another tiny

_______ 4. Gather 2 crimp beads, 1 clasp, and the beads for the desired necklace.

_______ 6. Gather your stringing materials. The best kinds are flexible beading wire and beading

_______ 7. Crimp the second end and cut the stringing material with slush cutters. It is not

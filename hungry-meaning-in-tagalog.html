30. the hungry mouse what is the exposition of the story the hungry mouse​


Answer:

The exposition of the story "The Hungry Mouse" sets up the main characters and introduces the problem. The story typically begins by introducing the main character, a mouse who is hungry and searching for food. The mouse may be described as small, weak, and vulnerable, highlighting the challenges it faces in finding food. The exposition may also introduce other characters, such as a cat or other animals that the mouse must avoid. The main problem or conflict of the story is also introduced, which is usually the mouse's struggle to find food in a dangerous environment while avoiding predators.

24. 1. For Kristine's birthday party, her mom cooked macaroni and cheese. She used 4 2/5 cups of parmesan cheese and 2 4/7 cups of cheddar cheese. How much cheese did Kristine's mom use in all?​


Answer:

244/35 or 6 25/35 or 6 5/7

Step-by-step explanation:

Mixed fraction to improper fraction

4 2/5 = ((4*5)+2)/5 = 22/5

2 4/7 = ((2*7)+4)/7 = 18/7

least common denominator of 7 and 5 is 35

(22*7)/35 + (18*5)/35 = 244/35 or 6 25/35 or 6 5/7

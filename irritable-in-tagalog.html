28. √24 RATIONAL OR IRRITIONAL​


Answer:

Is the square root of 24 rational or irrational?

The square root of 24 is a rational number if 24 is a perfect square. It is an irrational number if it is not a perfect square. Since 24 is not a perfect square, it is an irrational number. This means that the answer to "the square root of 24?" will have an infinite number of decimals. The decimals will not terminate and you cannot make it into an exact fraction.

√24 is an irrational number

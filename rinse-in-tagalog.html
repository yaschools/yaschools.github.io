29. Which of the following is the right step by step procedures in manual dishwashing. * A.Drain and air-dry, scrape and pre-rinse, rinse, sanitize, and wash dishes. B.Rinse, scrape and pre-rinse, wash, drain, air-dry and sanitize dishes C.Scrape and pre-rinse, rinse, wash, sanitize, drain and air-dry dishes D.Scrape and pre-rinse, wash, rinse, sanitize, drain and air-dry dishes


Answer:

D.

Explanation:

First, Scrape up all the dirt

Second, pre rinse to get rid of the leftover

Next, wash the dishes with soap.

Fourth, Rinse the soap with water

Fifth, Sanitize the dishes so that no dirt in the plate is left

Sixth, Put the plate into the drainer so no water is left

Lastly, Air dry the dishes

And You're Done!

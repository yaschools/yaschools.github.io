21. Mr. Baldonado is considering to pay his outstanding balance after 6 years of payment. The original amount of the loan is Php500,00 payable annually in 10 years. If the interest rate is 10% per annum and the regular payment is Php81,372.70 annually, how much is the outstanding balance after 6th payment? ​


Answer: 257,940.51

Step-by-step explanation:

Outstanding Balance (B)

Annual payment (R)

Interest rate (i)

Number of years (n)

Number of payments paid (k)

Remaining number of payments (z)

z=n-k

z=10-6

z=4

B= R((1-(1+i)^-z)/i

B= 81,372.70 ((1-(1+10%)^-4)/10%)

B=257,940.51

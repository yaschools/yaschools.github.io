A total of 37,000 cell phone owners were surveyed and of those surveyed it showed that 9,250 had someone who was talking or texting walk into them. If 192 students at Sebastian Middle School were randomly surveyed, I would predict that of them will have someone who was talking or texting walk into them.


Answer:

To predict the number of students at Sebastian Middle School who will have someone who was talking or texting walk into them, we can use the proportion of cell phone owners who experienced this distraction in the nationwide survey.

The proportion of cell phone owners who had someone who was talking or texting walk into them can be calculated as:

9,250/37,000 = 0.25

This means that 25% of cell phone owners surveyed experienced this distraction.

To predict the number of students at Sebastian Middle School who will have someone who was talking or texting walk into them, we can use this proportion:

0.25 x 192 = 48

Therefore, we can predict that approximately 48 out of 192 students at Sebastian Middle School will have someone who was talking or texting walk into them based on the nationwide survey results. It's important to note that this is just a prediction and the actual number could be different due to various factors such as the specific location of the school or the behavior of the students surveyed.

14. Mang Inot is going to put eggs in trays of 10 eggs & 12 eggs. What is the smallest number of eggs that Mang Inot can put using the trays? How will you solve the problem? What is the answer of the problem? ​


ANSWER

The smallest number of eggs that Mang Inot can put using the trays is 22 eggs.

SOLUTION

To solve the problem, we can use the least common multiple (LCM) of 10 and 12, which is 60. Since 60 is divisible by both 10 and 12, Mang Inot can use two trays of 10 eggs and one tray of 12 eggs, for a total of 22 eggs.

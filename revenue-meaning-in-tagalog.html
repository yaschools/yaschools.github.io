4. The following table shows last year's revenue for each Herman Hoagie's location Location: Revenue ( in thousands of dollars)Cedarton 121Rimber 189Linton 147Granton ?If the mean of the data set is 158 thousand dollars, find the revenue for the Granton location.


Mean

Mean shows the average of a number of data. The data collected can be in the form of single data or data with frequency.

Data from the last year's revenue for each Herman Hoagie's location shows a single data. If we count from the mean value, then the revenue for the Granton location is 175

Further explanation

Data is needed in the analysis of a study or experiment  

Data can be collected in the form of values

The data obtained can be expressed in the form of tables, graphs or diagrams  

From the data distribution, it can be seen the frequency of the values

Mean is one way to get the average value from numerical data  

Mean is the average value of some data. The mean value can be determined by dividing the sum of all data by the value of data  

Can be formulated  

[tex]\tt \bar{x}=\dfrac{x_1+x_2+x_3..x_n}{n}[/tex]

[tex]\tt \bar{x}=\dfrac{\sum x_n}{n}[/tex]

Given

Revenue for each Herman Hoagie's location  ( in thousands of dollars)

Cedarton : 121

Rimber : 189

Linton : 147

Mean : 158

Total data = n = 4

Required

the revenue for the Granton location.

Solution

Input the data in the formula, and assume for the Granton location = x

[tex]\tt 158=\dfrac{121+189+147+x}{4}[/tex]

158.4 = 121+189+147+x

632 = 457 + x ⇒combine like terms

x = 632 - 457

x = 175

So the revenue for the Granton location : 175  ( in thousands of dollars)

26. If marginal revenue is equal to zero, then * a. total revenue is zero. b. average revenue is zero. c. total revenue is at a maximum or a minimum. d. average revenue is at a maximum or a minimum.​


Answer:

In this situation, total revenue will be at a maximum or a minimum, depending on the initial level of total revenue. If total revenue is initially at a maximum, then it will remain at that maximum as the quantity of the product being sold increases. If total revenue is initially at a minimum, then it will remain at that minimum as the quantity of the product being sold increases.

It is important to note that if marginal revenue is equal to zero, this does not necessarily mean that average revenue (price) is also equal to zero. Average revenue is the total revenue divided by the quantity of the product being sold, and it can still be positive even if marginal revenue is zero.

Answer:

Marginal revenue product (MRP), also known as the marginal value product, is the marginal revenue created due to an addition of one unit of resource. The marginal revenue product is calculated by multiplying the marginal physical product (MPP) of the resource by the marginal revenue (MR) generated. The MRP assumes that the expenditures on other factors remain unchanged and helps determine the optimal level of a resource.

Define the marginal product of labor                                                                                  KEY TAKEAWAYS

Key Points

The marginal product of labor is not always equivalent to the output directly produced by that added unit of labor. When production is discrete, we can define the marginal product of labor (MPL) as ΔY/ΔL. When production is continuous, the MPL is the first derivative of the production function in terms of L. Graphically, the MPL is the slope of the production function. The law of diminishing marginal returns ensures that in most industries, the MPL will eventually be decreasing.

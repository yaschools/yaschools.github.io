1. how heat is fun in the electric iron ?​


Answer:

Heat is fun in the electric iron because it allows you to quickly and easily press your clothes. It can help you look your best for any occasion, and it's a great way to save time when getting ready. Plus, the heat from the iron can help relax wrinkles and give your clothes a crisp, professional look.

A heating element, typically made of a metal with a high resistance to electric current, such as nichrome wire, is used to heat an electric iron. The Joule heating effect causes the resistance of the wire to produce heat when an electric current is passed through the heating element.

The electric iron's temperature is typically controlled by a control mechanism like a thermostat or bimetallic strip. The soleplate of the iron is heated by the heating element as it heats up, which is then used to iron clothes and get rid of wrinkles.

The electric iron usually has a heat setting that can be changed, so the user can choose the temperature they want for different kinds of fabrics. The electric iron is a good illustration of how electrical energy can be converted into heat energy and serves as a useful tool for household chores.

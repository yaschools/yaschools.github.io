AUTHENTIC ---> means genuine

It's not JENRE, but GENRE ---> means any kind or sort or any category of literature, music or other forms of art or entertainment, whether written or spoken, audio or visual, based on some set of stylistic criteria.

CAMPAIGN ----> means a work in an organized and active way toward a particular goal, typically a political or social one.

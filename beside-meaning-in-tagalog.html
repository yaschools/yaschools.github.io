The origin of the word globalization is "GLOBAL". The word global may take different meanings in different languages. The most common meaning however is the 3D geometric figure. 20. "He makes me lie down in green pastures, he leads me beside to quiet waters"Psalm 23 verse 2what does this mean?​


This verse is written by David. "He makes me lie down" means the Lord, like a good shepherd, knew how to give David rest when he needed it, just as a shepherd would care for his sheep.

"lie down in green pastures" means the shepherd was aware of the ideal resting locations for his sheep. He faithfully leads the sheep to fresh pastures.

"he leads me beside to quiet waters" means the shepherd is aware of the sheep's needs at all times, including when they require clear waters and green pastures. The pictures provide a strong sense of relaxation, warmth, and caring.

The Lord knows all of our needs so we don't need to worry about anything. We just need to trust in Him that He can provide all of our needs.

24. when a dot is placed beside a note or rest. what does it mean?​


Answer:

A dot is written immediately after a note or rest, and it increases its duration by half. For example, a quarter note is equivalent in duration to two eighth notes; therefore, a dotted quarter note would be equivalent to three eighth notes.

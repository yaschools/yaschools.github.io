21. Why the torrid zones divided into tropics of capricorn and cancer ?​


QUESTION:Why the torrid zones divided into tropics of capricorn and cancer ?

ANSWER OR EXPLANATION:Torrid zone

This zone is bounded on the north by the Tropic of Cancer and on the south by the Tropic of Capricorn; these latitudes mark the northern and southern extremes in which the sun passes directly overhead. This happens once annually on these cusps, but in Tropics proper, the sun passes overhead twice a year.

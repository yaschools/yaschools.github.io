mixing finely grated ginger and lemon in hot waterconsuming fresh root ginger while chewing itcooking with fresh gingerincorporating fresh ginger into smoothies or drinkscombining fresh ginger with coconut water to make a ginger "shot"

    Hot ginger tea is a well-liked and successful home treatment for sore throats. The tea is a simple way to take ginger and allow it to come into touch with your throat, and the warm beverage may be soothing to an irritated throat. By reducing inflammation, ginger's anti-inflammatory properties can help ease a sore throat. According to research, ginger may achieve this by preventing the body's production of pro-inflammatory proteins. These proteins produce itching and inflammatory pain.

5. What is blurry vision?​


Answer:

Blurry vision is when you don't see as clearly or sharply as you once did. It's the most common vision problem. Blurry vision is different from loss of vision. Loss of vision means you become blind or can't see anything in all or part of an eye.

It's normal for your vision to slowly get blurrier as you get older, but glasses or contacts can usually help you see better

If you have sudden loss of vision, go to a hospital right away—vision loss is different from blurry vision

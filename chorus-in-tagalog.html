7. In a class of 50 students, 18 take chorus, 26 take band, and 2 take both chorus and band. Draw a Venn diagram and show how many students in the class are not enrolled in either chorus or band.


Given:

Total number of students: 50
Students who take chorus: 18
Students who take band: 26
Students who take both: 2

Solution for:

A). Take chorus only

18 - 2 = 16 students

B). Take band only

26 - 2 = 24 students

C). Not taking chorus and band

24 + 16 + 2 = 42
50 - 42 = 8 students

Venn Diagram:


There are 8 students in the class that are not enrolled in either chorus or band.

Both classes: 2
Chorus only: 16 (18-2)
Band only: 24 (26-2)

[tex]24+16+2+x=50[/tex] or [tex]42+x=50[/tex]
[tex]x=50-42[/tex]
x = 8

30. Pwede po bang halos magkapareho ang lyrics ng pre-chorus at chorus ng isang kanta?​


Oo, maaaring halos magkapareho ang lyrics ng pre-chorus at chorus ng isang kanta, lalo na kung ito ay ginagamit upang bigyang diin ang mensahe ng kanta. Ang pagkakapareho ng mga lyrics sa pre-chorus at chorus ay maaaring magbigay ng tugma at kasiglahan sa kanta, at maaaring rin itong magbigay ng pagkakakilanlan o identity sa kanta. Gayunpaman, kadalasan ay mas kinakailangan na magkaroon ng pagkakaiba ang mga lyrics ng pre-chorus at chorus upang magbigay ng pagkakaiba sa melody at struktura ng kanta.

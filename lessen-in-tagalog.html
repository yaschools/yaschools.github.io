2. give me five ways to lessen soil pollution,five ways to lessen air pollution,and five ways to lessen water pollution ​


WAYS TO LESSEN SOIL POLLUTION, AIR POLLUTION, AND WATER POLLUTION

Ways to lessen Soil Pollution

1. Planting trees or reforestation prevents soil erosion which can remove the healthy soil.

2. Avoid littering because as litter decompose, chemicals and micro particles are released.

3. Organic farming utilizes fewer pesticides, minimizes soil erosion, reduces nitrate leaching into groundwater and surface water.

4. Reduced usage of non-biodegradable products will result in less plastic pollution and, in turn, less land contamination.

5. Cleanup is a type of environmental remediation that involves the removal of contamination from the soil.

Ways to lessen Air Pollution

1. Plant trees because trees filter the air and provide shade.

2. Avoid burning waste because burning pollutes the environment by emitting hazardous gases.

3. Segregation of garbage helps to lessen burning waste.

4. Avoid Cigarette smoking because using cigarettes pollutes the environment by releasing hazardous air pollutants into the atmosphere.

5. Bike commuting does not necessitate the use of gasoline, thus there are no harmful car emissions or pollution released into the atmosphere.

Ways to lessen Water Pollution

1. Avoid throwing garbage in rivers or in the ocean.

2. Avoid flushing infected beverages, tablets, pharmaceuticals, or medications down the drain. There are numerous hazardous elements in these compounds that degrade the quality of natural water systems.

3. Lessen the use of herbicides, insecticides, and fertilizers. Excessive use of herbicides, insecticides, and fertilizers pollutes water supplies because the chemicals in the products often find their way into water systems via surface runoff or soil infiltration.

4. Proper sewage treatment is essential to decrease the number of harmful compounds that generally wind up in water systems such as lakes, rivers, and seas.

5. Conserve water at all times to take an active role in decreasing water pollution. When the taps aren't in use, make sure they're always switched off when not in use. The amount of contaminated water that needs to be treated is reduced when water is conserved.

WHAT IS THE MEANING OF LESSEN

ANSWER:

Lessen means to minimize or decrease something.

Synonyms: reduce, minimize, decrease, shorten, shrink, tone down

I decided to lessen the use of plastic to save nature.The restaurant lessened their menu of meat to help promote vegetables.My teacher decided to lessen the lessons to discuss.I realized I need to ride a bike to lessen the cause of pollution.

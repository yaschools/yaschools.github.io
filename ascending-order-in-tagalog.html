Ordering or Arranging Decimals

Ordering or arranging decimals is the same as the ordering of other numbers. You have to arrange the given decimals by comparing based on their place value and value. Ordering or arranging of decimals can be done in descending order or in ascending order. In descending order, you have to arrange the decimals from biggest to smallest while in ascending order, you have to arrange them from smallest to biggest.

How to Arrange Decimals

Below are the tips to guide you in arranging decimals:

If you think that bigger numbers are those with more digits, it is not. Add zeros to some decimals so they have the same number of decimal places before you compare.

Always start comparing from your left to right.

If the first digits are equal, then move to the next digits and compare.

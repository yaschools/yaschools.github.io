21. Why did plato prefer censorship of art in a society?​


Plato makes a systematic case for censoring all arts in his work "The Republic". He believed that to have a good society, children must be exposed to good material and shielded from bad material. It is the obligation of the State to educate its citizens and therefore the State should allow only good material and suppress bad material. This censorship applies also to art.

27. Is censorship in the media necessary???


Yes, censorship in media is necessary but must be done within certain limits.

Explanation details:

Censorship can be interpreted as limiting, hiding, or cutting some parts of certain media content to a group or all the people. There are several reasons for censorship. Among them, censorship is to hide content that is contrary to the existing culture in a community or to avoid unwanted effects of content, such as censorship of smoking scenes or beatings to prevent children who watch from copying it.

Is censorship in media important?  Yes

As we know in the era of globalization and current technological developments, there is a variety of content that emerges from various places. Therefore, there is a need for censorship to filter out content that is expected to have a negative impact.

How is media censorship done?

Usually, media censorship is done by cropping or blurring a scene. At a higher level, censorship is carried out by completely blocking content that is deemed unfit for broadcast.

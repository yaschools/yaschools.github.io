3. "Ang / Ti / nig / ng / Li / gaw / na / gan / sa". Anong elemento ang nangibabaw sa taludtod na Ito?​


Explanation:

INVERSE VARIATION

==============================

Question:

It takes 6 days for 4 workers to finish a certain jobs. How many days will it take to finish the same job if only 3 workers will do the job?

Answer:

\huge \boxed{ \sf \orange{8 \: days}}8days

==============================

Solve:

Let d be the days spent working

Let w be the number of workers

\begin{gathered} \implies \sf \large{d} = \frac{k}{w} \\ \end{gathered}⟹d=wk

\:

Find the constant where:

d = 6

w = 4

\begin{gathered}\implies \sf \large{6} = \frac{k}{4} \\ \end{gathered}⟹6=4k

\:

Multiply both sides to 4 to find the constant.

\begin{gathered}\implies \sf \large{6} \times 4= \frac{k}{ \cancel4} \times \cancel4 \\ \end{gathered}⟹6×4=4k×4

\implies \sf \large \boxed{ \therefore \sf k = 24}⟹∴k=24

==============================

Equation:

\begin{gathered} \implies \sf \large{d} = \frac{24}{w} \\ \end{gathered}⟹d=w24

\:

Use the Equation above to find the value of d when:

w = 3

k = 24

\begin{gathered}\implies \sf \large{d} = \frac{24}{3} \\ \end{gathered}⟹d=324

\begin{gathered}\implies \sf \large \boxed{ \sf \orange{ \therefore d = 8}} \\ \end{gathered}⟹∴d=8

==============================

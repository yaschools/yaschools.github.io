27. The salary of a junior auditor is 5/8 of the salary of a senior auditor. If a senior auditor earns 45 000.00 pesos a month, how much is the difference between their salaries


Answer:

The difference between the salaries is Php 16,875.00.

Step-by-step explanation:

Salary of senior auditor:

45,000.00

Salary of junior auditor:

(5/8)(45,000.00) = 28,125.00

Difference between their salaries:

45,000.00 - 28,125.00 = 16,875.00

The difference between the salaries is Php 16,875.00.

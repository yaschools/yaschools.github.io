4. For a group of 100 candidates the mean and standard deviation of their marks were found to be 60 and 15 respectively. Later on it was found that the scores 45 and 72 were wrongly entered as 40 and 27. Find the correct mean and standard deviation.


To find the correct mean and standard deviation, we need to first adjust the scores of the two candidates whose scores were wrongly entered.

The first candidate's score should be 45, but it was entered as 40. The difference is 5. The second candidate's score should be 72, but it was entered as 27. The difference is 45.

To adjust for these errors, we add 5 to the score of the first candidate and subtract 45 from the score of the second candidate. This gives us a new total score of:

Total score = (100 * 60) + 5 - 45 = 5955

To find the correct mean, we divide the new total score by the number of candidates:

Correct mean = Total score / Number of candidates = 5955 / 100 = 59.55

The correct mean is 59.55.

To find the correct standard deviation, we need to first find the sum of the squared differences between each candidate's score and the correct mean. We can use the formula:

sum of (X - mean)^2 = (sum of X^2) - [(sum of X)^2 / N]

where X is the score of each candidate, mean is the correct mean, and N is the number of candidates.

Using the original data (before adjusting for the errors), we have:

sum of X = 100 * 60 = 6000

sum of X^2 = (100 * 15^2) + 6000^2 = 1290000

N = 100

Plugging these values into the formula, we get:

sum of (X - mean)^2 = 1290000 - [(6000^2) / 100] = 90000

Now we adjust for the errors by subtracting the squared difference for the first candidate (5^2 = 25) and adding the squared difference for the second candidate (45^2 = 2025):

sum of (X - mean)^2 = 90000 - 25 + 2025 = 90200

To find the correct standard deviation, we divide the sum of the squared differences by the number of candidates and take the square root:

Correct standard deviation = sqrt(sum of (X - mean)^2 / N) = sqrt(90200 / 100) = 9.49

The correct standard deviation is 9.49.

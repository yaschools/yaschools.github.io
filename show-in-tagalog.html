6. Can you please show me the way in tagalog​


Answer:

Less formal (more commonly used):

Saan po ang daang papuntang (insert place)?

(This literally means "Where is the way to (insert place)" in English)

Formal (not often used):

Maaari niyo ba pong ituro sa akin ang daan?

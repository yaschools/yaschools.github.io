9. classify illuminating​


Answer:

Illuminating can be classified as a verb or adjective.

As a verb, illuminating means to provide light or clarity to something, to enlighten or educate someone, or to decorate or embellish something.

Example: The lamp was illuminating the room.

As an adjective, illuminating means providing insight or knowledge, or giving off light.

Example: The speaker's illuminating presentation provided valuable information.

19. microscope illuminating parts


Answer:

The condenser is placed below the

stage and concentrates the light, providing bright, uniform illumination in the region of the object under observation. Typically, the condenser focuses the image of the light source directly onto the plane of the specimen, a technique called critical illumination. Alternatively, the image of the source is focused onto the condenser, which is in turn focused onto the entrance pupil of the microscope objective, a system known as Köhler illumination

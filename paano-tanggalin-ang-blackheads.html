5. 6. Paano tanggalin ang damit na may kalawang?​


Ang pagtanggal ng kalawang sa damit ay depende sa laki ng kalawang at uri ng tela ng damit.

Kung maliit lang ang kalawang at hindi masyadong matigas, maaari mo itong tanggalin gamit ang mga sumusunod na hakbang:

1.Pahiran ang bahagi ng damit na may kalawang ng maliit na halaga ng suka o lemon juice. Pwede rin gamitin ang white vinegar.

2.Kapag natuyo na ang suka o lemon juice, pahiran naman ng baking soda ang bahagi ng damit.

3.Hayaan ang baking soda na mag-aksyon sa loob ng ilang minuto bago banlawan ang bahagi ng damit na may kalawang sa tubig.

4.Balutin ang bahagi ng damit na may kalawang sa isang towel at i-iron sa itaas nito. Ang init ng iron ay magbibigay ng tulong upang matanggal ang kalawang.

5.Kapag tapos na, tignan kung nakuha na ang kalawang. Kung hindi pa, pwede mo ulitin ang mga hakbang na ito hanggang sa matanggal ang kalawang.

Kung malaki naman ang kalawang at masyadong matigas, maaaring hindi na ito matanggal sa pamamagitan ng mga nabanggit na hakbang at mas mainam na bumili ng bago.

17. where are blackheads formed​


Answer:

Blackheads form when a hair follicle in the skin becomes clogged or plugged. Dead skin cells and excess oil collect in the follicle's opening, which produces a bump. If the skin over the bump opens, the air exposure causes the plug to look black, thus forming a blackhead.

Are blackheads caused by dirt?

Fast facts about blackheads

Blackheads are made of oxidized melanin and not trapped dirt. Squeezing or scrubbing at blackheads can make them worse. To reduce blackheads, avoid oil-based skin care products, humid environments, tight clothes, and skin products that contain alcohol.

Answer :

1. When removing ink stains that have pooled on a garment, use a washable microfibre cloth to blot the mark. Don’t be tempted to wipe or scrub: just blot until the ink dries and stops transferring off.

2. If the stain is very big, use a water-based stain remover to help break down the ink stains. Leave for 10 minutes, or follow the instructions on the product.

3. Pick up the cloth again to blot your stain. You should find that you are able to remove even more of the stain now that the pre-treatment has had time to work.

4. Place the garment in the washing machine, preferably by itself. Contrary to popular belief, you don’t need to boil your clothes, just use the same temperature you usually would for that garment.

5. Ensure that the ink stains are gone before drying. If remnants of the stain remain, blot, treat, and wash once more.

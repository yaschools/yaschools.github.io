Question:

Where should be the the education background be placed in resume?

A. the bottom of the resume.

B. the beginning of the resume.

C. either the top or bottom of the resume.

D. wherever you want to put it in your resume.

Answer:

C. either the top or bottom of the resume.

Explanation:

For education placement on your resume, think about where you are in your career and what you want to emphasize. If education is the most prominent part of your experience, you place it towards the top and if not you should put it in the bottom of your resume.

28. Give at least one example of Resume.​


Answer:

How to format a resume?

Set one-inch margins on all four sides.

Pick a 11 or 12pt resume font and stick to it.

Create a proper resume header format for your contact details.

Divide your resume into legible resume sections: Contact Information, Resume Summary, Work Experience, Education, Skills.

Use bullet points to talk about past jobs.

Be consistent with your resume formatting (stick to the same date format: for example 11–2018, or November 2018.)

Use single or 1.15 line spacing.

Add an extra space before and after each section heading.

Make your resume as long as it needs to be.

Don’t use photos on your resume. Unless the job description specifically asks for them. You’re looking for a job, not a date!


29. resume for job application letter example​


A resume consists of the following elements:

(Left corner)

        ID Picture

        Full name and contact details

Objective/s Professional experience/s Educational background Seminar/s attended Award/s received Skill/s Character references  (at least 3 persons with contact information)

Application Letter sample guide

Date

Name of recipient

Position

Company address

Dear Sir/Madam [last name]:

Introductory statement: Provide a brief background about yourself.  State your interest in applying for the position you are targeting.  

Body: Make a good pitch about yourself.  Include your skills and other credentials or qualifications that you possess.

Concluding statement: Summarize your application.  State that you have attached your resume for the company’s further reference about you.  Make it known to them that you are looking forward in meeting with them soon.

Thank you.

Sincerely/Respectfully,

(provide enough space for your signature)

Your name

Address

Contact number

E-mail address

(Notice the proper spacing of every element in the letter.  The letter should be in block format.  Thus, there are no indentations.)

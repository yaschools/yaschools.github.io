2. Dictionary meaning of mukbang


A mukbang, also spelled meokbang (/ˈmʌkbæŋ/; Korean: [mʌk̚.p͈aŋ] ( listen)) and also known as eating show, is an online audiovisual broadcast in which a host consumes large quantities of food while interacting with the audience.

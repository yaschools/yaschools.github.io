2. A cold water faucet can fill the bathtub in 12 minutes, and a hot water faucet can fill the bathtub in 18 minutes. The drain can empty the bathtub in 24 minutes. If both faucets are on and the drain is open, how long would it take to fill the bathtub?


Answer:

6 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

We can solve work problems with the following formulas:

Work = Rate * Time

Rate = Work / Time

Time = Work / Rate

Let us first determine the rate of each:

Cold faucet = 1 bathtub / 12 minutes = 1/12

Hot faucet = 1 bathtub / 18 minutes = 1/18

Drain = -1 bathtub / 24 minutes = -1/24

Combine the values to determine the time taken,

Time = 1 bathtub / (1/12 + 1/18 - 1/24 bathtubs per minute)

= 1 / (1/12 + 1/8 - 1/24) minutes

= 1 / (1/6) minutes

= 6 minutes

Therefore, it would take 6 minutes to fill the bathtub.

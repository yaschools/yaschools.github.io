3. give 3 examples of metaphor and their meanings.​


Answer:

His words cut deeper than a knife. Words don't materialize into sharp objects. In this metaphor, someone has said something hurtful to another.

I feel the stench of failure coming on. Failure isn't fun but it doesn't smell. So, when this metaphor is used, it means one of life's disappointments is on the way.

I'm drowning in a sea of grief. Here, grief is so overwhelming that the person feels helpless, like they're being pulled underwater.

I'm feeling blue. Until we become like the little girl in Willy Wonka's Chocolate Factory, none of us are likely to turn as blue as a blueberry. This metaphor means someone is sad.

She's going through a rollercoaster of emotions. Our emotions can't take a ride on a rollercoaster. This metaphor simply means the person's going through a lot of different moods.

6. What is metaphore ? Give an example of metaphore


 A metaphor is a figure of speech that is used to make a comparison between two things that aren't alike but do have something in common. Unlike a simile, where two things are compared directly using like or as, a metaphor's comparison is more indirect by stating something is something else. A metaphor is very expressive; it is not meant to be taken literally.You may have to work a little to find the meaning in a metaphor.

Metaphors can be very helpful for kids who are learning the meaning of specific words because they provide a more visual description of the word or thought.


The snow is a white blanket.
The hospital was a refrigerator.
The classroom was a zoo.
America is a melting pot.
Her lovely voice was music to his ears. 
Life is a rollercoaster.  
The alligator’s teeth are white daggers.  
Their home was a prison. 
The slide on the playground was a hot stove.
His heart is a cold iron.

8. Ano po yung Meaning ng Metaphor explain po sa madaling at paraan tagalog po sana


Metaphor is similar to Simile, nagkukumpara lang ng dalawang bagay (usually, yung isa sa kinukumpara is figurative ang use nya sa sentence). Ang pagkakaiba nga lang ng dalawa is that Simile uses mga palatandaan tulad ng "like" or "as" (in tagalog, maaaring "parang", "tila", "gaya", "tulad ng" at marami pang iba (mas marami kasi sa filipino kaya unti nalang yung binigay kong example).

Halimbawa:

This candy store is like heaven! - Simile

This candy store is heaven! - Metaphor

Question

240 is 25% of what number

Answer

960

Step-by-step explanation:

- We have, 25% × x = 240

or,  25 /100  × x = 240

- Multiplying both sides by 100 and dividing both sides by 25,

- we have x = 240 ×  100 /25

- x = 960

- If you are using a calculator, simply enter 240×100÷25, which will give you the answer.

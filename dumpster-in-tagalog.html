Answer:

Papers become harmful when burned because it can contribute respiratory problems to people when inhaled and can also contribute to global warming. Instead of burning, papers should be put in a compost pit since they are biodegradable.

2. Refrigerators become harmful when it is burned because refrigerators are made of different kinds of substances that can contribute to global warming. Refrigerators in other countries are sold back to manufactures or private companies focusing on techs to reuse and recycle parts and mostly to be fix in order to be used again.

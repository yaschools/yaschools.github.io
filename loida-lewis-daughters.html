4. lewis structure of oxygen​


Answer:

Each O is surrounded by four dots and two sticks or lines, representing another 4 electrons in the O2 double bond. So each O is surrounded by 8 total valence electrons, giving it an octet and making it stable. The two letter O's in the O2 Lewis structure represent the nuclei (centers) of the oxygen atoms.

11. Lewis structure of NH3​


Answer:

The Lewis structure of ammonia,  NH3, would be three hydrogen atoms bonded to a nitrogen atom in the middle, with a lone pair of electrons on top of the atom. This is the reason why ammonia acts as a Lewis base, as it can donate those electrons.

22. Loida walk 204 meters to her classmates house and together they walked 523 meters to school how many kilometers was covered by loida alone?.


Step-by-step explanation:

1 kilometer = 1000 meters

formula:

k (kilometer) = m(meter) divided by 1000

Given:

204 meters to her classmates house, 523 meters to her school

Solution:

k = m/1000

k = (204m + 523m)/1000

k = 727m/1000

k = 0.727 kilometers

hence, 0.727 kilometers was covered by loida alone after traveling from her classmate's house to school

Lewis symbol of magnesium?

Magnesium is element #12. To draw the Lewis electron dot diagram we picture in our minds the symbol for Mg in a box with all of its core electrons (i.e., 1s22s22p6).

24. (Ang mga leader ay tinuturing na huwaran).What does it mean?​


There may be many people who are capable of being a leader in terms of position. However, not all leaders are able to become leaders who are worthy of being role models. Of course, a good leader is a leader who can set a good example for all his subordinates.

The following are steps so that a leader can be used as a role model.

Consistent

Being a leader must be consistent and not wishy-washy. If a leader is inconsistent and does not have a stand, then he is not worthy of being a role model to his subordinates.

Then, how to be consistent? The easiest, get used to being on time. If he's used to being on time, he's more likely to stick to the other rules as best he can and become a consistent person.

Fair

A leader who deserves to be a role model is a leader who is fair and does not show favoritism. All his subordinates were treated the same, without any difference. Of course, everyone wants to be treated fairly.

Therefore, if we want to be treated fairly, we should first treat others fairly. Leaders who have a high sense of justice will definitely have loyal followers who also have a high sense of justice, on the other hand, leaders who are less fair will have no followers.

Honest

In addition to being consistent and fair, leaders must also be honest so that they can be used as role models. An honest leader means he is very open to his subordinates with nothing to hide. He will be transparent, so he is not a two-faced leader.

Two-faced leaders usually want to benefit themselves, not for mutual progress. Evidently, an honest leader will be loved by his subordinates more than a dishonest leader.

Not selfish

Selfish leaders only think about themselves without caring about others. Such a leader will certainly stop his career because his subordinates dislike him.

A leader who deserves to be a role model is a leader who puts the interests of others above his own. It is certain that a company or organization led by an unselfish leader will certainly be more prosperous and advanced.

Want to be directly involved

Finally, a leader who deserves to be a role model is a leader who wants to be directly involved and guide his subordinates, not a leader who wants to rule without wanting to be directly involved.

Leaders who can only govern are not good leaders, instead, they are used as role models. Meanwhile, leaders who want to be directly involved know more about the condition of their subordinates, and know their subordinates better, so that the relationship between superiors and subordinates can be closely intertwined.

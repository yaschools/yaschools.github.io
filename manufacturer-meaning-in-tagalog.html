22. What do you mean by manufacturers,suppliers and users of farm chemical all have an important role to play give examples


Answer:

Manufacturers, suppliers, and users of farm chemicals all have an important role to play in ensuring the safety, efficacy, and environmental sustainability of agricultural practices.

Manufacturers are responsible for developing and producing chemicals that meet regulatory standards for safety and effectiveness. They must also ensure that their products are labeled appropriately with accurate information on proper use, handling, and disposal.

Suppliers are responsible for distributing chemicals to farmers and other end-users while ensuring that they are properly stored, transported, and handled. They must also provide information and training to users on the safe and effective use of the chemicals.

Users of farm chemicals, such as farmers and crop consultants, have an important role to play in ensuring that these chemicals are used properly and responsibly. This includes following recommended application rates and timing, taking necessary precautions to protect human health and the environment, and disposing of leftover chemicals properly.

For example, a manufacturer of herbicides must develop and produce a product that effectively controls weeds while minimizing harm to non-target plants and animals. A supplier of pesticides must store and transport the chemicals safely and ensure that end-users are trained on proper handling and application. A farmer who uses these chemicals must apply them at the recommended rate and time to control pests effectively while minimizing exposure to the chemicals.

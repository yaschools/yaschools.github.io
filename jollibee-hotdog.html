20. Cyril was selling hotdogs and drinks. Each hotdog cost 15 pesos and drinks cost 18 pesos. At the end of the day cyril makes total of of 1003 pesos. He sold a total of 59 hotdogs juice. How many hotdogs and juice sold???​


Answer:

Cyril sold 42 hotdogs and 17 juice.

Step-by-step explanation:

x + y = 59 (the total number of hotdogs and drinks sold)

15x + 18y = 1003 (the total amount of money made from the hotdogs and drinks)

We can use the first equation to solve for one of the variables in terms of the other. Let's solve for y:

y = 59 - x

Now we can substitute this expression for y into the second equation:

15x + 18(59 - x) = 1003

Simplifying, we get:

15x + 1062 - 18x = 1003

-3x = -59

x = 19

So Cyril sold 19 hotdogs. We can use this information to find out how many drinks he sold by substituting x = 19 into the first equation:

19 + y = 59

y = 40

Therefore, Cyril sold 40 drinks.

2. what is the kinship terms of eldest sister second eldest sister and third eldest sister that vary with age differences in Jordan country?​


Answer:

In Jordan, the kinship terms for sisters that vary with age differences are:

Eldest sister: Ukhti al-kubra

Second eldest sister: Ukhti al-wusta

Third eldest sister: Ukhti al-sughra

Note: "Ukhti" means "my sister" in Arabic

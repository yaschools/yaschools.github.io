29. Sandy put 167 stamps from her stamp collection into 3 albumIn one album, she put 39 stamps. She put twice as many stampsin a larger album, and she put the rest of the stamps in the thirdalbum. How many stamps did Sandy put in the third album?​


Step-by-step explanation:[tex]167 - (39 \times 3)[/tex]50

Answer:

50

Step-by-step explanation:

She placed 39 stamps in one album and placed twice as many in the other. So you have to multiply 39 by 2 to figure out the amount of stamps she placed in the other. 39×2=78 Next we have to add 39 from the first album and 78 from the other. 39+78=117 Then Subtract it from the total amount of stamps which is 167. 167-117=50 Voila you got your answer

22. Q. Let's solve a riddle. Suppose that you are standing in one of the biggest palaces in the world.ㅤIn the palace, there are five guards. Each guard has been entrusted with five rooms & each room contains five pugs. Each pug has four baby pugs. Now you have to find that how many legs are there in the palace ?​​


SOLUTION⤵️

Let x be the legs of the guards

y be the legs of the pugs

Note that guards have a 2 legs and pug have 4 legs

Since they stated how many guards are there,I will just find how many pugs are there:

Now. In each room of 5 rooms, they have a 5 pugs with 4 babies each.

So, 5 x 5 x 4 = 100 pugs

Equation:

5x + 100y = total legs5(2) + 100(4) = total legs10 + 400 = total legs410 = total legs

ANSWER⤵️

There are a total of 410 legs in the palace.

24. what is the maximum of a pug dogs life?


Answer:

The life expectancy of a Pug dog is between 12 and 15 years, though the majority of Pugs succumb closer to the low end of this. The average male will live 12.8 years and female Pugs live a bit longer, with average life span being 13.2 years. This said, a Pug can live well into his teens (15, 16 or even 17 years).

16. ano Ang kahulugan Ng producer price index Tagalog​


Answer:

Ang Producer Price Index (PPI) sa Tagalog ay kilala bilang Indeks ng Presyo ng mga Prodyuser. Ito ay isang estadistikang ginagamit upang sukatin ang pagbabago sa mga presyo ng mga kalakal at serbisyo na ibinebenta ng mga prodyuser. Ito ay isang indikasyon ng mga pagbabago sa mga gastos sa produksyon ng mga prodyuser, kabilang ang mga pagbabago sa mga presyo ng mga raw materials, mga bilihin, at iba pang mga gastos sa produksyon.

28. 7. The following statements describe Kubing or Jaws harp of the Tagalog, Mangyan, Manobo, Negritos and Yakans EXCEPT: A. The instrument is made of bamboo with a plain or more omate design. B. The sound is produced by blowing air into the resonator. C. The sound is produced by placing the instruments between lips while plucking the bamboo tongue. D. A and C.​


Answer:

The correct answer is B. The sound is produced by blowing air into the resonator.

Explanation:

Kubing or Jaws harp of the Tagalog, Mangyan, Manobo, Negritos and Yakans is a traditional musical instrument made of bamboo with a plain or more ornate design. The sound is produced by placing the instrument between the lips while plucking the bamboo tongue. This creates a rhythmic sound that can be varied in pitch and volume by changing the shape of the mouth and the amount of air blown into the resonator. Therefore, option B- The sound is produced by blowing air into the resonator is incorrect.

8. What is the adjacent in this triangle and how did you know that is the adjacent?thanks


Just remember this mnemonic:   Soh Cah Toa

Soh:
Sin Θ = opposite/hypotenuse

Cah:
Cos Θ = adjacent / hypotenuse

Toa:
Tan Θ = opposite / adjacent 

So in your triangle:
Cos M = n/p   ⇒  n is the adjacent side; p is the hypotenuse

Cos N = 8/p   ⇒ 8 is the adjacent side; p is the hypotenuse 

Tan M = 8/n   ⇒ n is the adjacent side; 8 is the opposite side

Tan N = n/8   ⇒ 8 is the adjacent side; n is the opposite side

27. Which of the following represents the sine ratio in a right triangle?A. adjacent ————— hypotenuseB. opposite —————hypotenuseC. opposite ————— adjacentD. adjacent ————— opposite​


QUESTION:

Which of the following represents the sine ratio in a right triangle?

ANSWER:

use SOH CAH TOA, to easily remember the trigonometric ratios

[tex] \sf \sin( \theta) = \frac{O}{H} = \frac{Opposite}{Hypotenuse} \\ \\ \sf \cos( \theta ) = \frac{A}{H} = \frac{Adjacent}{Hypotenuse} \\ \\\sf \tan( \theta ) = \frac{O}{A} = \frac{Opposite}{Adjacent} \\ [/tex]

So looking at the trigonometric ratios above, SOH means sine is equal to opposite over hypotenuse.

Therefore, the correct answer is letter B.

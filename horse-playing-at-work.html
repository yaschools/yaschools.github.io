19. A horse performs 15000 joules Of work pupunningawagon for 20 seconds.what is the horse power.​


Formula:

[tex]\boxed{\textsf{P}=\dfrac{\textsf{W}}{\textsf{t}} }[/tex]

where

P = power

W = work

t = time

Solution:

[tex]\textsf{P}=\dfrac{\textsf{15000 J}}{\textsf{20 s}} =\textsf{750 W}\\\\\textsf{P}=\textsf{750 W}\times\dfrac{\textsf{1 hp}}{\textsf{746 W}}\\\\\boxed{\textsf{P = 1.01 hp}}[/tex]

Note: [tex]\textsf{1 hp}=\textsf{746 W}[/tex]

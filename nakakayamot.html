Yes, the number of batteries affects the load (the bulb). When more batteries are connected in series, the voltage across the bulb increases, and as a result, the current flowing through the bulb increases. This causes the bulb to emit a brighter light.

C. What do you think will happen if you connect either the positive sides and the negative sides of the batteries together? Will the bulb light up?

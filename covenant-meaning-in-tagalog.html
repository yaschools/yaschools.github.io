3. it is the meaning of agreement or covenant​


Answer:

When used as nouns, agreement means an understanding between entities to follow a specific course of conduct, whereas covenant means an agreement to do or not do a particular thing. Covenant is also verb with the meaning: to enter into, or promise something by, a covenant.

The Old covenant

The theme of the Old Testament (literally, the ‘old covenant promise’) is God’s agreement with Abraham and the people of Israel. God promises to bless and prosper Abraham and his descendants. He declares that Abraham will be the father of ‘many nations.

The New covenant

In contrast to the ‘Old Covenant’ agreement between God and the people of Israel, the New Testament tells of the ‘new covenant agreement’ made by God with both Jews and Gentiles (non-Jews) through his son, Jesus Christ.


11. dicuss the difference between old covenant and new covenant​


Answer:

The Old Testament was a foreshadowing of the New, a foundation for what was to come. From the book of Genesis on, the Old Testament pointed forward to a Messiah or Savior. The New Testament describes the fulfillment of God's promise by Jesus Christ.

The Old Covenant was established between God and the people of Israel after God freed them from slavery in Egypt. Moses, who led the people out, served as the mediator of this contract, which was made at Mount Sinai.

A covenant is a sacred agreement or mutual pledge between God and a person or a group of people in the gospel. God offers a blessing in exchange for adherence to certain commands when he makes a covenant.

What kind of world do we have if the 10 Commandments are not obeyed?

Because sin is the violation of the law, renouncing the law abrogates sin. Most Christians are taught that failing to follow the 10 commandments is a kind of disobedience to God. Disobedience to God may lead to eternal punishment.

Which commandment do you find it difficult to obey, why?

Because so many Westerners are no longer Christians, the first commandment (You shall have no other gods before Me) is the most difficult for many of us. This commandment suggests that God must be worshipped. It is said to be the most difficult since we worship other gods, people, and things more than the God who created us.

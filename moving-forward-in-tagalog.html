5. When a moving bus stops suddenly, the passengers move forward. ​


Answer:

The passengers in a bus tend to fall backward when it starts suddenly due to inertia as the passengers tend to remain in the state of rest while the bus starts to move. When the bus stops suddenly, people fall forward because their inertia as they are in state of motion even when the bus has come to rest.

16. Consider a moving grocery cart. As the person moves, it pushes the grocery cart forward; thegrocery cart pushes the person backward with an equal force. How come the cart moves forward?​


Answer:

Grocery carts have wheels, and most grocery stores have tiled floors which lessens friction between the cart's wheels and the floor. Even if the grocery cart pushes the person back with equal force, it moves since it has wheels and is on a smooth surface while the person has more friction between his/her shoes and the floor with his/her weight strengthening the friction.

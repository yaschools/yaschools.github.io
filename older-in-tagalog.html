9. Joshua is five years older than david. simon is four years older than joshua and nine years older than david. simon’s and david’s ages added together equal 15. how old are the boys?


Answer:

Joshua is 8 years old.

David is 3 years old.

Simon is 12 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the age of Joshua

y be the age of David

z be the age of Simon

x = y + 5 ----equation 1

z = x + 4

z = y + 9 -----equation 2

z + y = 15 ----equation 3

Equate equations 2 and 3

(y + 9) + y = 15

y + y = 15 - 9

2y = 6

y = 3

Plug in y = 3 to equation 2

z = 3 + 9

z = 12

Plug in y = 3 to equation 1

x = y + 5

x = 3 + 5

x = 8

21. United nations principles for older person older men and woman and the people younger than them share the same rights, our rights do not change as we grow older . Unfortunately what does change is that older men and women are treated less valuable in society​


Answer:

The United Nations Principles for Older Persons aim to protect and promote the rights of older people. One of the principles states that older men and women have the same rights as younger people, and these rights should not diminish with age. However, ageism is a prevalent issue in society, and older people are often treated less valuable and are subject to discrimination and neglect.

It is essential to recognize that older people have the same dignity, worth, and human rights as any other member of society. They should have access to healthcare, education, employment, and social services, among others, without discrimination based on age. Moreover, older people should have the opportunity to participate in decision-making processes that affect their lives and communities.

The United Nations Principles for Older Persons serve as a guide to policymakers, organizations, and individuals to ensure that the rights of older people are respected and protected. It is crucial to promote a society that values and respects people of all ages, and that recognizes the contributions and experiences of older people.

24. Joshua is five years older than david. simon is four years older than joshua and nine years older than david. simon's and david's ages added together equal 15. what is the age of joshua?


Answer:

8 y/o

Step-by-step explanation:

let:

joshua = x

david = y

simon = z

equation:

x = 5 + y

z = 4 + x

z = 9 + y

y + z = 15

find:

x = ?

solution:

x = 5 + y

but:

y + z = 15

y = 15 - z

and

z = 4 + x

therefore:

x = 5 + 15 - z

x = 5 + 15 - (4 + x)

x = 20 - 4 - x

2x = 16

x = 8

therefore, joshua is 8 y/o

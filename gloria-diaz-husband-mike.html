14. Was it understandable that Gloria reacted the way she did to her husband’s fate? Why or why not? (The World Is An Apple.)


Answer:

Yes, it was understandable that Gloria reacted the way she did to her husband's fate. She had put her entire life and her entire future hopes and dreams into their relationship, only for it to be taken away by a seemingly random accident. It would be emotionally devastating for anyone in her position. Furthermore, the fact that Gloria was already pregnant made the reality of her husband's death even more difficult to accept, as it robbed not only her, but also her unborn child, of ever having the chance to know its father. Therefore, it is understandable that Gloria was overwhelmed by grief and despair at her husband's end.

Kinatawan din ni Reyes ang Pilipinas sa World Cup of Pool, na nanalo sa event kasama ang kanyang partner na si Francisco Bustamante noong 2006 at 2009.

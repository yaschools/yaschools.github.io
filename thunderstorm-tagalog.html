27. what is the sound of thunderstorm​


Answer: *thunderclap or Peal of thunder*

Explanation:The sudden increase in temperature and hence pressure caused by the lightning produces rapid expansion of the air in the path of a lightning bolt. In turn, this expansion of air creates a sonic shock wave, often referred to as a "thunderclap" or "peal of thunder".

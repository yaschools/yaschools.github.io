b. MN = 15

d. <Y = 45°

a.

2x + 5 = 15

2x = 15-5

2x = 10

2x / 2 = 10 / 2

x = 5

b.

MN = 2x + 5 = 2(5) + 5 = 15

c.

Since ∆MAN ≈ ∆BOY, so,

< M = < B

Therefore, the measure of angle M is 60°.

d.

Since ∆MAN ≈ ∆BOY, so,

< Y = < N

Therefore, the measure of angle Y is 45°.

e.

Given : < B = 60° , < Y = 45°

<B + <Y + <0 = 180

60 + 45 + <0 = 180

<0 = 180 - 45 - 60

<0 = 75°

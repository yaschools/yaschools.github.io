16. at which time of the day is the shadow the longest


Answer:

Sunrise and sunsets

Explanation:

Sunrise and sunsets produce the longest shadows of the day, because that's the most extreme angle possible before the horizon blocks the sun. So, in real life, the longest shadow we can observe is during the sun rise or the sunset.

21. at which time of the day is the shadow the longest


Answer:

Shadows are longest in the early morning and late afternoon/early evening when the sun appears low in the sky. As the Earth rotates on its axis, the sun hits each location in the morning at an angle. This becomes more vertical as the sun appears to pass more directly overhead around noon.
